As a user of the TraceNoizer website you agree with the way TraceNoizer processes this data. Your personally identifiable data will absolutely NOT be shared with or sold to third parties. Network traffic The TraceNoizer-Team monitors network traffic to identify unauthorised attempts to upload or change information, or to otherwise cause damage. Moreover, TraceNoizer collects data on network traffic to assess the performance of the website and to obtain (general) statistical information on the usage of the TraceNoizer website. Storage of Collected Data The collected data are stored in a secured database on (TraceNoizers' own) servers, which are hosted in a secure environment.
