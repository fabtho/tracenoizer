The most significant development to emerge from the group to date has been the Ceilidh and the CourseMaster systems. Ceilidh is a general course management system whose core is an automatic-assessment facility for marking students' computer programs. Ceilidh has been used to teach classes of up to 160 at Nottingham and was adopted as the basis of a three year development throughout UK Higher education funded under the UFC's Teaching and Learning Technology Programme (TLTP).
