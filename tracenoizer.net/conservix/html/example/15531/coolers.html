5MW Adiabatic Dry Coolershave recently been installed in Hemel Hempstead and Bristol for telecommunications company 186K Limited. They provide cooling water to the absorption chillers serving the Combined Cooling and Power system designed and installed by Brotherhood Aircogen in partnership with Lattice Energy Services. The coolers operate dry through most of the year and only change to adiabatic mode during times of high ambient and maximum cooling load. The Adiabatic Dry Coolers are fully automatic and have the benefit of POWETRONIC fan speed control which gives accurate temperature control and reduced noise levels.
