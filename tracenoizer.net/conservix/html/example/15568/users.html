Built in response to user comments, the Perseus table of contents (TOC) is a customizable, automatically updated listing of materials in the Perseus digital library. With the Perseus TOC, users may view a complete listing of the contents of the library or select a subset of materials based on various criteria. Since this is a scripted page, not a series of static html pages, information listed in the TOC is always up-to-date. The Perseus TOC is linked from several locations in Perseus, most notably from the Texts and Translations page.
