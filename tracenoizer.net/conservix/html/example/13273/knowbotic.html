Knowbotic Research was awarded a Golden Nica by the Prix Ars Electronica jury for his entry Simulationsraum-Mosaik mobiler Datenklaenge in the category Interactive Art . . Knowbotic Research Knowbotic Research group of scientists and artists Abstract 'Knowbotic Research' is a group of media artists and scientists who are creating projects with the goal of interaction between both disciplines. Christian Huebler (A), born 1962, is an independent media artist and studied at the Art Institute for Media, Cologne. He has received grants from various European institutions and taken part in various international media art festivals and exhibitions (most recently the Mediale in Hamburg, the Videonale in Bonn, and the Interior Architecture Festival in Graz).
These extensions of the cultural environment provoke new cultural and aesthetical parameters in order to prevent an ideological closed circuit in an information based society. (Prix Ars Electronica 93, Golden Nica for Interactive Art; German Media Art Award, ZKM Karlsruhe 95). The installation Dialogue With The Knowbotic South will be on show at V2_Organisation during the Next 5 Minutes and can be visited daily between 12. 00 hrs, at V2_Organisation, Rotterdam, Eendrachtsstr.
