SOUND ADVICE is the weekly guide to the world of classical music and recordings. Rick Phillips makes classical music selection easier for both longtime classical devotees and new fans of all ages. SOUND ADVICE includes reviews of new classical music CDs, often with musical comparisons to other recordings still available. There are also hints on how to listen critically, how to appreciate music more, what to listen for, and a host of other topics. There's also a Library segment every week, where Rick looks at how to build a classical music library, or brief surveys of some of the great performers from disc, or explanations and musical examples of some of the basic forms in classical music.
