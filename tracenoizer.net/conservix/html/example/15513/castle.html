The 14 century castle, perched atop a 60m peak in the center of Bran Village, was in fact built by people of Brasov in 1382 to defend the Bran mountain pass against Turks. In 1413, the Wallachian ruler allowed the people to set up custom posts on the Brasov road and Bran pass.
