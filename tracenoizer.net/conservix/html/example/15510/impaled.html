This man single handedly impaled and cooked people to their miserable deaths. He lived from 1431 to 1476 and throughout his life he was ruler of Wallachia three different times. He was born in the Transylvania town of Sighisoara, son of the formidable Vlad Dracul, Prince of Wallachia.
