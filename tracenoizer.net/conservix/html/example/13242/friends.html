. Have children appoint one person in the group as the recorder. Each group brainstorms (for designated period of time) a list of things friends do (share ice cream, laugh at each other's jokes, go places together, etc. The recorder prints all ideas on large piece of paper. When the allotted time is up, the groups share their lists with one another.
