Provides Intelligent and Optical Character Recognition services (ICR and OCR). Operates an extensive in-house data capture bureau service. DRS products and services are used for a wide range of applications including education administration and examination processing, electoral registration and ballot counting, large volume or complex survey work and census. DRS manufacture its own range of Optical Mark Readers (OMR) and provide a range of optical character recognition (OCR) and intelligent character recognition (ICR) technologies, as well as providing an extensive in-house data capture service from its bureau facility. DRS technology is able to capture data from many sources including paper forms and booklets, fax and electronic media, photos, fingerprints and signatures.
