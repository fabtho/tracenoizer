Healthy Child Care America Healthy Child Care America In many communities around the country, innovative but scattered projects are forging new ground to ensure that children are in healthy and safe child care environments. These communities recognize that the public health community can combine efforts with the early childhood community in order to create the best care for the millions of children in child care. To reinforce these efforts and to stimulate others nationwide, the Federal Child Care Bureau and the Maternal and Child Health Bureau have joined efforts to launch the Healthy Child Care America Campaign. The American Academy of Pediatrics, with support from the US Department of Health and Human Services Child Care Bureau and Maternal and Child Health Bureau, assumed coordination of the campaign in October 1996.
