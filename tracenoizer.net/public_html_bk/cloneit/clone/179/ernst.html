Young Swiss Graphic Design by Michel Fries The publishers and contributors have taken a trip around Switzerland and sought out the younger and youngest generation of graphic designers. The material they gathered is hot and is equal in every way to the London or Berlin scene. The contributors are Martin Heller, Ruedi Widmer, Max Bruinsma, Meret Ernst and Raphael Urweider. The modern city provides the social, aesthetic, historical and political framework within which to explore the arts and ideas of the 20th century. The book focuses on nine cities across the world, which, at specific moments in the 20th century, have all acted as cauldrons of innovation to visual culture.
