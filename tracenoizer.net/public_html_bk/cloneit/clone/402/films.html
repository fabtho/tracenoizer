. MUFS - Manchester University Film Society Welcome to MUFS. The Manchester University Film Society shows films to professional cinema standard in the Union's main hall. If you join, you get free entry to all films, a colour booklet containing reviews of the films, and a handy wall planner. .University Film Society University Film Society provides campus with top cinema The University Film Society (UFS) is a non-profit film exhibitor right on the U of M campus. Called the epicenter of local film culture by the Minnesota Daily, the UFS runs films at Bell Auditorium and Nicholson Auditorium 360 days a year. Although it brings in top-notch art house films and the occasional revival show, foreign films are its real specialty. The UFS has been a part of campus life since 1962, when it was founded as Minnesota's first (and only) non-profit organization to exhibit foreign and independent films.
