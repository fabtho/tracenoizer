Aberdeen University Cinema Club The Aberdeen University Cinema Club was started in 1992 by a group of students who were interested in films. We attempt to show a wide range of films, not always available at other cinemas, including popular, as well as foreign and art-house films.
. MUFS - Manchester University Film Society Welcome to MUFS. The Manchester University Film Society shows films to professional cinema standard in the Union's main hall. If you join, you get free entry to all films, a colour booklet containing reviews of the films, and a handy wall planner. Webster University Film Series The Webster Film Series is a year-round program that prides itself as the only alternative film series in the St. Now in its 21st year of operation, the Series offers a premiere film almost every weekend and frequently screens classic cinema on weeknights as well. Webster has become the host sight for many international tours and continues to be the only venue in the area that regularly hosts artists working in film.
