Smith Johann Christoph Bach 1642-1703 Johann Sebastian's second cousin (and grandfather of his first wife, Maria Barbara) became town organist in Eisenach in the year 1665 and court chamber musician there in 1700. Johann Christoph was well-respected in the Bach family; Johann Sebastian's uncle (twin brother of his father, Ambrosius) was named Johann Christoph as were Johann Sebastian's older brother and second-to-youngest son. Johann Christoph's brother, Johann Michael, was also well respected.
Carl Philipp Emanuel named Pachelbel as a composer whose works his father had admired. . Smith Johann Pachelbel 1653-1706 Johann Pachelbel began his musical instruction under Schwemmer and later at the Universities of Altdorf and Ratisbon. In 1671 Johann moved to Vienna where he became student and deputy organist to Kerll at the Imperial chapel. In 1677 he was organist for one year in Eisenach--the city of Bach's birth eight years later.
