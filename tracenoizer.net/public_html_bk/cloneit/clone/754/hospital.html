Services offered Daily surgery for routine and emergency care. Emergencies and appointments outside standard surgery hours and house calls are charged extra. Our clinics foster professionalism and excellence in all the phases of hospital operations. Starting with accurate record keeping for every patient and our concern for preventative medicine, our well-trained veterinarians and surgical staff are committed to the highest standards. All surgical procedures, both routine and specialist, are performed in a separate fully equipped surgical suite where proper anaesthetic procedures are followed to create safe and comfortable conditions for your pet during surgery.
