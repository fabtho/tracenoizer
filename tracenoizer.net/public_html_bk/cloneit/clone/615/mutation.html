Here we present the results of a screening for CNGA3 mutations in patients with RM and other types of cone photoreceptor dystrophies, which enables us to establish a first comprehensive mutation spectrum of the CNGA3 gene. In 6 cases only a single heterozygous mutation could be found. 10 of the mutations were observed recurrently in different families.
