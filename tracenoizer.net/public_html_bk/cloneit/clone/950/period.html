Use space-period-space-period-space-period-space instead (a perfectionist would then kern these to be more closely spaced). Using Bold, Italics, and Underline Don't use bold or underlines within blocks of text -- use italics (although bold works better than italics in web pages). An Integrated Power and ACS (IPAS) has the capability to provide a lower duty cycle for the instruments. An IPACS is a combination of magnetically suspended momentum storage and energy storage wheels. The solar arrays spin the wheels up during the sunlit period and the wheels generate electricity during eclipses. GEC is almost ideal for IPACS because GEC needs a large momentum vector (for a momentum bias ACS design) and an efficient power system. One of the putative advantages of an IPACS design is that its overall efficiency can be better than a battery.
