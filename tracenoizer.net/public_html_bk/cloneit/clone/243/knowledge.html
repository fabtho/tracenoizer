Graphic Design 101 with Stephanie Schleicher teaches creative solutions to real-life design problems. Successful design needs careful planning and research before ideas are explored. Approaching a problem without knowledge or preparation will make the problem more difficult to solve. You need to use basic design principles to achieve success in whatever you are trying to design. In this 1 day course you will learn the four basic design principles with the use of type and images.
Includes 24 page insert designed and produced in Zurich, Switzerland. It's said that a little knowledge is a dangerous thing. But there's nothing nearly so dangerous as an awful lot of ignorance.
