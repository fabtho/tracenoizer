Bach composed music in all the prevailing genres of his time. For voices he composed cantatas, masses, and oratorios. His instrumental works include concertos and sinfonias for orchestra and an enormous amount of keyboard music, for both organ and harpsichord. Some elements of the original notation have been modernized and many problems have been resolved. Works now known to have been spuriously attributed to Bach are excluded.
