Sustainability When we think about the industrial revolution, and the rise of design for manufactured products, one striking fact is the linearity of the system. You will see that almost every product that surrounds you will end up as waste because that is how it is designed. The idea behind sustainability is to try to make our human systems function more like nature's systems, which are balanced and self regenerating so that there is no waste.
