During the second year of the course students are scheduled to spend three months gaining practical experience with professional film and television production companies. Students must pass an intermediate examination before beginning the Advanced Studies segment and a further examination to begin their diploma year. During the course exercise films (16 mm) and videos will be produced. Usually individual and group films are created once a year. During the fourth year of their studies students produce a diploma film.
