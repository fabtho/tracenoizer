One focus of the collection are the pictures of the Hamburg Secessionists, a movement founded in 1919. Bunte remained in contact with these artists in later years, acquiring not only their Secessionist-period works but also paintings that testify to their new beginnings in the 1940s-50s. The Bunte Collection was on view until March 6, 2000 in a large-scale presentation at the Kunsthalle of Hamburg and the Hamburg Sparkasse. The Kunsthalle then showed a representative selection of approximately eighty works from all epochs of the collection. .
