As many as 2,341 works were submitted for the 17th International Poster Biennial. For the exhibition, the judges selected 451 posters designed by 280 artists from 35 countries. The president of the jury, Seymour Chwast, praised the artistic quality of the advertising and sociopolitical posters in particular. "I am glad that even in these times of the Internet and modern media, the quality of traditional poster art continues to be very high," he said. It was sponsored by the State Fund of Culture PRO SLOVAKIA. The Trnava Poster Triennial 2000 was the fourth year of this international poster exhibition and competition. The only condition for participation was high artistic quality of posters, which was guaranteed by the selection committee consisting of experts in graphic design. In 2000 the competition was entered by almost 1800 posters. An extraordinary point of this year was a special ICOGRADA prize award, presented by the vice-president of ICOGRADA, the graphic designer Sanja Rocco from Croatia.
