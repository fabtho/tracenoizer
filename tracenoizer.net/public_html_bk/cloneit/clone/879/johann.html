Smith Johann Christian Bach 1735-1782 The youngest surviving son of Johann Sebastian studied with his father, his brother Carl Philipp Emanuel, and Padre Martini of Bologna. became organist at the Milan Cathedral in 1760 where he became known as the "Milan" Bach. Two years later he became the "London" Bach when he entered the service of the Queen of England.
