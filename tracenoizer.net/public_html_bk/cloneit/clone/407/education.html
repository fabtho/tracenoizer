Interactive Education was formed this year to address the rapid changes that are happening in schools in terms of ICT. We are dedicated to providing ICT solutions to Education so that ICT can become transparent in all curriculum areas. The founders of Interactive Education Ltd have been involved in ICT and Education as ICT Co-ordinators for a number of years.
