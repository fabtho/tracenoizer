TRAM Line Number 7 (black) for example from the Main Station, direction WOLLISHOFEN; stop at POST WOLLISHOFEN; walk down toward the lake. You will pass under a railway bridge and you will see the red bricks of the FACTORY right on the other side. You'll be at Zuerich Main Station in max 15 minutes. S-BAHN (wich is a kind of regional Subway, to be taken at Zuerich Main Station). Take Number S 1 or S 8; stop at ZUeRICH-WOLLISHOFEN Station.
