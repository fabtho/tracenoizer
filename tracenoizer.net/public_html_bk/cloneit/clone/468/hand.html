By researching the work of Ernst Cramer the significance of the Swiss garden art is emphasized and the history of the European garden art will be enhanced. The basis of the scientific research of the work of Ernst Cramer is the systematic computer supported inventorying, the investigation and the competent cataloguing of substantial historical documents in cooperation with the Archive for Swiss Landscape Architecture in Rapperswil. The estate 5MB database Cramer meanwhile comprises approximately 1300 files, in which circa 5000 design and several thousand photo and script documents are at hand. Voluminous research of literature of historic roots augment the knowledge. Reconstruction drawings and models give an impression of the original expressiveness.
