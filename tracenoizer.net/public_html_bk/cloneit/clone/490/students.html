Like European Studies at the University of Oregon, the program combines humanities and social science approaches to the study of Europe. Opportunities to study American and British social history, international business and international development are also provided. Another feature is the presence of distinguished visitors from many different countries, both European and non-European, who give lectures on a wide variety of current international topics or who present short courses for interested students. Students may participate in this program on a Spring Semester (UO Winter-Spring term) or year-long basis. Students who choose the year-long option are eligible to apply for internships situated throughout Europe.
OIP - Language Programs Studying German at Brown and Abroad Beginning German (GM 10-20) A year-long course in the language and cultures of German-speaking countries. Four hours per week plus regular computer and listening comprehension work in the Language Resource Center. At the end of the year, students will be able to communicate successfully about everyday topics and read a variety of texts. Intensive Beginning German (GM 11) Students who wish to complete the German 10-20 sequence in one semester may do so by enrolling in German 11 for two semester course credits. The material covered will be similar to that covered in regular German 10-20.
The University and Location The Eberhard Karls Universitaet Tuebingen was founded during the Reformation by the Duke of Wuerttemberg and quickly became one of the most important centers of humanist scholarship in southern Germany. Tuebingen boasts a world renowned Theologie department and is strong in the natural sciences, philosophy and philology. Former students include the philosopher Hegel and the poet Holderlin. Today, there are approximately 26,000 students in a town of 85,000 inhabitants. The University is located in the picturesque town of Tuebingen, which was virtually untouched by World War II, and is situated near the Black Forest, in Swabia.
. OIP - Programs, Germany University of Tuebingen and Humboldt University, Germany Academic Program . This exchange program between Brown and the Eberhard-Karls-University in Tuebingen and Humboldt University in Berlin provides an opportunity for students to study directly in these important German universities. For Tuebingen, the program is preceded by a month long orientation in German language and culture provided by the Akademisches Auslandsamt of the Eberhard-Karls-University at a mountain town near Tuebingen. After that students directly enroll in university courses.
