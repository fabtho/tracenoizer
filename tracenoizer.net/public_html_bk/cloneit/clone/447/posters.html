French movie posters exist in a few different sizes beginning with a Petite format (15" x 21"), a Medium (24" x 32"), and the most commonly used Large format (46" x 63"). A new size has been added in recent years at 48" x 72". This new size is used in bus shelters and street kiosks throughout France.
