Cinema Studies Programs 2001-02 Program Handbook Cinema Studies Faculty Scholarship Information Cinema Studies Resources Extra-curricular Activities Innis College Town Hall News and Events Mailing List Contact Cinema Studies The Cinema Studies Programs at the University of Toronto treat film as a unique and powerful twentieth century art with its own traditions, history, conventions, and techniques. Classified among the Humanities programs of the Faculty of Arts and Science, Cinema Studies embraces the study of film analysis, film history, and film theory. Students in our programs learn how to understand, to analyze, to discuss, and to explain film in a way that is analogous to the skills acquired in reading literature or discussing painting in a university English or Fine Art course.
