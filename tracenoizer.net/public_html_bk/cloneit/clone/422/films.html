Annette Apon was born in Amsterdam in 1949 and attended the Netherlands Film Academy from 1968 to 1972. In 1968 she was co-founder of the film magazine Skrien. In 1973-74 she directed two short films There's No Place Like Home and Arthur and Eve. In 1974, she was one of the founders of the Amsterdam City Newsreel collective. She directed eleven films within the collective, both fiction and documentaries.
