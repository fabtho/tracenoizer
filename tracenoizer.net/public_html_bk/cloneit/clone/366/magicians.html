AUSTRALIAN SOCIETY OF MAGICIANS INDEX HTML Welcome to the home site of the AUSTRALIAN SOCIETY OF MAGICIANS INCORPORATED The Australian Society of Magicians Incorporated (ASM), oldest Magical Society in the Southern Hemisphere, fourth oldest in the world, was founded in 1907 by George Lesley Cole (The Great Levante) so that magicians might meet to practice and promote the art of Magic. Ten branches, known as Assemblies, have been formed around Australia since those early days. Meetings, open only to members, are held on the second Monday of each month with January excepted.
