CAST(ARTLAB Prospect2-Moeller's Works) "Controlling Concept of the Kinetic Light Sculpture" 0 degree1. 5 degree8 degree18 degree (previous) Temperature, wind, rain - as well as acting as a constant function of time - are the parameters of the light sculpture that changes in real time. The yellow patches are moved in accordance with the direction of the wind from left to right or from right to left. The strength of the wind governs the speed in which they move over the surface.
As a reaction, the software system generates and projects an organic plasmatic mirror image of the visitor. Since the intelligent system also builds up an artificial memory of each person and their behaviour and since the projected image is partly determined by this memory, the visitor sees not so much himself as a digital Golem. This Golem reacts in real time in an unpredictable way to the visitor's actions. ..
Su trabajo se ha expuesto en museos y festivales internacionales de renombre. . De esta manera el observador se mueve (y es movido) a la vez en el entorno virtual y en el real. . eRENA Homepage An Artist Directed Mixed Reality Environment We report on task 7b. It is a three site distributed augmented reality art installation that articulates and explores fundamental issues related to the linking between real, surrogate and virtual spaces. We describe the concept of the installation within the frame of human-to-human-communication in mixed environments, the artistic intentions, and the future development from the interactive installation to the extended performance environment. This heterogeneous undertaking combines a range of mixed reality techniques based on communication between physical humans, an avatar, an autonomous agent, and a robot-puppet.
