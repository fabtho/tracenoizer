A seminar series towards employees' organisations has been started. It has close co-operation with departments for flexibility, sustainable contacts with relevant research frontiers and admittance to undergraduate, graduate and doctoral programmes as well as the universities' channels towards privat firms and public agencies. How can users participate in this process, and how can different design professionals participate and co-operate. We are a group of researchers and designers with a background in architecture, systems design, work psychology, educational drama and physical work environments who have formed a project to explore these questions in practice. The envisionment workshop is implemented in an interdisciplinary laboratory environment for learning and change where real work can be simulated in full scale, envisioning working environments, work organisation, production technology and premises.
A new wage system was developed, but has not been put into operation yet. The employees were worried about the new system mostly because of lack of information about it and about how it will function. The positive support from the management was very important for a successful result of the change process.
