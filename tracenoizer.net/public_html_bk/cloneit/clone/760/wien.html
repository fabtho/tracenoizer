20 stations, of which 4 are interchange stations with the S-Bahn and 6 with other metro lines. Between Heiligenstadt and Karlsplatz the line is mostly in tunnel, running alongside the Danube Canal. Between Karlsplatz and Huetteldorf the line is mostly on the surface, running alongside the river Wien. Construction started in 1894, opened as Metropolitan Railway (Stadtbahn) in 1898 Closed 1918, electrified and re-opened as line WD in 1926. Gradually transformed to new metro; 1st section opened in 1976, completed in 1981.
