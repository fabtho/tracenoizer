Introduction to Animation 1685 In 1685 Johann Zahn published his 'Oculus Artificialis Teledioptricus Sive Telescopium' with many descriptions and illustrations of both the camera obscura and magic lantern. Zahn used the lantern for anatomical lectures, illustrated a large workshop camera obscura for solar observations using the telescope and scioptric ball, demonstrated the use of mirrors and lenses to erect the image, enlarge and focus it. Zahn also designed several portable camera obscuras for drawing using the 450 mirror, and used side flaps to shield unwanted light.
