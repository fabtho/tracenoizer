Sonic Zones (Klangzonen) is a concept of interactive installations in sound and space as well as a work in progress. It was shown at the Donaueschinger Musiktage in 1992. It won 1st prize in the interdisciplinary project category at the exposition of the Kunstkredit Basel-Stadt in 1993. It was selected by the International Jury for the ISCM World Music Days 1995 in Germany and 1996 in Denmark. A Sonic Station was installed in the Foyer of the National Versicherung Basel in 2000.
