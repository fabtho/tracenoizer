CHILDREN'S BOOK AWARD PROGRAM The South Carolina Children's Book Awards Program is sponsored by the South Carolina Association of School Librarians (SCASL) with the cooperation of the South Carolina Department of Education and the College of Library and Information Science, USC. The purpose of the program is to encourage the reading of good quality contemporary literature. The author of the book chosen by student vote is honored at the annual SCASL conference.
