Lewis was born 2 Sep 1812 in TN, according to the census records. He moved to The Republic of Texas the later part of 1839 along with Joseph Gideon and Isaac Gideon (probably his brothers). We believe his father is a brother of Catherine Gideon who married William Wise, and is probably Abraham or Isaac Gideon. This Gideon family moved from SC to TN and then MS. Zephaniah Gideon served in War of 1812, and enlisted while living in Blount Co, TN.
I will have to admit that I have done very little research on this family in recent years. I say recent because I grew up chasing this family thru out the United States. My Grandmother and Grandfather (Garland and Viola Kovar Gideon) deserve the credit for the research of this family along with my father (Arnold) and my aunt (Dianne Dick). I am now slowly trying to pick up the pieces from where their research ended in 1983 with the publishing of the book "The Gideon Family Record". I am sad to say that my grandmother recently passed away.
