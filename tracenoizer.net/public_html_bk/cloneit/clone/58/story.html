Die Linke hat es immer noch schwer mit Bildern und mit besonders dem geheimen Auge der Sexualitaet. Man muesste ueber Zints erotische Bilder zu schreiben - irgendwann.
The festival will introduce two examples from contemporary Hungarian cinema. One of them is Csajok (Trollops) directed by Ildiko Szabo, which holds the institution of marriage up to the light from the woman's point of view. It tells the story of three women who revolt against their status within marriage. A reluctance to compromise with the values of society versus their love for their husbands, families, lovers and others. The harsh rules of society versus love as an irresistible feeling.
