ch Samuel Schluep is a research scientist at the Institute of Hygiene and Applied Physiology (IHA) of the Swiss Federal Institute of Technology, Zurich (ETH). He studied electrical engineering with specialisation on communications technology. His research concerns user centered design and development of interactive multimedia applications for computer aided learning and teleteaching. Christoph Schierz leads the research group "Environmental Ergonomics" at the Institute for Hygiene and Applied Physiology of the Swiss Federal Institute of Technology (ETH) as a senior assistant. His special area of research interests is the influence of physical parameters as lighting, electromagnetic fields and noise on human health and well-being.
