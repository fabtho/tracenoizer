In 1995 he was invited as a Conducting Fellow to the Tanglewood Festival to work with Seiji Ozawa, Leon Fleisher and Robert Spano. 1996 he was appointed Assistant Conductor to Vladimir Ashkenazy and the Deutsches Symphonie-Orchester Berlin with whom he also gave his professional conducting debut. Since 1998 Mueller works together with the Scharoun Ensemble of the Berlin Philharmonic Orchestra and is now this ensemble's Principal Guest Conductor. In this function he also appeared at the Salzburg Easter Festival and the Berliner Festwochen.
