That is according to the first study into the real cost of private practice in Irish hospitals, published today by the Economic and Social Research Institute. The Irish hospital service is a unique and often confusing mixture of private and public - up to one fifth of patients in public hospitals receive private care. For the first time, this study has estimated the extent and nature of private practice in public hospitals, and its costs.
