Healthy Families - University of Maryland A large percentage of employees who come to the FSAP do so to seek guidance for family issues. When we set up the FSAP we felt that it was important to extend our counseling services to family members and have provided our service to may siblings and spouses of faculty and staff. A family member is defined as someone who lives in the same household as the employee, although exceptions have been made.

The Weber Family Welcome to Our Home Page We are a happy go-lucky family living in a small Southern California village named Tujunga that is located in an area called the Arroyo Verdugo Cities. The Arroyo Verdugo Cities are located roughly halfway between Pasadena and Burbank along Interstate 210 and nestled in the Foothills North East of Los Angeles . We are a Scientology family and all of us are founding members of the International Association of Scientologists.
