Labour and Nation is a filmprogramme investigating new images of labor. Which ideological values are invested into the representation of labour. What is the representation of new forms of labour induced by the global restructuring of economic relations, such as labour in the service sector or the so-called "shadow-economy". What is the difference between the perception of labour in the seventies and the nineties. Which racist-sexist implications are carried by the iconography of the heroic proletarian worker cherished by the political left.
