The Arizona Office of Tourism now is funded with a percentage of the state's sales taxes on hotels, restaurants and entertainment venues. The formula was approved by voters as part of Proposition 302, which also includes money for a new Arizona Cardinals stadium. Officials from the restaurant association and the state tourism office said there will not be any changes in tourism promotions as a result. "AOT certainly recognizes now its budget is underwritten by these primary categories of the state's tourism structure and that we're committed to providing more interaction with these constituencies in our ongoing planning processes," said Mark McDermott, AOT director. Joe Yuhas, executive director of the restaurant trade group, agreed and said he was "happy to leave (tourism campaign development) up to the professionals at the Office of Tourism.
