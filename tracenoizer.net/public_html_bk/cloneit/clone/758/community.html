Community United Against Violence (CUAV) is a 15 year old nonprofit agency which addresses and prevents hate violence directed at lesbians, gay men, bisexuals, and transgender persons. CUAV also provides services to gay men who are battered by their partners. CUAV offers speakers for schools and community groups and safety monitoring for community events.
