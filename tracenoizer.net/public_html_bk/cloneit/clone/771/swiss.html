Swiss to hand over papers to Russia in Aeroflot case Swiss to hand over papers to Russia in Aeroflot case From Reuters July 19, 2000 GENEVA, July 17 (Reuters) - Switzerland said on Monday it would hand over seized documents to a Russian investigator who is probing money-laundering charges involving Aeroflot airlines and two Swiss-based firms. Juerg Blaser, a spokesman for the Swiss Federal Prosecutor's Office, told Reuters that special investigator Nikolai Volkov would be in Switzerland from July 26 to 28 to obtain some 200 out of about 600 files relating to the probe. While a first batch of documents was already transferred last December, it will be the first handover since the high court ruling.
