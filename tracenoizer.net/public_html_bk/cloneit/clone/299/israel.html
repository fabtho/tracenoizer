. The story of Gideon comes from the book of Judges, Chapter 6 in the Bible. The children of Israel had done bad things and God caused them to be defeated by the Midianites for seven years. God was very patient with Gideon when Gideon needed proof that it was an angel of God. This caused so much confusion in the camp that the Midianites turned on each other and killed each other.
