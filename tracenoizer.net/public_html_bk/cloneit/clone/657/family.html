Together Everyone Achieves More Purpose The purpose of the BCSHA is to support and supplement the educational program of Berlin Community School through meetings, planned events and fundraising activities, and to promote communication between active participation of all segments of the school's community in its programs. The BCSHA is a non-profit, non-sectarian and non-partisan in its activities, policies, and procedures. Membership We encourage every family at Berlin Community School to join BCSHA. Membership is open to anyone and privileges include the right to vote on all matters, elect officers and hold any office of this association, approve the official policies of this association, adopt and revise Bylaws and annual budget.
