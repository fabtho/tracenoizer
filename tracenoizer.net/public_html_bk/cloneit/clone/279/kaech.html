Statement of Dr Kaech in German Surgery of the Institute in Baden, Switzerland other extensions of the Institute The Institute of Media Diseases has access to other related specialized departments. . institute for media diseases Dr Markus Kaech Director of the Institute of Media Diseases Guidelines of the Institute of Media Diseases The activities of the Institute deal with the diagnosis, analysis and cure of skin and body parts infected by media diseases. With modern technical advancements important data can be collected to discern given media diseases in a federal register. This database enables the Institute to fight effectively against these diseases.
