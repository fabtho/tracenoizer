. 19) Henrry, Bartolome Dispensation from the third and fourth degree of consanguinity asked by Henrry in order to marry Juana Burque. Bartolome's father as well as hers came down from New Madrid where they had been robbed by the Indians. He asks (Penalver) to send the necessary order to the pastor of Balenzuela so that he may marry them. Father (Isidro) Quintero states at the beginning of the letter that Bartolome appeared.
