. There are a few reasons that the USC School of Cinema-Television is the best place in the nation to study film. One is the high quality of the education it provides, another is its location in the world's most important film and television center, and yet another is the resources available to its students. Together, these elements make USC the first choice for those who want excellent education and experience in cinema-television. Students here don't just watch movies--they write, direct, produce and critique them.
