Statement
This Privacy Statement is part of the overall Legal Notice, Terms and Conditions of Use of the TraceNoizer website, maintained by the TraceNoizerTeam. Use of personal data The TraceNoizer-Team is committed to safeguarding the privacy of users of the TraceNoizer website.

Any (personally identifiable) data collected by TraceNoizer through its website may be used to tailor our services to your specific interests. As a user of the TraceNoizer website you agree with the way TraceNoizer processes this data. Your personally identifiable data will absolutely NOT be shared with or sold to third parties.

Network traffic
The TraceNoizer-Team monitors network traffic to identify unauthorised attempts to upload or change information, or to otherwise cause damage. Moreover, TraceNoizer collects data on network traffic to assess the performance of the website and to obtain (general) statistical information on the usage of the TraceNoizer website.

Storage of Collected Data
The collected data are stored in a secured database on (TraceNoizers' own) servers, which are hosted in a secure environment. The collected data is accessed only for the purposes specified in this Privacy Statement.
