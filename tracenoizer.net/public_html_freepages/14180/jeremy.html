Forward Corey Woodfolk got his first goal of the season in the 17th minute on an assist from midfielder Paul Nowak. Two newcomers to the starting lineup contributed to the Sounders first goal, which tied the score at 1-1. Midfielder Scott Bower, who joined Seattle on a one match loan from the San Jose Earthquakes, assisted on Jeremy Sweet's first goal as a Sounder in the 20th minute. Bower and Sweet got the start in the midfield positions while regular starters Sean Henderson and Jason Farrell were not able to play. This was a physical match with Minnesota out fouling Seattle 20 to 18.
