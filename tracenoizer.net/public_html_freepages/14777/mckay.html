Meet The Family Joe Niermann founded Niermann Weeks in Memphis, Tennessee, in 1978. The Company has grown considerably since its early days and Joe and his family are actively involved in the business. Eleanor McKay, Joe's wife, is Chief Executive Officer of Niermann Weeks. Niermann and McKay's daughter, Eleanor Niermann, represents the Company's second generation as Vice President of Merchandising. Both Eleanor Niermann and her sister, Claire Niermann, contribute to the Company's strategic planning and growth initiatives as members of Niermann Weeks' Board of Directors.
Ownership, copyright and title of this folk music CD review belongs to me, Kevin McCarthy. .com With his pleasing, inviting voice and grassroots folk song style, Joe McKay is reminiscent of your musical Uncle Fred providing a good time at family functions with his singing and playing. McKay doesn't bowl over listeners with breathtaking metaphor or puzzling obliqueness--his songwriting style is fairly straightforward. However, he possesses a knack for keenly and sincerely bringing to life people, places and times from throughout his life.
