It exists to represent the interests of lesbian, bisexual, gay and transgendered students, and to provide a social and support network for queer life in Cambridge. This means that Lesbigay isn't like a normal society in that there is no such thing as membership, there are no subscription charges and you don't have to register to come along to our events. We run social activities and provide welfare services to all Cambridge students. Entertainment There's plenty to do in Cambridge with many regular events running weekly. Through the long vacation the Vac bar runs in King's College on a Wednesday night.
