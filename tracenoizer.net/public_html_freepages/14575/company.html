. Since 1921, Toro has walked shoulder to shoulder with golf course superintendents looking at fairways and scrutinizing greens with the goal of finding ways to build and maintain better turf. The golf market is a critical business for the company and Toro is the preferred provider of golf course maintenance equipment around the world. In the 1960s, Toro pioneered the use of plastics in irrigation products and introduced the first central satellite irrigation system and sprinklers that revolutionized golf course irrigation.
