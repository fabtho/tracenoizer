Visual Culture is part of the School of Literary, Visual and Performance Studies at Monash Unviersity. The section is concerned with historical and critical studies of visuality in art, architecture, film, photography and the media. Visual Culture staff are located on the seventh floor, south wing of the Menzies building, Clayton campus.
