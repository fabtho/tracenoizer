A retry to fill functional Techno with contents that surpass pure drum-programming and thereby provide an alternative to the doctrine of the simple Loop-Techno. 'Two Times High' comes as a strong DJ-Tool with Body and Soul. The LP with it's 8 essential techno bangers, concentrates more on the DJ aspects, whilst the CD contains a couple of nice listening pearls on top.
For more mood on your floor you need look no further. . All the cuts are definite dancefloor builders, but display a broad range of styles and emotions - the latter being something often missing from danceable techno. For more mood on your floor you need look no further. .
Featuring live percussion, straight vocal harmonies and saxophone recordings - the result of the combination of the best sounds of analog old school gear with the use of new incredible computer possibilities. . The day after, a friend of mine who used to work for a radio station told me that it was called Techno music. All these works in 2003 are could be considered as teasers when compared to the forthcoming new album due to be released on Kanzleramt, April 26th 2004 - which will probably be the year of Fabrice Lig and Melodic Techno. It is certainly the most rich and personal album from Fabrice.
