Adam Beyer sites Drumcodes 1 (Planet Rhythm 1995) as the first record to define his style; hard, percussive and loop oriented. His need to create the perfect dj tool gave rise to the launch of the Drumcode label allowing him total musical freedom. After six releases Beyer launched his second label Code Red focusing on a slightly softer side of techno.
