. The day after, a friend of mine who used to work for a radio station told me that it was called Techno music. All these works in 2003 are could be considered as teasers when compared to the forthcoming new album due to be released on Kanzleramt, April 26th 2004 - which will probably be the year of Fabrice Lig and Melodic Techno. It is certainly the most rich and personal album from Fabrice. Featuring live percussion, straight vocal harmonies and saxophone recordings - the result of the combination of the best sounds of analog old school gear with the use of new incredible computer possibilities.
