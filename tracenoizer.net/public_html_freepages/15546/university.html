. The VPR advises the Provost on research programs, technology transfer and planning initiatives related to research. The VPR has primary responsibility to stimulate the scholarly work of the university community by providing matching funds, developing and implementing new interdisciplinary research initiatives and fostering inter-university and university-industry research collaborations. The VPR is the primary contact between the university and the federal and state funding agencies as well as industrial partners. The VPR is responsible for increasing both the amount and quality of research at Lehigh.
