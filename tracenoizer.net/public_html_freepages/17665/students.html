The office is responsible for the administration of all licence agreements and applications for university accommodation including vacation periods, the charging of fees and room allocations. The University Accommodation Office offers an information and advice service to assist both students and staff with finding suitable housing, on or off-campus, before they arrive and during their time studying at University. The Accommodation Office also helps students to find accommodation in Canterbury and its surrounding area. It produces lists of properties, all of which have been inspected and approved by the office to ensure that they meet stringent safety requirements.
