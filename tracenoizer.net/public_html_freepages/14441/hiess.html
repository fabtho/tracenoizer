Robinson had travelled from the southern state of Victoria to the "Top End" of Australia to go hunting. He had hoped to kill a water buffalo with his father's prized Winchester rifle. Robinson met Daniel Hiess and Peter Kamm at an Outback store. In a fatal decision, he agreed to go wild pig hunting with the two older men in the mulga scrub that mats the Gulf of Carpentaria. It is some of the most inhospitable land in Australia, accessible only on foot.
