A few of us IPSC shooters made our way down to Jackson Florida and shot the Area 6 Championship. We did OK at the match and had a lot of fun watching really good shooters show us how its done. I also spent several hours checking out the operation of the Gateway Rifle and Pistol Club, the host range. I asked a lot of questions and learned a lot about how a really successful gun range operates. The one big difference between what they do and what we are doing here is they have a day rate for nonmembers and a different operating plan.
