Fenerbahce has supplied over 200 athletes to the national team some of which have won the Balkan and Mediterranean championships, finished with bronze medals in the Olympics and European Championships and won the Inter-Army World Championships with new records to their name. . The Fenerbahce Athletics team has put a Turkish club's name at the top of Europe thanks to Mehmet Karasu. Karasu the head of the Athletics Branch and is also a member of the board. The captain of Athletics is Turhan Sahin whilst the manager is Edip Akarsu.
