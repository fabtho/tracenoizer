The design of The Future Learning Environment, embodies a model of progressive inquiry, which relies on recent achievements of cognitive research on educational practices and computer-supported collaborative learning (CSCL). In the design of FLE, special emphasis has been given to developing metacognitive tools for structuring users' activity. FLE consists of several modules that are designed to facilitate collaborative knowledge building at university and vocational education.
