On using footage from the Romanian revolution in Images of the World They are not so much surveillance images. They are more images made by cameramen or by amateurs or by people who worked with a newsreel and then turned the camera around. There was nearly no surveillance material in this film.
Faced with the Vietnam war right at the start of his career as a cineast, Farocki invented, with Feu inextinguible, an original way of talking about napalm designed to shock and awaken the spectator (we see him burning his hand with his cigarette). . Ce film-essai rassemble des images d'origines multiples (dessins, photographies, peintures, etc. Ces images sont pour la plupart des images montrant des machines. Farocki analyse ici le dispositif de la photographie de guerre, entre conservation et destruction.
