CLINTON POSTPONES TAIWAN TRIP CLINTON POSTPONES TAIWAN TRIP Taipei, Sept. President Bill Clinton has postponed his schedule to visit Taipei from Wednesday following the unprecedented terrorist attacks in New York and Washington. Clinton was originally scheduled to arrive in Taipei from Australia Wednesday night for a four-day visit at the invitation of the United Daily News. "For the reason of security, he has decided to put off the trip," said an official with the newspaper early Wednesday. .
