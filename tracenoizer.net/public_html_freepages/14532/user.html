Unfortunately, the administration of networked PCs and thin clients leaves a lot to be desired. Solving problems for end users is often a full-time job for IT staff members, and they are constantly working just to maintain consistent desktop configurations throughout the organization. ScriptLogic solves this problem by delivering the configuration created for each user wherever they log in. Users can log in from any PC, running any Windows 32-bit OS, over any LAN, dial-up or VPN connection -- and instantly have access to their unique desktop configuration.
