In 1995 the Nova Gorica Municipality and the Jozef Stefan Institute founded the first private postgraduate school in Slovenia, the Nova Gorica Polytechnic. It has the status of an independent faculty, providing courses leading to master's and doctoral degrees in environmental sciences. The Jozef Stefan Institute and the Valdoltra Orthopaedic Hospital founded the Research Institute Valdoltra as a centre of advanced interdisciplinary research in engineering, physics and mathematics applied to medicine. They are open to university research groups and other users and, in some areas of scientific or applied research, provide the only available facilities in Slovenia. Being well aware of the international nature of science, the Institute has devoted considerable efforts to international co-operation.
