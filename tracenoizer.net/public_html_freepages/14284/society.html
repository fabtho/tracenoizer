The challenge is for all the rest of us, in both the public and private sectors. You also combine in an exemplary way local, European and global concerns. The information society and the new knowledge-driven economy have the potential of significantly improving environmental learning and sustainability. I welcome your participation to turn this potential into reality. Feel free to contribute also to the creation of the eEurope Action Plan which is being prepared for the June European Council meeting in Feira.
Liikanen is fluent in English, French and Swedish, Finnish being his mother tongue. . Erkki Liikanen was appointed as Member of the European Commission responsible for enterprise and information society. Liikanen served (from 1995 to 1999) as Member of the European Commission, responsible for budget issues, personnel and internal administration. Liikanen was the Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary from Finland to the European Union.
