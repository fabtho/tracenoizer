Residing now in Tampa, Fl, Jimmy and Becky Pearce are not only writing and producing their music, but also are traveling worldwide again. After spending many years working toward building several great churches with great men of God, they are now presenting a powerful ministry of music, worship, and the Word to congregations everywhere they are received. Recently, they earned their Bachelor of Fine Arts in Music and were both honored with an earned Doctorates in Sacred Music from Life Christian University for their many, many years of life service and experience in the music ministry to the body of Christ .
