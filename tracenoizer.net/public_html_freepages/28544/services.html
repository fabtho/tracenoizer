HrOpen is a self financed, non profit association organized similarly to other EurOpen national associations. It has been a member of EurOpen since 1993 and it participates in all of the group's activities. The association is open to everyone who wishes to contribute to the development of HrOpen services, its advertising or any other activity in the open computing field.
