British born Holmes has certainly had a diverse and eclectic career. Starting as a DJ working the English and Irish club circuit, his earliest success was with the dance floor hit 'DeNiro', based on a sampling of Ennio Morricone's theme from Once Upon A Time In America. Songs from his first solo album 'The Film's Crap, Let's Slash The Seats' featured in a number of film soundtracks and trailers. This, together with his second album, led Holmes to work with Soderbergh on Out Of Sight.
