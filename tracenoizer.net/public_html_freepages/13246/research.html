The data provided by the Research Services Group is based on the bibliographic and citation information that ISI collects from its multidisciplinary database of thousands of influential, peer-reviewed journals. The Research Services Group maintains a file of publication and citation data spanning 1981 to 1999 that is specially structured in a relational database format for bibliometric analysis. It is multidisciplinary in scope and exhaustive in coverage of bibliographic information and citation counts (essential measures of research influence and impact).
RSG staff use major statistical software packages including SPSS and SAS, and can convert data to and from many other database and statistical packages. . Sociometrics Research Support Group The Research Support Group (RSG) consists of Ph. and Masters level social scientists who provide assistance with customized data set extracts; user-defined statistical tables and analyses; data archiving, management and analysis services; customized CD-ROMs; and training workshops. Prices for services are determined on an individual basis.
Technical committees have been established to cover four technical strands - Subsurface, Seabed, Environment and Metocean - and to define and recommend projects for execution.
