Its aim is to increase awareness, knowledge and understanding of the issues among developing country research institutions, NGOs and governments. When he retired, in 1985, and decided to live full time in San Felipe, he felt the need for connectivity to be able to stay in touch with the work he had been involved with and to satisfy demands upon him for consulting and collaborating services.
