1st International Workshop on Networked Reality in TeleCommunications (NR'94), held 13-14 May 1994, at the NTT Media Lab, Tokyo Japan. The special focus, "Networked Reality", a new term, refers the the organizers' ideas of acquiring reality at one location and presentating it at another. So this is somewhat different, although clearly related to, the more usual terms, virtual reality (VR) or artificial reality (AR). It also includes remote conferencing, virtual conferencing, virtual environments, etc. Almost two dozen papers were presented and a few demos.
