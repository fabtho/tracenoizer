Small Fish Tale is a performance developed by Masaki Fujihata, Kiyoshi Furukawa, and Wolfgang Munch for software originally developed at the ZKM in Karlsruhe. The Small Fish software is primarily an interactive music-producing picture, whose score is changed through the manipulation of graphical objects. The DVD recording features multi-angle video options, as well as Dolby 5. Additionally, the disc is also a CD-ROM, and contains the different software systems used in the two performances.
