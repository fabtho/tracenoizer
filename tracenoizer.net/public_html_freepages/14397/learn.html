The Maine Historical Society consists of the Research Library, Center For Maine History Museum, and the historic Wadsworth-Longfellow House. The Maine Historical Society Research Library is the place to research your personal roots as well as the history of the state and the nation. On our genealogy page, you'll learn how to start a genealogical search, how to use Maine Historical Society resources, and to chat with other genealogists in our online forum.
