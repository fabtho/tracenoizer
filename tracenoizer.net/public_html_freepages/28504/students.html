A classroom teacher should understand how students learn and develop and should be able to apply that knowledge in the teacher's teaching practice. Individual Advice Sessions Anyone considering study at the University is able to make an individual appointment to discuss their admission to the University, study needs, career goals, etc. A computerised, interactive career guidance program 'Career Builder' is available as a starting point for those who are unclear of career directions. Secondary Schools Liaison Metropolitan and rural schools in Western Australia are visited each year to provide information about Curtin University and its courses, and to assist Year 12 students with study options and career decision making. Parent evenings can also be arranged to suit the particular School's needs. 'University Information Evenings' offered by Western Australia's five universities will again be held across various regions of the metropolitan area this year.
