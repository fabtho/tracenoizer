Each year a campus recruitment campaign is arranged for employers who come onto the campus to conduct information sessions, and in many cases, to interview applicants for positions with their organisations. Sessions are generally held between 12-2pm and details are advertised on notice boards around campus and also in the Careers Service area. Students should register their interest in attending an employer session at the Careers Service. Student Employment Service Full-time graduate employment, vacation employment and work experience opportunities are advertised around campus and in our Service. Casual employment is advertised only in the Service, so interested students need to come in and check the files on a regular basis.
