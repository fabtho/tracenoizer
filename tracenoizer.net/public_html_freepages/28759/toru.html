Tsumetogi wo uchini oite imasuga, zenzen kyoumi ganakutte, Cat Nip ( Nihogo wasure mashita)wo tsukete mimasu. Mata uchi deha sofa de tsumewo togunowo yurushite imasuga, carpet de yatte shimasu nodesu. Byouin de kanzen ni tsume wo toru koto ha saketai no desu ga. Moshi watashi no sunderiu America no pet jyouhou nikyoumi ga arukata ga ireba, itsudemo kite kudasai. Senjitsu neko ga carpet de tsumewo togukoto de advice shite itadaita, neko desu.
