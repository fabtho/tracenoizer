In addition to underwear, the company offers a lot more, from women's loungewear to smiling watches to the Joe Boxer Cyberscooter, all with a quality guarantee and a wacky edge. Perhaps the most innovative feature is the Zipper, a 100-foot-long message strip running along a 6,000-square-foot billboard in Times Square. The Zipper is interactive, and any visitor to the Joe Boxer home page can send a message to the strip.
