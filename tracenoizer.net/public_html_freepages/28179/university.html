Introduction In 1999-2000 JMU is implementing a revised planning process. The revisions are based on an extensive evaluation of the current planning processes and have been designed to be more focused, collaborative, and responsive to the changing environment. Rose, JMU's new President, directed that the university study its mission and priorities to determine the type of institution it should be at its centennial in 2008. In the fall of 1998 a Centennial Commission was formed. After six months of thorough study and deliberation, the Commission developed a summary of characteristics for JMU in 2008 and recommendations for achieving each characteristic.
