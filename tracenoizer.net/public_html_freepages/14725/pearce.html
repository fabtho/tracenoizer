The success of the series has been such that there are Euphoria club nights around the country and a hugely successful weekly Euphoria night in Ibiza. The man with his finger on the cross-fader selecting the tracks and mixing the album is Radio 1's Dave Pearce. Dave is one of the UK's top DJs, host of Radio 1's Dance Anthems show, boss of the Nu Life record label (home to the likes of Truesteppers and Ian Van Dahl) and a club DJ in demand across the UK for his trademark euphoric trance and harder house sound. Vizzavi caught up with Dave to talk about the new album, new music, Ibiza, and the rather curious old skool revival that's currently sweeping the country.
