If heat of reaction is greater than heat of activation, you have an explosive mixture--this is how a bomb detonator operates. Just getting a small portion of the chemical to burn is enough because this portion generates enough heat to set off a bigger portion, which sets off an even bigger portion and so on until it blows up.
