Weldon Memorial Prize WELDON MEMORIAL PRIZE The prize is awarded by the University of Oxford since 1911 every 2-3 years for outstanding contributions to Biometric Science. The prize is given without regard to nationality or membership of any university. Walter Frank Raphael Weldon (1860-1906) was born in London on 15 March 1860.
