. Daffy Duck in Hollywood is a fun colourful platformer, that goes way above expectations. You play as Daffy Duck and jump your way though great looking levels among, cool looking baddies, that actually look like cartoons. The graphics aren't the best part of the game though, the gameplay is also just as good, and stands way above the crowd of mediocry that seems to be around the Platformer genre. The sound, however is very bad, and its pretty disappointing, because this could have been a brilliant title if it had a few catchy tunes to play to.
