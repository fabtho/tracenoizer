The Critical Research Initiative (CRI) program, now entering its eighth year of competition, was established to stimulate continued innovation and outstanding scholarship on this campus. The CRI program also supports research initiatives not yet ready for external funding or areas where there is presently little prospect for funding but initiatives that would improve the intellectual life on campus. Novel interdisciplinary approaches and emergent programs are especially appropriate candidates to request support from the CRI program, although funding will not be restricted to such projects.
