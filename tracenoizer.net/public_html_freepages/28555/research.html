Rowett Research Services Rowett Research Services Ltd (RRS) is the commercial subsidiary of the Rowett Research Institute. Founded in 1988, RRS combines technical, project management, intellectual property management and marketing skills to facilitate links between industry and Institute scientists. The RRS team works closely with its commercial partners to address their specific requirements using the resources of the Institute.
