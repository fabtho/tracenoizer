They typify the spirit of Dallas leaders Max and Rosa Goldblatt, whose legacy is honored annually by presenting DCCCD students with the Max and Rosa Goldblatt Award in Community Service. , April 20, from the DCCCD Foundation, which administers the Max and Rosa Goldblatt Endowment for Community Service.
