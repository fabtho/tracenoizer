NC Crop Improvement Association The North Carolina Crop Improvement Association (NCCIA) is a non-profit educational and service organization recognized as the official agency for seed certification in North Carolina. Certification is a system of records, inspections, and standards conducted with the cooperation and supervision of the College of Agriculture and Life Sciences of North Carolina State University and the Plant Industry Division of North Carolina Department of Agriculture. NCCIA is managed by a board of eleven directors elected from its membership. NCCIA is a member of the Association of Official Seed Certifying Agencies (AOSCA).
