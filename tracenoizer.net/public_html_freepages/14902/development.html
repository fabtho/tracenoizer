Following internship and residency, he completed a Kaiser Family Foundation fellowship in internal medicine at the Beth Israel Hospital in Boston, followed by joint appointments as the inaugural Medical Director of the Beth Israel's Walk-In Clinic and Instructor at Harvard Medical School. Deitz served as Medical Director for Optimed Medical Systems (OMS), where he was instrumental in developing Optimed into a premier clinical software system. While at OMS, David oversaw the development of Optimed's clinical analytic reporting system and medical management system. Optimed Medical System's applications serve managed care clients responsible for more than 25 million covered lives. Deitz worked as a private consultant for the Health Data Institute, Private Healthcare Systems and several other healthcare organizations.
