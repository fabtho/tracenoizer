Definitions Research Patient Care Costs are the costs of routine and ancillary services provided by hospitals to individuals participating in research programs. Research Patients refers to inpatient and outpatient subjects, volunteers, or donors participating in a research protocol. Routine Services are the regular room services, minor medical and surgical supplies, and the use of equipment and facilities, for which a separate charge is not customarily made.
