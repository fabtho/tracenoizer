In addition to Nationally produced programs honoring Ellington, WJFF producers will air an extensive array of his music as well, exploring the diversity of Ellington's music. Many many musicians and friends came out on Sunday January 24th for a three hour Jazz extravaganza as Live From Studio B celebrated its eighth Birthday on the air here at WJFF. Studio B Co-hosts David Dann and Carl Deitz have been bringing live jazz to our listening area almost every Sunday afternoon from 2-3pm since 1991. Live From Studio B features some of the best Jazz musicians in our area. You might hear the House duo (Carl Deitz on drums, Jake Lentz on Piano) with a bass player one week, and an eight piece latin Jazz band the next.
