(1906-1983) Career newsman - BN General Manager Born in Winnipeg and raised in Moose Jaw and Regina, Charlie Edwards began his news career as a sports writer for the Winnipeg Free Press. In 1944, he became head of Press News, the forerunner of Broadcast News Ltd. When BN was formed in 1954, Charlie was named its manager and secretary, and in 1966 was appointed general manager and secretary, a position he retained until retirement in 1971.
