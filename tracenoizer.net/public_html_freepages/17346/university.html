Murray State University News Releases Hal Riddle remembers his alma mater William Harold Riddle, a 1942 Murray State Teachers College alumnus, is making special plans to give back to his alma mater. Riddle has pledged to donate his Golden Era Hollywood movie collection back to the place where he gained the educational fundamentals to pursue a Hollywood acting career. Riddle's acting career spans over 40 years as a character actor in such films as "Johnny Dangerously" with Michael Keaton, "Hang 'Em High" with Clint Eastwood and "Speedway" with Elvis Presley, and has also included television and commercial roles as well.
