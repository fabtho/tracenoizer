Until recently, he co-managed a research project on informal mechanisms to protect intellectual property rights in SMEs. Martin also directed a research project on inventiveness of university researchers. He was engaged in a study that, among other issues, explores the importance of knowledge-intensive business services. Martin's research focused primarily on the commercialisation of science-related technologies and on academic spin-offs. Prior to joining the Finnish Institute for Enterprise Management, Martin worked as a researcher and project manager at the Institute of Strategy and International Business of Helsinki University of Technology since 1999.
