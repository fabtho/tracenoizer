Dutch Literature De Nederlandse Letteren has an index to Dutch authors in the various Web collections as well as links to general information on Dutch literature and to new journals and writers publishing on the Internet. French Literature Catalogue critique des ressources textuelles sur Internet (CCRTI) is a well annotated, selective list of textual and other literary sites. Many texts from the ARTFL Project are available only to ARTFL subscribers and may not appear in the lists of generally available texts. Literatur im Netz and OLLI have descriptive entries for contemporary literature published on the Web and for literary journals on the Web.
