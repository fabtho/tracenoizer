The data required for a local housing strategy will be both quantitative and qualitative. Qualtitative data adds meaning and can aid interpretation of quantitative data. They are complementary and in combination, these two types of data can be used to uncover root causes of problems within housing systems. Good practice examples of how data might be combined to look at specific housing problems and issues are available. In some cases data will have to be collected because it does not exist in any readily accessible form.
