. Certainly all avant-garde films present revolutions in cinema's codes. In this course students will explore the rich avant-garde film traditions of Europe and the US, beginning with the Expressionist and Surrealist films that emerged around WWI and extending up to contemporary work in digital animation. There will be weekly screenings of radical and rare experiments with the form of film. While exploring the terms of film analysis, we will also examine the relationship of politics and representation.
