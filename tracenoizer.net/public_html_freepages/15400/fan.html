Zalman has a reputation of producing some of the finest finished products in the industry, this Aluminum and Copper design was no exception, in fact given that the base is partially formed by the fins compressed between two blocks of aluminum, the machining of the base almost looks to be a solid piece. The newer HSF kits coming from Zalman now feature a 92mm fan, an adjustable fan bracket and a speed controller. The speed controller will adjust the fan from 1600 RPM (silent mode) up to 2800 RPM (still pretty quiet mode in my book). .
