Linda's kidnapper had obviously prepared for him, leaving him in total darkness, and using the sound system to play noises to distract him. Various clanging noises disguised the sealing of the door, trapping Wally within the silo. After his eyes adjusted, he saw Linda lying in the center of the room, obviously dead. He raced to her anyway, hoping against hope that his eyes were deceiving him.
Subsequent research has identified her as Elizabeth5 Lawton, daughter of William4 Lawton of Rhode Island. Further evidence links the Southworth family to the Lawtons and the Hoars through the marriage of Sarah5 Southworth to William4 Lawton. Identifying William4 Hoar's wife has opened the door to colonial family connections of significant genealogical interest.
