. 6 December 2000 12 January 2001 WALES-BASED ARTISTS TO STAGE EXHIBITION IN MILAN Under Wales Arts International (WAI), a joint initiative by the Arts Council of Wales (ACW) and British Council Wales, seventeen Wales-based artists have been selected to take part in an exhibition as part of the Wales in Lombardy season. This event will include video work and installations, and should be a dramatic, thought-provoking and highly visual exhibition. Cardiff-based artist Iwan Bala will be giving a lecture, which will look at the history of the visual arts in Wales, the present situation and the agenda for the visual arts at both the national and international level. .
