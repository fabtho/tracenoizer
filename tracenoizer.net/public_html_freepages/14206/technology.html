In the seven episodes of the series, Z Productions director Paul Granjon presents the latest Z Lab inventions (including the famous cybernetic parrot sausage and the cow saving hamburger duplicator). Z Productions is a company specialized in low technology applied to electronic arts and popular food, its research center is the mysterious Z Lab. Arno Coenen (Deventer 1972) and Reni Bosma (Harlingen 1970) have started collaborating halfway their education at Minerva Groningen artschool, where they graduated at the media art dept. They are living and working in Amsterdam and will operate as SWAT.
