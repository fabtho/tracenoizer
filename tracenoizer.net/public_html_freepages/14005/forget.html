Really a combo guard -- could become a Steve Kerr kind of player in the NBA. Small school competition usually a question, but played well in the NCAA tournament. Clutch, of course (who can forget the NCAA shot versus Ole Miss. Has good leadership qualities -- Valpo made the NCAAs all 4 years he was there. Looks like a late 1st round pick at the moment, but a good performance in Chicago could raise his stock.
He was known for his overall basketball skills, his relentless rebouding ability and his competitive spirit. He played on two final four teams in 1978 and 1979. He is the fourth leading scorer in the school's history.
