This Division has a distinguished history, being one of the first health services research programs in the United States, and continues to pursue its mission in health services research and public health. Health services research is an interdisciplinary field of research that examines the organization and financing of health care, seeking to understand the determinants of access to health care, quality of health care, and outcomes of health care. The course Introduction to HSR from the National Information Center on Health Services Research and Health Care Technology at the National Library of Medicine is an introduction to what health services research is and its history.
