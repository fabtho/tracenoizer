The most important American case dealing with the liability of computer bulletin board users was Suarez Corp. In Suarez, a journalist, Brock Meeks, posted defamatory messages on a computer bulletin bo ard about the plaintiff. The plaintiff had sent electronic mail messages to Meeks promising to make him rich. 24 This argument would postulate that Suarez is a public figure and, as such, he must prove tha t Meeks made his statements with actual malice.
