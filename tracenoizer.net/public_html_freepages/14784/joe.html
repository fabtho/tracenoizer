- joe -

Christopher Nolan's one-of-a-kind film stars Guy Pearce as Leonard Shelby, a man on a mission to find the thug who supposedly raped and killed his wife. The trouble is that Leonard has lost his short-term memory and must use notes and tattoos to keep track of events, which is unfortunate for him since nothing or nobody is what they seem. The sketchy characters include Teddy and Natalie (played by Joe Pantoliano and Carrie-Anne Moss, both of "The Matrix") who may or not be out to help him. It's disappointing then, that a film of this caliber gets a DVD that lacks any real special features. It is widescreen and contains the usual theatrical trailers and subtitle options, but the extra materials stop there.
