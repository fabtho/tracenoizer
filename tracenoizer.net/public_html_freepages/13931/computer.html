Winboard results Winboard results Intro I made different programs play against each other. The goal was to obtain relative strength of the different programs and to compare better algorithms with worse algorithms. Games were played with the winboard interface on the same computer under Window 95. In the second tournament I let them play on the same computer under Windows 2000. I strongly recommend not to do this manually but use an available winboard tournament manager.
