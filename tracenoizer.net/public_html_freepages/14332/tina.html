Tina lives in Pickering, Ontario with her husband and two children. . "Tina" is an instructor with the Faculty of Business at Ryerson University, where she has taught for the past 8 years. During the first 6 years she spent most of her time teaching Introductory Marketing, Retail Management, Marketing Research Methodology and Statistics within the School of Business Management. In 1997, Tina began development of a Marketing series for CJRT's Open College radio program.
