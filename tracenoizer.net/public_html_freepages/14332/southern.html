The new FasseTrack Inventory Control System is directly speech-enabled so that blind, visually impaired and print impaired users can operate almost all system functions independently. The accessible version is now being offered as part of the standard package. The speech-enabled version of the FasseTrack Inventory Control System was originally developed for Southern Foodservice Management of Glynco, Georgia. Compusult plans to offer speech-enabled versions of our other FasseTrack products in the near future. Sterling Creations, a company focused on making the Internet a barrier-free environment, has announced a course offering on accessibility development.
