.edu When materials and devices are shrunk to a sufficiently small size, their electrical, optical, and thermal characteristics become distinctly different from those on a larger scale. Professor Chen's research addresses thermal problems in micron and nanoscale materials and devices using analytical, experimental and numerical methods. The goals of this research are (1) to understand fundamental heat transfer processes in small scales and (2) to solve important heat transfer problems in electrical and optical engineering and materials science. The mission of the CES is to promote research on the Chinese economy and to facilitate China's economic reform and development. The CES is a member society of the Allied Social Science Association (ASSA) of the United States and organizes three or four academic sessions in the annual meetings of the ASSA in January of each year. The CES publishes an international academic journal China Economic Review (the CER) (by North-Holland) in English that is devoted to the study of the Chinese economy.
