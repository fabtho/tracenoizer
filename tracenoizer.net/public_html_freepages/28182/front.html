An area where cold fronts (coming from Canada) come upon a warm front (from the ocean) will cause tornadoes, the tornado belt through Texas and Oklahoma areas are an example of this climate. A location near a large body of water will change your climate. Large bodies of water retain heat, warm humid air is blown over the landmass from the water in the winter and cool humid air is blown over the landmass in the summer creating a mild climate.
