. Return to Act 1 (in the difficulty that you beat the game) and place Wirt's Leg and a Tome of Town Portal in the Horadric Cube. In the cow level, you'll find a load of "Hell Bovines", along with a unique monster called the Cow King (lightning enchanted). Avoid getting swarmed, and try using area effect spells to defeat them. There isn't any particular award to clearing the cow level, but you'll definitely get a lot of gold and items.
Place a Tome of Town Portal, and Wirt's PegLeg (you should have picked it up when you were in Tristram, rescuing Cain. A portal will be opened, which will lead you to the Cow level. If you can manage to avoid killing the Cow King, you will be able to repeat the level. They're Hell Bovines, with Polearms, and they aren't exactly happy to see you. It isn't a very large area, but there are an incredible number of cows in it.
