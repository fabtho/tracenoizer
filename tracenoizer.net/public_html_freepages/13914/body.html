All you have to do to come back is whenever you beat the Cow Level just save and exit, when you load your game again just go back to Tristam, get Wirt's Leg from his body, buy a Tome of Town Portal and Transmute them and another portal to the Cow Level appears. Use this process to play the Secret Cow Level as many times as you want. However if you do not kill the Cow King, you can keep going there and get lots of items and gold.
In order to access the secret cow level, you'll need the horadric cube (found in Act 2), a tome of town portal (purchasable at vendors) and Wirt's leg. To find Wirt's leg, return to act 1 and take the red portal in the Stoney Field that leads back to Tristam (where Deckard Cain was found). Somewhere along the west edge of Tristam (next to the wall) you'll find the body of Wirt (the gimpy kid from the original Diablo). Click on his body and he'll drop quite a bit of gold as well as "Wirt's Leg". Once you've gotten his leg, return to the Rogue Encampment.
