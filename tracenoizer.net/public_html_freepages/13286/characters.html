The relatively starless Merrie Melodies were the first Warner toons to switch to color, which they did in 1934. Several animators were promoted to director, including Isadore "Friz" Freleng, who remained in that position until the 1960s. It was Freleng who introduced such classic characters as Sylvester Pussycat and Speedy Gonzales. Harman and Ising owned the Bosko character, so Schlesinger's crew had to come up with new ones.
