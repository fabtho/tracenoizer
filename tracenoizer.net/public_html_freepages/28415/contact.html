One hour workshops on Resume Writing and Interview Skills are conducted on a regular basis throughout the year free of charge. Students need to register for a session at the Careers Service. Lecturers need to contact the Careers Service to arrange a session to suite their particular needs. Students can deliver resumes and covering letters for checking and collect them four working days later with comments suggesting areas for improvement. Students are able to make an appointment with a Career Adviser to discuss career options and destinations, job prospects, job search techniques, etc.
