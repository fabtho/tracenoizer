SE - SAM 's budget is established , yearly , by the State and all expences are covered by the Ministry of Culture. SE - SAM is acting as a bridge between filmmakers , theater owners and the State , thus being the sole responsible organisation concerned with moviemaking toward the State. SE - SAM is the only organisation to whom foreign and local , private or state owned film companies, can apply and appeal to. SE - SAM protects and takes care off all the rights , be them moral or material , of the Turkish filmmakers. All festivals , cumulative showings and retrospectives to be held in Turkey can be achieved through SE - SAM's approval.
