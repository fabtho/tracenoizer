TOP Behind The Scenes Sitting at home watching your favourite TV program you can mistakenly think it's only the actors and presenters who are needed to make a television program. In fact, the task takes many highly skilled people. Every Television Network has a Program Manager whose job is to supervise the scheduling of all the programs, to oversee on-air promotions for the station itself and to ensure that weekly television program details are provided to newspapers and magazines. The Program Manager's most important task is to place programs in time slots where they will appeal to as many people as possible. So, the Program Manager has to be very skilled at anticipating viewer tastes and trends.
