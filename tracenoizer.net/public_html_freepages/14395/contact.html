Charlie Edwards November, 2001 Hollywood, California - Colony Entertainment, a newly formed Los Angeles record label is pleased to announce the addition of Charlie Edwards to its' artist roster. Edwards' first release with Colony, Bougainvillia, is currently being tested in a number of domestic radio and retail markets. If you work in the industry, please contact Colony to request promotional copies of this release. Bougainvillia is published by Seven From The Sand Music (ASCAP) Following the 1999 release of Charlie Edwards, For A While, Bougainvillia is the artist's second collaboration with Los Angeles based producer, Tree Adams. 95 includes all sales tax and shipping and handling fees.
