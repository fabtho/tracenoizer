Epcot Characters At A Glance AllEarsNet - Deb's Unofficial Walt Disney World Information Guide - WDWIG EPCOT - Characters At A Glance Character appearances in Epcot have been changing quite a bit in the last year. The characters arrive by bus and get off to have photos and sign autographs. There are usually 4 shows daily at Showcase Plaza and one each at China, Italy and Japan.
