Composed of representatives elected from each faculty and five executive members elected in a campus-wide general election each March. Much of Student Council's work is done through various statutory and ad hoc committees. Students' Council meetings are open to all students who wish to attend, although if you wish to speak at the meeting you must be sponsored by a Council member (your faculty rep will usually do this for you). Meetings are usually held every second Tuesday at 6 pm, in the Council Chambers (2-1 University Hall). You can check the meeting schedule to see when the next meeting is.
