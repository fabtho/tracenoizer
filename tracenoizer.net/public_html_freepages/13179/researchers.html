Search This Site Funding Information Resources For Saint Louis University researchers in search of funding, this site offers direct links to commercial Internet funding databases such as the Illinois Researcher Information Service (IRIS), Community of Science (COS), and SPIN. These databases are updated daily, and provide researchers with detailed information regarding over 10,000 funding opportunities. The Community of Science database also provides organized electronic daily editions of the Federal Register and Commerce Business Daily.
