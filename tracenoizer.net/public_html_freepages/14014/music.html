Michael Barrett is co-founder and co-artistic director of the critically acclaimed New York Festival of Song with Steven Blier. Barrett has had a long association with Leonard Bernstein, serving as Mr. Bernstein's assistant conductor and currently as music advisor to the Bernstein estate. As music director of theatrical productions, he has collaborated with directors Jerome Robbins, John Houseman, and Sir Peter Hall; his credits as producer or director extend to concerts produced for the Great Performances series at Lincoln Center. He has released two compact discs with the Brooklyn Philharmonic; one as soloist playing Blitzstein's Piano Concerto, with Lukas Foss conducting, and the other as conductor for works of Wilder and Corigliano with oboist Humbert Lucarelli.
