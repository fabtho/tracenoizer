In addition to his demonstrated management skills, Mr. Tran has extensive experience in the development, evaluation and application of air quality simulation models, from simple Gaussian dispersion models to complex photochemical grid models. He developed the photochemical trajectory model TRACE and the inert Gaussian model OCDCPM. These models have been applied by AMI and other organizations to assess impacts from a variety of emission sources including offshore oil and gas development and power plant siting in complex terrain.
