A Westminster film and television graduate has scooped an award for his final year project at a high-profile international film festival. Adam Wimpenny, 23, won first prize in the family entertainment category of the JVC Tokyo film festival for directing a film entitled Angel Cake. It was filmed last year in beautiful Yorkshire countryside and deals with the themes of childhood, tragedy and renewed hope.
