Irrespective of real-world biology, bringing midi-chlorians into the SW universe does mess with the mysticism, and seems to make the characters in some sense pawns of the midi-chlorians. Previously the appeal of the Force was that it was value free, it was not a judge or commander, simply a resource. Midichlorians would be fine in a series such as Star Trek, (which I like, btw) but they do not belong in Star Wars. They seem to reduce the mysticism that was so important just to technobabble. One of the things that appealed to me about Star Wars as I grew older was the mysticism, especially as I began to question my own religious beliefs and practices.
