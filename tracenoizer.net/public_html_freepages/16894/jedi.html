Star Wars Customizable Card Game One expansion set to this card game by Decipher includes an Ewok who first appeared in the Ewok comic from Marvel's Star Comics. Star Wars Galaxy Magazine A bounty hunters article (and poster) written by Andy Mangels features Valance, who appeared in a handful of Marvel issues. Mention is also made of Shira being shot down by Luke, and the Jedi student Flint. The article also mentions that Arden Lyn speaks "Old Galactic Standard" and Sarcev Quest speaks "High Galactic", both mentioned in the Marvel stories. Quest's skill with a "short lightsaber" is a reference to Luke's little saber seen in the last few issues of the Marvel series.
