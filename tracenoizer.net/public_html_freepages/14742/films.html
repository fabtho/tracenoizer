It shares key resources and methods of online research so that art historians can find the resources relevant to their work. com provides an access to a centralised and reliable source of information on artists. It is intended to be a source for teachers of art history who are considering showing clips or entire films as part of their presentation of the traditional arts of painting, sculpture, and architecture. Citations are arranged in (very) approximate chronological order of the artworks, not the movies (the order I deemed most useful for teachers looking for sources on particular art-historical subjects). Belton of the Department of Fine Arts, Okanagan University College.
