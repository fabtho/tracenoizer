Focusses, at the high school level, on helping adolescents make the transition to a physically active adult lifestyle. Actively teaches cooperation, fair play, and responsible participation in physical activity. All children, from prekindergarten through grade 12, should participate in quality physical education classes every school day. Professional associations, academic experts, and many teachers across the country are promoting and implementing quality physical education programs (Appendix 10) that emphasize participation in lifelong physical activity among all students. Quality physical education is not a specific curriculum or program; it reflects, instead, an instructional philosophy that emphasizes Providing intensive instruction in the motor and self-management skills needed to enjoy a wide variety of physical activity experiences, including competitive and noncompetitive activities.
