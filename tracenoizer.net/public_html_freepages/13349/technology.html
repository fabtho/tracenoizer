Broadband non-Radio Technology Broadband non-Radio Technology On 9 November 2000 the then Minister for Small Business and E-Commerce, Patricia Hewitt announced a package of measures for the control of potential interference from the telecommunications systems digital subscriber line (DSL), power line technology (PLT) and home local area networks (LANs). One of the measures announced was the adoption of a standard to limit interference from DSL and similar broadband technologies based on the specification MPT 1570 and to be implemented under section 10 of the Wireless Telegraphy Act 1949. This announcement is available on the UK Parliament website.
