We learn from watching and giving each other feedback. We keep journals of ideas for work, analytical thoughts and vague creative longings, and we have a chance to talk about them. We join Simone in her current exploration of a process which begins with stream of consciousness, looks at the result for intimations of deeply felt intent, and proceeds to performance which is spontaneous while being informed by this intent. The goal is to develop self-guidance and artistic maturity.
