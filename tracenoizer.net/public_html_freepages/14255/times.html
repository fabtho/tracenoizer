Clarion Workshop Writer James Patrick Kelly James Patrick Kelly (June 26-June 22) James Patrick Kelly is an alumnus of the 1974 and 1976 Clarion classes, has been an instructor for six workshops, and has served as a member of the Board of Directors. His work has been recognized with two Hugo awards--1016 to 1 (2000) and Think Like a Dinosaur (1996). He was a finalist for the award in 1987 and 1998 and a finalist for the Nebula six times between 1985 and 1998. In 1998 Kelly was appointed to the New Hampshire State Council on the Arts and in 1999 was appointed to the Board of the Directors of the New England Foundation for the Arts. Kelly has been a resident of New Hampshire since 1975 and currently lives in Nottingham, NH with his wife.
