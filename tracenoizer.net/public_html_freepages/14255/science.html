. Background Background includes teaching in both architecture and computer programming. The latest developments in many sciences, especially the physical sciences and computer science are followed, with a special interest in areas with possible application to architecture. Methods Students are instructed in the necessary software early in the term and set to work on a design project resulting in a multimedia presentation. Monitoring and evaluation of the process produces data and new knowledge which is recirculated to the following year's program.
