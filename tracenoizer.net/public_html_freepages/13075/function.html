We have identified an extremely drought and heat resistant line of maize that synthesizes unusual proteins. We have obtained genetic evidence that these proteins are important to the plant's drought and heat resistance. Our objectives are to identify the genes that control the synthesis of these proteins and to elucidate the protein function. Ultimately, we hope to insert these genes into other plants to improve their drought and heat resistance. Ristic Z, Yang G, Weeldreyer T (2000) Correlation between endogenous levels of abscisic acid and xeromorphic features of leaf epidermal cells in maize.
