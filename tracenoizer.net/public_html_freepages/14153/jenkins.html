. To watch Neil place the ball and carry out his meticulous routine is a reassuring site for any Welsh supporter. When the Welsh team crumble, Jenkins represents a glimmer of hope. With this in mind, it is apt the Neil Jenkins became the highest points scorer in the history of international rugby union amidst Wales' defeat against Samoa in the 1999 rugby world cup. Michael Lynagh had set the target, with a four-year-old record of 911 for Australia.
