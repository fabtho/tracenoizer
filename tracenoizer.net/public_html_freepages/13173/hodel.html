Michael Iannucci, as Motel the tailor, gave a very humorous and endearing performance as the poor, spineless tailor who must stand up for himself if he is to marry Tevye's eldest daughter Tzeitel. Speaking of Tevye's daughters, his middle daughter Chava (Dana Lynn Caruso) demonstrated both great acting ability and a beautiful voice. And second daughter Hodel (Tamra Hayden) had perhaps the best voice of the entire cast. There was also some excellent Jewish and Russian dancing. But besides that, most of the acting and singing were fairly average, even by the main characters.
