The Complete Factual History of Vampires Vlad Dracula Timeline Classic Vampire Literature Historical Vampires The Library Dracula's Birth 1430-31 Vlad Dracula was born. 1443 Dracula, now 12 and his younger brother Radu The Handsome become hostages of the Turks to guarantee the loyalty of their father who is put back on the throne of Wallanchia. 1446 Turks take Greece 1447 Dracul (beheaded) and his son Mircea (buried alive) get assassinated for complicity with the Turks.
Walachia was founded in 1290 by a Transylvanian named Radu Negru, or Rudolph the Black. It was dominated by Hungary until 1330, when it became independent. The first ruler of the new country was Prince Basarab the Great (1310-1352), an ancestor of Dracula. Dracula's grandfather, Prince Mircea the Old, reigned from 1386 to 1418. He participated in one too many losing battles against the Turks and was forced to pay tribute to them.
