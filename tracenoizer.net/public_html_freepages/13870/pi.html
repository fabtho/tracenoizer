History The first Pi Sigma Alpha chapter was founded in 1920 at the University of Texas to bring students and faculty together in the study of government and politics. The Alpha Zeta chapter of Pi Sigma Alpha was founded in 1938 at the University of Maryland, College Park. Membership Membership in Pi Sigma Alpha is by invitation only and is based solely upon academic merit.

PhiAlpha Pi Alpha Alpha Honorary Society Pi Alpha Alpha, the national honorary society for public affairs and administration, was established in 1974 now has more than 13,000 members in 94 chapters. The purpose of the society is to encourage and recognize outstanding scholarship and accomplishment in public administration and public service. Students are selected from the top 20 percent (by GPA) of their graduating class.
