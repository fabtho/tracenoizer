Newport broke new ground in becoming the first Welsh side to host the Uruguayans. Their success in winning the principality cup last season earned the Black and Ambers the right to tackle the tourists, who reached the Rugby World Cup finals in 1999. And this result will certainly provide a huge boost for the South Wales team and will go some way to lifting the gloom engulfing the club following consecutive defeats against Leinster in the Heineken Cup. It was a great opportunity to show what they could do and they came through it very well. .
