Water Resources Data System Water Resources Data System State Climate Office At the Department of Civil and Architectural EngineeringUniversity of Wyoming An Overview of the Water Resources Data System The Water Resources Data System is a clearinghouse of hydrological and climatological data for the State of Wyoming. Funded from an allocation from the Wyoming Water Development Commission, the System offers a wide range of products and services to its users.
