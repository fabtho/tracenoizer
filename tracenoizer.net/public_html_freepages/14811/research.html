The Australian Journal of Agricultural Research is one of the series of journals published by CSIRO PUBLISHING for CSIRO and the Australian Academy of Science. . It publishes high quality research papers and review articles addressing strategic science issues relevant to agriculture. In all instances, the research reported must address a hypothesis tested with rigorous experimental design and statistical procedures appropriate for the discipline. The journal encourages papers utilising molecular technologies, or addressing agricultural biotechnology provided the application to agriculture is addressed.
