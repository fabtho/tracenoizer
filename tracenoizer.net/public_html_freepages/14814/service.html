Living in the northern Great Plains presents a special challenge where stress to people and their livelihoods is experienced in numerous ways. We are committed to the biostress mission -- a unifying focus that links the numerous facets of the Agricultural Experiment Station and citizens' needs. Knowledge gained through our research helps to provide one of the strongest educational programs around, because we are also involved in teaching courses at SDSU and working with our partners in the Cooperative Extension Service. The South Dakota Agricultural Experiment Station is Making A Difference for all of South Dakota. -- South Dakota cattle producers may participate in a program to discover the value of their cattle.
