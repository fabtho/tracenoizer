John Hoeven today announced that Lance Gaebe will join his staff as agriculture policy advisor effective June 18th. In his new position, Gaebe will work with North Dakota farmers, businesses and the new Commerce Department staff to promote the development of value-added ag production. He will also work on federal farm policy and the changes needed to produce a more stable agricultural economy in North Dakota. degree in 1989 and an MBA in 1993 from North Dakota State University. From 1995 to 2000, Gaebe was company manager for Association Management Services, Inc.
