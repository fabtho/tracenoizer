FASIMS is a multi-scale geographic information system (GIS) application designed specifically to deliver information associated with the Texas Imported Fire Ant Survey (referred to hereafter as the SURVEY), the area-wide management program, and the research program. FASIMS provides access to information at local, county, regional, and state scales. The system facilitates organization, integration, analysis, interpretation and display of information associated to each of the areas of focus of the Texas Imported Fire Ant Control and Management Plan.
