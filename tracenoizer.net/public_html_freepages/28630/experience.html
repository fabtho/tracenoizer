Caroline has been with the University of British Columbia for 25 years; she started in the Department of Pharmacology and Therapeutics, where she was involved in research, administration and laboratory teaching to Medical and Pharmacology students; she still has an honorary faculty appointment in the department and lectures in the PCTH 300 course. Bruce became Director of Administration at the Biomedical Research Centre, where she was involved in the administration of a research facility operated by a UBC for-profit corporation, BR Centre Ltd. It was a natural step for Caroline to join the University's Industry Liaison Office with her combined experience in research, technology transfer, business and knowledge of the UBC community. Effective September 1, 1999, Caroline was appointed Associate Director. Caroline is actively involved in developing research collaborations as she feels this is an important part of strengthening ties between academia and industry.
