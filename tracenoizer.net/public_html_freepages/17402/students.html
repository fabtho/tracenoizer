How Many First-Year Directors Participate Each Year. Participants may have served as library directors at other types of institutions, but the key qualification is they have not previously served as a college library director. The program is designed for library directors at small colleges, which usually means institutions of fewer than 2,500 students. Also, the participants must have a masters degree in library science from an ALA accredited library school. About 75 librarians participate on this discussion group.
