After completing my doctoral work, I taught geology and mineralogy at Youngstown State University in Ohio and within a few months I began a postdoctoral program at the Jet Propulsion Laboratory in Pasadena, California. I joined the science staff of the laboratory 2 years later and have been there ever since. My current research interests involve understanding the scattering behavior of electromagnetic radiation upon interaction with planetary surfaces.
