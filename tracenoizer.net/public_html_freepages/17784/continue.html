Kirstenbosch is South Africa's oldest and largest botanical garden and home to the National Botanical Institute. It provides glorious walks and views in 528 hectares that are almost entirely covered with indigenous species, including ericas and proteas. From here, proceed along the False Bay coastline to Boulders Beach to visit an inland colony of jackass penguins and then continue on to the Cape of Good Hope Nature Reserve. Contrary to popular belief, Cape Point is not the southernmost tip of Africa. It is however, the place to watch the seas of both the Atlantic and Indian Ocean.

Burst fire, defined as a shot composed of multiple projectiles where the trigger must be pulled for each burst, is also not penalized. Full auto weapons for game purposes will fire 3 projectiles per Melee Phase that they are operating. A character may continue full auto fire even in Melee Phases where he has no actions. Having multiple actions in a Melee Phase does not increase the rate of fire of a weapon. Full auto fire will take precedence over all further actions a character has, with the exception of simple actions like talking or walking, until he ceases fire after which time other actions may occur.
