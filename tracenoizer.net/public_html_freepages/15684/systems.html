Hesselberth is the founder and CEO of Spectracom Corporation. Upon completing his BSEE in 1957, Hesselberth joined General Dynamics Electronics where he spent five years as an electronic engineer. In 1963 he accepted a position at Harris Corporation and soon became engineering section head and program manager for government and commercial single sideband transmitter systems. He holds patents on tunable doubled-tuned circuits with variable coupling and a disciplined oscillator system with frequency control and is active in national professional organizations and within his community in Rochester, New York.
