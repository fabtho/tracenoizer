Books - QUESTIONS PRESENTED - page 2 ________________________________ QUESTIONS PRESENTED Beginning in the 1950s, the Fraternal Order of Eagles donated to cities and towns throughout the United States and Canada granite monuments containing, among other things, the text of the Ten Commandments. One such monument has stood on the lawn of the City of Elkhart, Indiana's Municipal Building since 1958 as part of a display which includes a Revolutionary War Monument and a Freedom Monument. A divided panel of the Court of Appeals for the Seventh Circuit held that Elkhart's inclusion of the Ten Commandments monument in the display violates the Establishment Clause.
