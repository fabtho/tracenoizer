Daruwalla was the examining physician of two murder victims in Goa. Now, 20 years later, he will be reacquainted with the murderer. When I started this book, I was not expecting very much. For one thing it was set in India, and for another thing the title didn't grab me. It would be a welcome addition to the John Irving books in my library.
