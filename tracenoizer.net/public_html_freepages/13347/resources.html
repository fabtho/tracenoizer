Welcome to my web pages on Maritime History on the Internet. I hope to create a site that guides visitors in methods of doing maritime history research on the internet. Throughout these pages, you'll see that I believe you must use print resources and libraries to do comprehensive research, but as resources become available on the internet I hope to highlight them and bring them to your attention here. The first set of links contains pages focusing on doing research. The second set updates my original pages on maritime sites on the internet.
