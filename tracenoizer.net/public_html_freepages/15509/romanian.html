LINGUISTIC AFFILIATION Romanian is a Romance language belonging to the Italic branch of the Indo-European family. Although closely related to Italian, Spanish, and Portuguese, it is the only Romance language to have developed in eastern Europe. For this reason, it is sometimes classified as Balkan Romance.
