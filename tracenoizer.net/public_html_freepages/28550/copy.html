. shelf-name Purpose The equivalent of a save declaration followed immediately by a clear action. After a save-clear saves the shelf, it clears the copy it made. The shelf-type is an optional herald indicating the shelf type. If the current contents of the saved shelf are not required in the local scope that performed the save, then save-clear can be used instead.
