We are using genomics technologies, such as gene expression microarrays and high throughput polymorphism detection, to determine relationships between molecular genetic changes and phenotypic changes that occur during the development and evolution of animals. Current Research Using functional genomics approaches, my laboratory studies basic problems in developmental and evolutionary genetics. Primarily using Drosophila as a model system, we have three main areas of research. At the end of larval life, the release of the steroid hormone ecdysone is responsible for tissue-specific responses that result in the metamorphosis of Drosophila.
