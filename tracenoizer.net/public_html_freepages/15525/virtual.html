From the remnants of collaborative email performances and recycled network messages, Breeze constructs intricate image poems, transforming information into enigmatic texts with multiple and discontinuous boundaries. without doubt one of the most consistent, prolific, innovative artists working in new media today. She describes her input as the product of a "virtual jillaroo". MEZ co-moderates the Webartery mailing list and works as a freelance journalist, web designer, and technopagan in her spare time. She is the 2001 Resident Artist at the WCG, has been awarded the 2001 VIF Prize by the Humboldt-Universitaet in Berlin, was shortlisted in 2001 for the Electronic Literature Organization's prestigious Fiction Award, and has just been named the JavaMuseum's Artist Of The Year for 2001.
