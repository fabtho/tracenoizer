In conjunction with the JBMarine Soccer Club of St. Louis, we will present the 3rd year of the Girls Premier Bracket that will offer a separate setup where teams will play 4 round robin games (2 Saturday and 2 on Sunday) and a final match early on Monday. U8 thru U10 will play a 25-minute half; U11 thru U14 will play a 30-minute half; U15 thru U19 will play a 35-minute half (see rules for game length for final matches). Team and individual awards will be given to all 1st and 2nd place finishers. All games will use a three-man referee system utilizing USSF certified referees (assigned by the tournament referree assignor).
