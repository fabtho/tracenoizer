While it's not directly related to Optigan stuff, there is Optigan to be found on the album, and generally speaking I think the spirit of the whole project falls right in line with the spirit of all this Optigan research. I took loads of found audio recordings and collaged them into one big mess of everyday people talking and singing about all sorts of things. Sources of found audio include homemade phonograph records, open reel tapes, and cassettes. Click on the links below to hear some excerpts in .
