Nicholls Family 1600's-2000 Nicholls Ancestral Page My branch of the Nicholls family in the seventeenth century originated from England in the County of Hereford from Longtown and Llanveynoe. In the 17th century my family owned six farms in this locality, which falls within the parish of St Clodock Church (St Clodock British King and martyr) where several tomb stones record the family name. The church has been on this site since 1110 AD, built in the reign of King Henry 1.
