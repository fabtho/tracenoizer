The Music Department sponsors three choruses of various competitive levels, the University Singers, the Concert Choir, and the Harding University Chorus. If you are interested in a vocal performance, groups like Belles and Beaux and the Good News Singers may be of interest. Harding has a wide variety of opportunities if you want to be active in technical theatre, performance, or both. There are at least three major shows a year, and several one act shows. If you are looking for something more personal, there are children's and performance groups like Pied Pipers, Theatron and the Campus Players.
How to Use this Manual This Users' Guide is designed to facilitate access to the information contained in the Basic Educational Data System's (BEDS) Institutional Master File. The Guide is divided into five sections to simplify use. This section is followed by an outline of the technical specifications and informational content of the Institutional Master File. Sections III and IV describe the State Archives and Records Administration's (SARA) role in the preservation and availability of archival records of New York State Government. These sections also provide information on SARA's access and reference services.
