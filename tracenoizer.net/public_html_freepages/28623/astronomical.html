In addition to its databases, the ADS provides access and pointers to a wealth of external resources, including electronic articles, data catalogs and archives. . The main body of data in the ADS consists of bibliographic records, which are searcheable through our Abstract Service query forms, and full-text scans of much of the astronomical literature which can be browsed though our Browse interface.
