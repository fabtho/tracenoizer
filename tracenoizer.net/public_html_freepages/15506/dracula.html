When a prince is powerful at home, he will be able to do as he wills. If I am feared by the right people, Romania will be strong. At the time, Dracula's father, Vlad Tepes II Dracul, was living in exile in Transylvania and attempting to gather support for his planned effort to seize the throne of Wallachia (2). Dracula can literally be translated as "son of the devil. " (2) Wallachia was an independent province of Romania which was the subject of a constant power struggle between its powerful neighbors, Hungary and Turkey.
Walachia was founded in 1290 by a Transylvanian named Radu Negru, or Rudolph the Black. It was dominated by Hungary until 1330, when it became independent. The first ruler of the new country was Prince Basarab the Great (1310-1352), an ancestor of Dracula. Dracula's grandfather, Prince Mircea the Old, reigned from 1386 to 1418. He participated in one too many losing battles against the Turks and was forced to pay tribute to them.
