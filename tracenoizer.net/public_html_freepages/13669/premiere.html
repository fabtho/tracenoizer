. A Tribute to Tim Curry - 1st Snow of Winter A Tribute to Mr. World Premiere November 15th, 1998, on the Disney Channel. The original choice for providing the voice of Voley was a fabulous Irish comedian, Dermot Morgan. The original voice cast's accents worked well in Europe, but Tim was selected to make the Irish accent more accessible to a US audience.
