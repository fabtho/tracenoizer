(Planning and Execution Assistant and Training System) is a program designed to provide cueing and planning assistance for people with memory and attention disorders. Typical users include patients with traumatic brain injury (TBI), neurodegenerative conditions including Alzheimer's disease, and attention deficit and hyperactivity disorder (ADHD). PEAT lets people with such conditions become more independent by helping them plan daily tasks and keeping them on schedule.
