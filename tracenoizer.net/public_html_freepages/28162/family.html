At present Dance Master Luu Hong is re-establishing the Maxim Dance Group and cooperating with the Lac Hong Performing Arts Group to propagate the traditional cultural quintessence of Vietnamese music and dance to the young generation and to preserve and promote Vietnamese arts and culture in the USA. . In his childhood, he learned tap dance and together with his brothers and sisters, established a family dance group and performed at dancing clubs in Vietnam. This dance group traveled far and wide and performed at theaters from Saigon to Hanoi with famous singers, actors, and actresses. In 1944, King Bao Dai, the last king of te Nguyen Dynasty, invited Dance Master Luu Hong and his family dance group to perform at the Imperial Court.
Last summer (June, 2000) I migrated to Canada and decided to lead a new life in the new hometown, thus I have made an English name for myself - Hudon, which is the major body of my Chinese name without tails in each character (Hui to Hu and dong to don). This is a bit creative work to try to have a real English name for others to easily access meanwhile keep the trace of my source - China and an memory of my native land. Lee is another memorial to the original root of my ancestors - Korea as people there use Lee in English spelling for the family name I hold while Chinese use Li instead . Therefore, at last, my new English name turned to be Hudon Lee. .

Admissions officers would also consider the quality of educational opportunities at the applicant's high school.
