Forensic psychology services Psychology Information Online Your Internet resource for information about the practice of psychology Forensic psychology is the interface between psychology and the law, so all psychological services provided for the legal community are forensic psychological services. However, forensic psychologists provide services which are both clinical and forensic in nature. When a psychologist treats an individual who was emotionally traumatized by an accident, the treatment is clinical in nature, designed to assist the individual in recovering from the trauma.
