The California Census Research Data Center The California Census Research Data Center The California Census Research Data Center (CCRDC) is a newly established partnership between the U. Bureau of the Census and the University of California. Under this partnership the Census Bureau provides access to non-public microdata from the its economic (business) and demographic (household) data programs for research conducted in the CCRDC's laboratories at UCLA and UC Berkeley.
