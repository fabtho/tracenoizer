Creating and Performing Strand Arts Curriculum Framework THE PRACTICE OF CREATING Republished in this protected area for the purpose of curriculum development. Creating and performing form the core of arts education. In the classroom they are intertwined as activities that are lively and learner-centered. Creating and performing often entail learning by interacting with others in a group. Teachers and students are partners in inquiry as they observe, respond physically, demonstrate techniques, and talk with one another.
