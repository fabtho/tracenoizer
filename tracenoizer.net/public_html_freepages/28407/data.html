Each table contains a citation of the data source(s). . We attempt to present data in the most meaningful format. Your suggestions, based on actual use of the Digest, are always welcome, as are suggestions to include frequently needed information. However, some information is obtained from other University offices, and we are grateful for the cooperation and assistance of those offices in annually providing data.
The data warehouse is a copy of transaction data specifically designed to give decision makers instant access to information through the usage of query and reporting tools, enabling knowledge tools such as analysis, forecasting and trending. . To support this the UDW contains multiple years, or sessions worth of data, and various point in time snapshots. These tools allow decision makers to ask new questions and an opportunity to solve problems differently. The warehouse empowers institutional decision makers by placing inquiry and analysis tools at their fingertips.
The University has designated official custodians for personnel records. . Users will access only data which are required for their job. Users will not make or permit unauthorized use of any University data. They will not seek personal or financial benefit or allow others to benefit personally or financially by knowledge of any data which has come to them by virtue of their work assignment.
