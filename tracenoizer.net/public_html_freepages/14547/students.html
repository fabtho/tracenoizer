When can a faculty promote a new course, prior to CRICOS approval. Strictly, a course cannot be promoted without CRICOS approval. International students cannot be given an offer for a course that does not have CRICOS approval. Check the faculty website for the latest information on availability to international students. Until Victorian government endorsement is granted, and a CRICOS code allocated, the courses will not be available to international students.
