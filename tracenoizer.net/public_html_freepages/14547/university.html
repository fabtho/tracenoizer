. New information and resources are being added to the Distance Education Clearinghouse on a continual basis. The Clearinghouse is managed and maintained by the University of Wisconsin-Extension, in cooperation with its partners and other University of Wisconsin institutions. Often hard to find, and sometimes harder to define, the case studies linked to in this section each address some aspect of distance education. Included are collections from both business and education.
