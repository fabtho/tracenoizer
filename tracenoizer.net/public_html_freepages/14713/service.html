Continuity as a family business is assured with sons Andrew, as Managing Director, and Philip as Director. The company is a committed Member of the National Merchants Buying Society (NMBS), the Builders Merchants Federation (BMF) and the CEMCO Buying Group. A modern distribution fleet including Crane Off-Load and Box lorries, together with a same day delivery vehicle enable us to give a first class delivery service. In addition to our Head Office branch, we have specialist Plumbing and Heating Depots in Bicester, Rugby and Witney offering the same high levels of customer service. Further expansion is planned, with additional satellite branches to be opened during 2002 and more to follow next year.
