Electronic mail, databases, shared document databases and electronic forums are the components of a groupware solution. Modern communications technology also enables several people to view the same image on their screen, no matter how far apart they are. An electronic forum, where everyone can see the same material related to a particular case, can be established in the field, so that the participants can process the case jointly and simultaneously . Business Process Automation Workflow is a group tool in that it requires the participation of a group of people in a company. But it is particularly geared toward administrative processing of an external event, from the moment it comes to the attention of the company until its processing is completed.
