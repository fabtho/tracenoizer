An ability to read music is helpful but not required - tabor pipe is quite easy to pick up by ear. Class size limited to 6, although if you bring your own tabor pipe in the key of C, I'll let that grow a little bit.
All formal writing must be correctly documented according to the MLA, APA, or Chicago systems outlined in Faigley and Ruszkiewicz. Always keep an extra printed copy and a back-up computer file of work you turn in. Regular attendance and participation in small-group and whole-class discussions and workshop activities. Apart from quizzes, individual exercises and papers will be evaluated in descriptive commentary but will not be stamped with a letter grade. More than three unexcused absences from class for any reason will have a negative effect on the final grade.
