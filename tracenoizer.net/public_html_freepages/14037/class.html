PPE - General Encyclopedia C5 Commune - Competition Jump elsewhere Jump Back to Class Action - Commonwealth Jump Forward to Computers COMMUNE A self-governing municipality in the Middle Ages. Its inhabitants enjoyed personal liberty while the commune regulated trade, collected taxes and ran its own justice system within the walls of the town. In England, France and the Low Countries communes were recognised through charters granted by the Aristocracy.
