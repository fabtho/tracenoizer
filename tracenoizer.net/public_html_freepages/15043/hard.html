PAH, MD at the University of Rochester PAH, MD works to improve the quality of healthcare offered to deaf and hard of hearing individuals through education of consumers and healthcare providers. We also encourage deaf and hard of hearing individuals to pursue careers in healthcare delivery. The University of Rochester chapter invites anyone with an interest in this topic to join us.
