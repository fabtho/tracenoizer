The Division of Biology, in conjunction with the Konza Prairie Research Experience for Undergraduates and Kansas State University Summer Undergraduate Research Opportunity Program, will present an undergraduate research forum. Participants in the forum are 12 students who have been in residence this summer and have done research on a wide variety of research topics. Talks will follow the Division Forum for Graduate Student Research format (8 minute talks with 2 minutes for questions).
