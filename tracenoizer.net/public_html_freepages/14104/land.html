George Ertel, with four sons and one daughter, came to America from Bavaria, Germany about 1829. The family settled in Philadelphia for a while and then moved on to Cincinnati, Ohio about 1836. In 1840, George Ertelwith two other men purchased 80 acres of land in Indiana--the east half of the Northwest quarter of Section 13 in township 11 of range 12.
