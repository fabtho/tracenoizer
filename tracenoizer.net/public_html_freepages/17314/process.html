Science for Families Science for Families This project will implement a statewide Science for Families project with the goal of involving teachers, parents and children in science inquiry. The program provides science content appropriate for K-8 learners and is based on the teaching of science for conceptual understanding for all students, with a focus on female, ethnic and racial minority students. Primary emphasis is on involving adults and children in the science learning process, particularly through the use of concrete materials, cooperative learning and relevant learning experiences. We hope to have this training available by the spring of 1999. .
