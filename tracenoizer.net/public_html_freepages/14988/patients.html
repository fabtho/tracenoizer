USING YOUR OWN BLOOD -- AUTOLOGOUS DONATION Using your own blood can minimize the need for transfusion with donor blood. Using your own blood will reduce, but not eliminate, the risk of transfusion-related infections and allergic reactions. Autologous blood donations are not an option for all patients. You may want to ask your doctor if it is safe for you to donate. Autologous blood collections may not be available at the hospital in which your surgery will be performed.
