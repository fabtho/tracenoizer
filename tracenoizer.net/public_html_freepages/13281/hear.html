Overview This identification game presents random sets of bird photos and sounds grouped by habitat. For each habitat your goal will be to connect the bird sounds with their matching photos. There are roughly 10 birds available for each habitat so you will have to play the game multiple times to see and hear all the available birds. Choose your skill level and then click on 'Start Matching. You get points by correctly matching bird sounds with bird photos.
