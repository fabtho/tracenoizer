If you are an incoming freshman straight out of high school or you are a non-traditional student who just cannot seem to get started on a particular writing assignment, the LWC is the place for you. At the LWC students receive personalized instruction in writing from trained student tutors and staff professionals. Many students think that peer tutoring indicates that they are remedial in some way. Students taking Freshman English as well as seniors working on an independent research project come to the LWC.
All tutorial services are available to all students at no additional fee. . LIU The Southampton College Learning and Writing Center helps students realize their full academic potential through its Placement Testing In order to identify first-year entering students who are underprepared, the Learning and Writing Center administers placement tests to all incoming first-year students whose scores are below 530 on the verbal SAT and 480 on the math SAT or whose high school GPA's are below 85. On the basis of the results of these tests, students may be placed in developmental English or math classes. Developmental Program The developmental program offers courses specifically designed to strengthen basic skills in reading, writing, and math.
Undergraduate Research Program - Homepage Search for Welcome The Undergraduate Research Program (URP) facilitates research experiences for undergraduates with UW faculty members across the disciplines. URP maintains a listing of current UW research opportunities and related national programs. The Annual UW Undergraduate Research Symposium provides a forum for students to present their work to the university community and the public. We invite faculty members to post their student research opportunites on this web site, and to use our Faculty Resources section for information on incorporating undergraduates into research and scholarship, and for local and national funding sources. URP staff are available for consultation with students and faculty during drop-in hours or by appointment.
