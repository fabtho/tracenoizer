. 18, 1998 AIR FORCE ANNOUNCES OTS SELECTIONS RANDOLPH AIR FORCE BASE, Texas -- The Air Force is giving 44 enlisted members the chance to trade in their stripes for gold bars after being chosen to attend Officer Training School by a board that met here Oct. Air Force Recruiting Service conducted OTS Selection Board 9905 at headquarters AFRS. Overall, 381 applied to attend OTS, and 142 were selected for a 37 percent select rate. As part of the selection process, board members review objective and subjective factors.
