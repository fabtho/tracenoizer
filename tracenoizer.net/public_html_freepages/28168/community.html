About - Downtown San Rafael About the BID The Downtown San Rafael Business Improvement District (BID) is a group of merchants and professional people working together to revitalize and promote the downtown business area through increased public facilities improvement, stimulation of new capital investment and blending of public and private economic development strategies. It is our continued mission to increase business for all merchants in the downtown area and to make the downtown a fun, safe and friendly place in which to eat, shop and live.
