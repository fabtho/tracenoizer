The Tempest 23-27 July The 2002 Derby Jubilee Festival celebrates 25 years as a City, 50 years of the reign of the Monarch and 75 years of the Cathedral. As part of the Festival, the Derby Shakespeare Theatre Company is delighted to present an open air production of The Tempest in the marvellous setting of the grounds of Derby Grammar School. Tickets available from the Assembly Rooms Box Office, Derby 255800. The Company is most grateful to the Derby Grammar School for allowing them to perform in the grounds of the school.
