Several test runs later I started pushing the revs to check vibration levels, etc. I stripped the motor and had it welded and strengthened at a local ally welding company. After seeing the power of the motor, I also redesigned the mounting hardware to include 12 x 6mm bolts. Running the motor in the fuselage was much better with little vibration, except at very low rpm. I had cut the header off the kart muffler and bent up a aluminum tube exhaust (see pic) which I had welded once I had achieved the correct shape.
