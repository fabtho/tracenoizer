DETROIT, MI - (September 15, 2000) - Some of the world's most talented and well known documentary photojournalists have come together in an online multimedia fund raising project called "Visions of the Sacred. " The goal of the unique Internet project is to raise money to help feed the homeless, and provide funds for treatment for the addicted, at The Capuchin Soup Kitchen and Jefferson House in Detroit, MI. The project is an online auction of documentary photographs taken by the photographers and is the idea of Brother Vincent Reyes, O. Capuchin, the pastoral director of the Capuchin Soup Kitchen.
