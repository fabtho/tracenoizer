Brother Vincent Reyes is the Pastoral Director of the Capuchin Soup Kitchen of Detroit, but he's also a photojournalist who has enjoyed capturing the stories of people's lives on film for more than thirty-five years. As a Brother in the Order of Friars Minor Capuchin since 1961, Brother Vincent has served in local Parishes, worked in Migrant Ministry, and spent twelve years as a Retreat Director.
