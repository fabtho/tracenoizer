Pylade by Pier Paolo Pasolini is a rhetorical performance based on one of the major works of European dramaturgy. East Midlands - Derby Playhouse Youth Theatre The outstanding Derby Playhouse Youth Theatre used song, music, movement and dance alongside Shakespeare's verse to retell the story of Pericles. Ninety young people performed an adaptation, abridged and directed by Pete Meakin, on the main stage of the Derby Playhouse.

For many of these members in the proposal deliberations, it is a first time to be intimately involved in the pre-production and conceptual stage of new media art.
