Garrison Associate Dean Faculty of Continuing Education In June 1994, a new graduate program was approved by the Board of Governors at The University of Calgary. This program is a Master of Continuing Education specializing in learning in the workplace. The program's content and its method of delivery are innovative; courses offered during the fall and winter terms will be facilitated by computer conferencing. This is the first application of computer conferencing at the university to fully support the delivery of a graduate course at a distance. The computer conferencing technology facilitates sustained two-way communication in a cost-effective manner.
