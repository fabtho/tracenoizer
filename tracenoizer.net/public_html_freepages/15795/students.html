Conrad Grebel takes seriously its distinctive point of intersection between the warmth and welcome of a small affiliated college and the vast opportunities of a large and renowned public university. In any given year the Grebel residence is home to 116 young men and women. Upwards of 2,800 students from across the UW campus also take courses each year at the College. The academic program of Conrad Grebel University College complements the programs of the University of Waterloo. Only rarely do Grebel offerings duplicate those of the university.
