The third gathering was held at the different venue of the Birmingham and Midland Institute, on 31st May 1986. A little over 20 people attended, with four persons from Australia or New Zealand. The fourth gathering was held at the family's ancestral village of Ansty, near Coventry, at the Ansty Social and Working Men's Club, on Sunday 30th August 1987. Around 70 people supported the event, with many of those being new members of the society. The fifth gathering was also held at Ansty, on Saturday 30th May 1992.
