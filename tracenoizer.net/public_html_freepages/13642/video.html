The show will explore our changed way of life and forms of analysis after September, based upon our newly widened vocabulary of biological implications and our ever-increasing quantities of political, economic, and social pressures. The conceptual tone of the piece is substantially slower, evoking ideas of familiarity, stillness, and solitude. Also of the subtler variety, yet bringing in the element of literal motion is the video installation by Romeo Doron Alaeff, entitled Irreconcilable Differences (Midtown Tunnel, NYC. One camera was mounted facing forward, while the other was placed to record the backward, receding view. The result is a bridging of the monotonous with that of an atmospheric and oddly all-knowing visage.
