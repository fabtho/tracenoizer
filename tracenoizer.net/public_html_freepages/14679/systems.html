Next, the workshop focuses on where data models fit into the data warehousing process and the skills required and techniques used to produce the models. Without data models, it is difficult to implement and maintain a data warehouse. In addition, modeling data warehouses presents new issues, complexities, and challenges for individuals familiar with modeling data for transaction-oriented systems. Modeling constructs that emphasize time, location, dimensionality, and redundancy mean that even experienced data modelers need to learn new skills.
