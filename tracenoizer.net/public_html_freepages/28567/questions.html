In addition, there is an instructional support network comprised of central office curriculum supervisors and administrators. The networks essentially operate as the link between the teachers in the district and the TIMSS data. From their study of the TIMSS data, the networks created a series of 13 questions that guide their work. Investigators from TIMSS and the North Central Regional Educational Laboratory examine the data to answer their questions. Based on those responses, the networks develop a series of findings that can be used to guide the staff development in each district.
