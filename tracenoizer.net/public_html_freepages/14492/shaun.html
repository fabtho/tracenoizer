Large scale mosaics in high quality natural materials. Our philosophy is to work with the integrity of natural stone to enhance its spiritaul nature using sound craft practice. Shaun and Peter met four years ago and work as a team on major commissions, both public and private, through out England and Scotland. It is hoped to bring many commissions to the workshops, to run courses in stone carving, extablish training and work with people with disabilities. A particular interest by both artists in Celtic, Anglo-Saxon and Viking designs and styles of carving could lead, it is hoped to courses in carving these styles.
