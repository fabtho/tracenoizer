Any artist who cannot work in partnership with local people to realise the communities aims should consider their position as community artists. We have worked with many communities, enjoy the challenge and consider the contribution made by local people, of all ages, not only to be desireable but essential to the successful completion of a joint project. "We don't want to stand anything on its head, but create to the best of our ability the aspirations of the community". ..
