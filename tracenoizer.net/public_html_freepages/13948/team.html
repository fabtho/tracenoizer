Eight fearsome trios are set to lock horns around the 2. 6 kilometres of asphalt and gravel of the Circuit Ciudad Deportiva Islas Canarias to decide which nation is the fastest of them all. The team-sheets read like a World Motorsport Hall of Fame. Topping the bill from rallying will be the winners of the last three rounds of the 2001 World Rally Championship, with Marcus Gronholm (Telstra Rally Australia), Jesus Puras (Rally of Corsica) and Gilles Panizzi (San Remo Rally) all eager to wow the enthusiastic crowds. From bikes, the recently crowned champions of both the 500cc GP and World Superbike Championships, Valentino Rossi and Troy Bayliss, will be similarly looking to set the crowds alight.
