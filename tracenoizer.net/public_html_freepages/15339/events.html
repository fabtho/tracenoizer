Volleyball 2001 Golf Season Outlook The ECC golf team is off to a great start for the 2001 golf season. We have two returning players this year-Kevin Konacki,a Streamwood High graduate and Jack Scurte, from St. The Spartans have finished second in their first two Skyway Conference events this year, and Mitch Clements was the conference medalist for both of these. The team has the Region IV tournament the first weekend in October and hopes to qualify for the Nationals in June at Scottsdale, Arizona.
