. Lake Zurich Middle School North History The History of Lake Zurich Middle School North Lake Zurich Middle School North was built in 1929 but was a high school till 1972. Before 1929 Lake Zurich High School students had to go to Barrington or Wauconda for their education. When LZMSN opened their doors in 1929 they were school district 125. Ela and Vernon students attended school together till 1965.
