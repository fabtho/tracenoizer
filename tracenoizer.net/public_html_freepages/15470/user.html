. ISAACTM is a patented, powerful, fully individualized cognitive prosthetic system specifically designed for individuals with a wide range of cognitive disabilities. It allows for the organization and delivery of individualized procedural and personal information and prompts, assisting the user to live and work more independently. The information and prompts are created -- in English or Spanish -- by a rehabilitation professional using ISAAC AuthorTM software on a standard PC. Since the information and prompting content is fully individualized for the capabilities, needs and situation of the user, this system is well suited for both compensatory and some restorative assistive technology applications.
