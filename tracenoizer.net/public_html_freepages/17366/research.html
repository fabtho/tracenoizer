Vanderbilt Science and Research Communications anderbilt University is taking a bold new approach to informing the American public about the exciting world of science and engineering research. The goal is to break down communication barriers that exist between the researchers and the public so that all can understand and appreciate the importance of science and engineering in our lives. A new contract interdisciplinary major has been created that combines science, engineering, technology and communications in a way that prepares students for a broad range of fascinating careers.
