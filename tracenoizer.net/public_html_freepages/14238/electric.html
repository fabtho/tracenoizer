These pieces were pre-selected from a group of 63 submissions received from 18 countries. 0 competition is shared by Scott Draves for Electric Sheep, a collectively generated screensaver, and Haruki Nishijima for Remain in Light, an interactive sound and light installation. This latter project was also the public's choice at the presentation of the awards in Madrid.
