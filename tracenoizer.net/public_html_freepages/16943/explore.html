nl Helen Thorington is administrator of a primarily new media arts organization in New York City. She is also the initiator and producer of the New American Radio series which has been in existence for the last ten years. New American Radio has commissioned hundreds of artists to explore and create for the radio medium. Thorington is mostly known as a sound or radio artist. She is concentrating on new media and the internet the last few years.
