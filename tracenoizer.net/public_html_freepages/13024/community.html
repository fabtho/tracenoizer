Bastian Interviews Bastian Bastian was originally called Parkersburg. Bastian was influential in bringing the railroad and Virginia Hardwood to town and it was renamed in his honor. Bastian was a vibrant and thriving community in the 1930's and 1940's. It was home to the large Virginia Hardwood Lumber Company and the site of a Civilian Conservation Corps Camp during the Great Depression. It was the first place in Bland County to have electricity.
