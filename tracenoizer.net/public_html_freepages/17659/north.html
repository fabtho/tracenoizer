THOR North System Information THOR-North System Information The RAP portion of the THOR-North project is a fairly large deployment of both the NCWF (National Convective Weather Forecast) and Auto-Nowcaster systems on areas in the NorthEast corridor of the US. The NCWF portion of the system is being called RCWF (Regional Convective Weather Forecast) because it is being run on lower resolution data from just this corridor. The Auto-Nowaster portion of the system (which I usually just call the Nowcaster) will be run on 2 of the radars in the corridor.
