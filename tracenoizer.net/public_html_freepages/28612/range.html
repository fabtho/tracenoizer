Reflections Eight years into implementation of the step-salary scale that sought immediately to adjust salaries to correct past inequalities and to establish a salary base on which to plan and build future increases, it is clear that it has made a difference in staff compensation. Concurrently, it is imperative to continue to make a strong financial commitment to the Assistants to attract and hold capable professionals and to continue to reflect better their work, their role in the classroom, and the esteem in which they are held. A continuing goal should be to reflect education, degrees earned, and years of experience in determining and setting even-handed Assistant pay in the future, as recommended by the Long Range Plan. These salary adjustments grow from a base of prudent and effective financial planning. These numbers reflect our commitment to the people of MSR.
