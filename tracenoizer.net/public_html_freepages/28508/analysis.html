We make no claim to be the definitive word on any of these issues. Instead, the discussions are intended to stimulate thinking about the issues, and to provide a starting point for thoughtful analysis and research. Although only five of the resolutions included will actually be used in competition during the year 2002, each analysis provides commentary on issues that recur within Lincoln Douglas debate over a period of time.
