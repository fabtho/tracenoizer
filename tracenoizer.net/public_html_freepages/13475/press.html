Editor, The Times Herald, Olean, April 1997 to August 2001. Began at The Times Herald in March 1982 as a general assignment reporter and worked in different editorial jobs before becoming editor. Prior to that, he worked as a reporter and later as city editor at the Salamanca Press, January 1979 to March 1982. Bonaventure with more than 20 years of experience as a reporter and editor. During that time, he established a reputation as one of the most respected journalistic voices in the region among both the general public and his colleagues.
