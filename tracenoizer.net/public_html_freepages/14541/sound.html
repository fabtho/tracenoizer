In 1996, the Federal Communications Commission (FCC) approved a new U. standard for the next generation of television digital television. In a digital system, images and sound are captured using the same digital code found in computers ones and zeroes. High Definition Television (HDTV) is a much higher resolution or clarity than today's analog television. And because HDTV is digital, you will have the benefit of six-channel CD-quality Surround Sound.
