The artist positions herself as both spectator and media spectacle in scripted performances; she uses video to record and add dimension to the complexity of her position as a woman in a technocratic society. Kristin Lucas received a BA from Cooper Union for Advancement of Science and Art. Her videos have been screened in The Young and the Restless exhibit at the Museum of Modern Art, NYC. Her work was included in the 1997 Whitney Biennial, NYC.
