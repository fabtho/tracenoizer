U of W Library's Electronic Data Service University of Waterloo Library Electronic Data Service The University of Waterloo Library Electronic Data Service (UW-EDS) is a data location, retrieval and delivery service for University of Waterloo faculty, students and staff to support teaching and research. Besides servicing data delivered throught TUG Data Resources, UW-EDS also acquires and maintains collections of machine-readable data files specific to the needs at the University of Waterloo.
