Gradually, a fictional character comes into being, a woman sitting behind her computer somewhere in Western Europe. Edit Kaldor does not only offer an exciting reflection on the nature of the relationship between human beings and their computers, on a further level she manages to convince us in Or Press Escape that a desktop can be turned into an eventful stage. She studied literature and drama at Columbia University (New York) and at DasArts (The Amsterdam School for Research in Theatre and Dance). She presently lives and works in Amsterdam and Brussels, making theatre performances that often integrate various forms of digital media.
