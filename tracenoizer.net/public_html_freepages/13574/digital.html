Hosted by the picturesque Austrian city of Linz (4. 99), the festival has been at the forefront of digital media art, querying the intersections between technological change and cultural production. Starting out 1979 as an electronic music event, initiated by the ORF (Austrian Broadcasting Company in Upper Austria) and the cultural centre Brucknerhaus, the vast developments in IT soon caused the organisers to include other art forms which utilise digital media. Over the years AEC has become more and more of an authority within the digital arts community.
