He was also co-founder of a biotechnology company, Xenometrix GmbH, in 1995, and of the Internet portal swisstox. . Hodel is a MD promoted at the University of Basel, Switzerland in 1962. He followed a postgraduate training in pathology as assistant and resident in the institute of pathology in Basel from 1962 to 1967 and was board certified. Hodel was a research associate at Yale University in New Haven, Connecticut, USA in the anatomy department doing advanced training in electron microscopy and cytochemistry.
Hodel also enjoys singing lieder recitals, and has recently performed Schubert's Die Schoene Muellerin, Schumann's Dichterliebe, Beethoven's An die ferne Geliebte and Liszt's Tre Sonetti di Petrarca. . Hodel could be seen performing the roles of Lensky in Tchaikovsky's Eugene Onegin, Nemorino in Donizetti's L'Elisir d'amore, the Duke in Verdi's Rigoletto and Rudolfo in Puccini's La Boheme. During last season, he was seen in such diverse roles as Don Jose in Bizet's Carmen , Count Almaviva in Rossini's Barber of Seville and Alfredo in Verdi's La Traviata.
