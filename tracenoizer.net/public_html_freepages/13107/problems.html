Math for Kids - A Medieval Adventure in Problem-solving click here to view this site A ThinkQuest Junior 1998 Entry Click image for the Site Site Desciption Math for Kids - A Medieval Problem-Solving Adventure, is a site for fourth graders who want to improve their math problem-solving skills for school or for competitions like Math Olympiad. All the math problems were written by the two fourth grade team members, and we hope through our form page to get new problems from other students visiting the site. Many math students have trouble with word problems. One section of the site is directed at learning how to do word problems and practice.
