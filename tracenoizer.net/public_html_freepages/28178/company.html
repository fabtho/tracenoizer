Altinex ASA (Altinex) has sold its commersialisation rights for the Masko-Zoll patent (sludge de-watering technology) to WWET Technologies Ltd. WWETT is a registered company in Calgary, Canada who is planning to list on CDNX during 2001. WWETT has acquired Altinex' sole rights to international commersialisation of the company's patented Masko-Zoll technology. Altinex subsidiary Mercur Envirotech AS has at the same time acquired a non-exclusive license from WWETT for use of this technology within the market areas where Mercur Envirotech has its interests.
