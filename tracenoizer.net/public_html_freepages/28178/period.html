On Wednesday, June 17, the MEC Grievance Committee filed an MEC grievance (MEC 98-06-11) against the Company seeking relief from the Company's misinterpretation and misapplication the US2 Letter of Agreement. This grievance is a result of the Company's decision to unilaterally assign additional duties to MetroJet (US2) pilots and ordering these pilots to perform post flight walkarounds at the conclusion of the pilots' last fight in a duty period. The Company made this decision despite ALPA's protests during meetings of the MetroJet Task Force and subsequent objections against this attempt to assign additional duties without proper compensation. The Association has requested expedited consideration of this grievance and will pursue expedited arbitration if necessary.
