GCMD Science User Working Group GCMD Science User Working Group Representing the broad range of Earth science disciplines including life sciences, oceanography, geophysics, and atmospheric science is an active GCMD Science User Working Group. . Planetary Data System Home The Planetary Data System (PDS) archives and distributes scientific data from NASA planetary missions, astronomical observations, and laboratory measurements. The PDS is sponsored by NASA's Office of Space Science. Its purpose is to ensure the long-term usability of NASA data and to stimulate advanced research. Additional information is also available in the PDS Annual Report.
