Titch Kits These are designed to fit into greetings cards, frames, hoops, coasters etc. You can frame the finished article in any way you like as framing medium is not supplied. All designs are supplied with sorted thread, symbol chart, full instructions and an illustration. They are simple and novel making them fun to sew and fun to receive. .
In the unlikely event of you not being entirely satisfied, return the items concerned in a clean and resaleable condition within 14 days of receipt and your money will be refunded or credit given. New designs are being added regularly so if you do not find what you want listed it is always best to telephone to see if we have anything suitable since the catalogue was compiled. If you purchase a chart only pack you will receive a graph, a key for thread colours, basic instructions, and a picture of the finished design. If you purchase a kit you will receive all the above plus 14 hpi Aida fabric (unless a different arrangement is made), plus cut and sorted thread on a useful organiser. Stitch count is given in preference to size to enable the stitcher to calculate the size of the finished work using any fabric they choose.
