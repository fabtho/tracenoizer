I have participated in art shows from New York to Miami. My paintings adorn many a collectors' walls world wide. In 1984, my entire one-man-show of 80 original paintings was purchased by an individual from Pennsylvania for his private collection. In 1987, I was chosen by the White House to decorate Easter eggs for First Lady Nancy Reagan. These are now on display in the Smithsonian as part of the National Treasury.
