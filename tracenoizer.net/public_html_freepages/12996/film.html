DAFFY DUCK (C) 1992 HI-TEC This arcade adventure needs no intruduction. The game is multi-load, loading each of the seven colorful cartoon levels separately. The first level portrays 'The Film Studio' as a horizontally scrolling corridor with enemies to jump over or temporarily stun and includes doors that lead to various rooms and film sets. They sometimes give you useful items, but only if you bring them a requested object. The second level takes Duffy underground to 'The Sewers' under the film studio, past gushing pipes and mutant fish as he searches for an exit key.
