Here he continues to grow as a painter and professional artist. He looks forward to doing more landscapes and perhaps working in oils. But most of all he enjoys the daily inspiration of New Mexico, with its light, landscape, and mystery. Artist's Statement As an artist I am powerfully drawn to close up objects in nature, as well as the space that surrounds the object. I enjoy taking something out of its reality and placing it along surrounded by light or shadow.
