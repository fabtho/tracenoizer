To make this record even more exciting, Static invited guests such as Ronald Lippok (To Rococo Rot, Tarwater) and Justine Electra to join him in his studio. - Great album by this young Japanese band putting together electronic with abstract jazz influences, using samplers and evident deep roots in the musical history. Samples, loops and electronic are used in all possible ways and crossed with staccato rhythms, creating a really rich and exciting sound in today's modern electronic music. A definitely very interesting, thrilling and modern release. This record immortalizes the concert week happening in the Batofar in Paris, including the artists who performed at the festival in December 2001 and other Japanese artists.
