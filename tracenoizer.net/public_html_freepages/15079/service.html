Mission Statement ITD's mission is to use information technology to facilitate access to government information and services when and where it is convenient to Iowans. Department Vision To provide the best data processing services to our customers through the effective utilization of existing and new information technology. ITD will implement and support technologies and processes that will increase service to customers and enhance staff productivity. 2002 ITD strategic planning goals Implement enterprise initiatives that provide e-government information and services 24 hours a day, seven days a week for our customers. Maintain an IT infrastructure that supports enterprise technology solutions, facilitates interoperability and connectivity, achieves consistently high service level and supports research and development efforts within the enterprise.
