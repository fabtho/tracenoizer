00 Slammin' hip-hop beats, spooky electronics on this 1997 release, only a few of which remain. 00 The debut release from San Francisco's own Gresham Taylor, known and appreciated until this release only for his live performances. 00 Consisting of MADDER ROSE's Billy Cote and friends, this group's debut features vocalist Don Greene kickin' his dark poetic style on the A side, with a very DJ-friendly instro on the flip. Downtempo trip-hop with lots of alternative, college rock and experimental pop overtones. Cool beats, bass grooves and turntable effects perfect for that night drive through the desert.
