Get Netscape here WINDING RIVER REVIEW The Winding River Review is a student produced anthology of works by students from area school districts. Students submit written and graphic works to a student editorial board who then publish the anthology. Editors are selected from nominations submitted by Media Technology Initiative (MTI) schools and receive a stipend, e-mail address and serve on a multi-district board.
