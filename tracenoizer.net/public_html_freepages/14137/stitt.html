In 1991, release of 'Nightmare' the first truly 3D virtual reality game with Daniel's soundtrack. In 1993 he was invited to play at 'Les Rencontres Poetiques Internationales de Bretagne' in Saint-Malo. In 1995 Daniel stated a new project with Stitt and he composed a String quartet which was premiered in October 1995 at The Burgh House in Hampstead, thanks to the help of his composer friend Stewart Lane. Images and recollections of the past pervade the meditative atmosphere of the first movement. This asymmetrical time signature gives the impression of relentless progression somewhat thwarted because of its slightly inconclusive nature.
