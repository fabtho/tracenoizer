The course of study is flexible and is tailored to students' individual needs. Some pursue their own talent across a wide range of flora and fauna, others concentrate on the production of a more focussed body of work. There is no standard approach to work, no 'house style' or conformity among the Natural History students. Natural History work is carried out either on location or in the studio. There are lectures and seminars on the environment and its conservation, designed to place illustration in a wider cultural and political context.
