Twenty-one established and emerging artists have been commissioned to make new site-related works over a four-week period, throughout October 2001, at Dilston Grove (formerly Clare College Mission Church) in Southwark Park. In focussing upon residencies for performance artists, Span2 proposes process as a vital ingredient of performance art, possibly more so than in other art forms. This might seem obvious, as performance artworks are always temporary and often influenced by the place and time in which they occur. Yet, it seems that the role of process in performance art does not receive as much critical focus as in other art forms.
