The faulting in the area between San Diego and Tijuana is complex. Several major potentially active faults pass through or close to the International Border Planning Area. In Mexico, these include the Coronado-Agua Blanca fault zone and the Vallecitos-San Miguel fault zone. Of these, Rose Canyon and Vallecitos-San Miguel are considered the most hazardous on-shore fault zones, followed by the off-shore Coronado-Agua Blanca fault. The major fault zone in the San Diego area is the Rose Canyon fault.
