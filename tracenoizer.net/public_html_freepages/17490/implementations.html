By July 1973, considerable changes from the last versions of FTP were made, but the general structure remained the same. RFC 542 was published as a new "official" specification to reflect these changes. However, many implementations based on the older specification were not updated. In 1974, RFCs 607 and 614 continued comments on FTP. RFC 624 proposed further design changes and minor modifications.
