The principal rivers are the Wisla or Vistula and Oder or Odra. Rainfall mostly occurs during the summer months and the average annual precipitation varies from 500 mm (20 inches) to 1,220 mm (48 inches) depending on the region. Average temperature ranges in Warsaw are from -5 to 0 degrees Celsius (23 to 32 degrees Fahrenheit) in January to 15 to 24 degrees Celsius (59 to 75 degrees Fahrenheit) in July. Other ethnic minorities include Ukrainians, Belarussians, Germans and Jews. In 1947 the PPR won parliamentary elections and banned all opposition parties.
