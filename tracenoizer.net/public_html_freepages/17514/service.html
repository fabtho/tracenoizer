And who had known that I'd spent that first night of my freedom, my first night as a centaur out in the world, here, on that very spot. Being the good corporate centaur that I was (sure, right), I arranged to have the car towed home. I had to go up to level 1 to use my cell phone; no service down in the concrete cave that is level three, naturally. While I waited, the mind was working in overdrive, and after going over this mystery for the umpteenth time, I guess I was a bit frazzled. I know my front hooves were tap-tapping all over the place, so anyone watching who knew me could've told you that I was getting agitated.
