Solar Electric Propulsion The principal of SEP design (Figure 2) is similar to that of a basic rocket motor where gases are generated in a contained area and directed out one opening in the containment. This creates an uncompensated force on the opposite side of the combustion chamber which thrusts the motor forward. However, SEP design does not use an oxidation-reduction chemical reaction between propellant and oxidizer to create the expanding hot gas.
