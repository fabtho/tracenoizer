Surveys Client Satisfaction and Utilisation Survey August 2000 This page reports the results of a client satisfaction and hearing aid use survey of clients of the Hearing Services Voucher System which was undertaken by the Office of Hearing Services in August 2000. To determine how helpful the hearing aids were in everyday activities. To determine and measure the level of problems clients experience with their hearing aids. To determine if action had been taken by the client and the practitioner on problems. To determine any gaps in information provided by practitioners.
