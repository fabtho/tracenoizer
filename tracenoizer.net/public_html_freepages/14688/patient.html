Empysema is a progressive, destructive lung disease, usually caused by smoking. The disease destroys air sacs in the lungs which enable oxygen to enter the bloodstream and let carbon dioxide leave the body. Eventually, the damaged air sacs inhibit the patient's ability to breathe properly. As the disease continues, there is progressive inflation of the lungs, resulting in further impairment.
