ArabNet -- Algeria, History, French Colonisation French colonisation (1830-1962) Algeria was annexed to France despite intense popular resistance. Resettlement programmes were implemented by the French government using land-owning incentives to draw French citizens to the new colony. The colonials exploited the country's agricultural resources for the benefit of France.
