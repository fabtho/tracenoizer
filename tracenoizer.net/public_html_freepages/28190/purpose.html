Communication Team Communication Team Objectives To inform and apprise customers, (the individuals that will be expected to use the reports and on-line screens as part of their job duties), of the project and it's purpose. To solicit input from customers and provide feedback to the project team to avoid significant issues or concerns prior to implementation of the reports and on-line screens. To convey how using the reports and on-line screens will benefit the individual customer.
Library Working Groups Charter RATIONALE Effective communication is essential to a productive organization. It is important now as the Library moves to a more collaborative environment. PURPOSE To view broad-based communications as an essential part of the Library organization. To promote effective communication skills as an individual responsibility. To foster standards of efficiency, relevance and openness for information collection and communication within the Library.
