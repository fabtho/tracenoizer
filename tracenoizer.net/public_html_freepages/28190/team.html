The team is strongly focused on limitations to current technology and on exploring means to overcome these limitations so that quality services with more features can be delivered in a cost effective manner. .The strength of the team is built on interactive usage of their skills and the skills of a well established network with industry and academic institutions. The team has a history of successfully completed development projects with the radio, audio and communication industries within New Zealand and internationally. In addition the team has an impressive record of scientific publications and presentations at conferences.

It must be possible to link with others within the colony, to communicate easily with workers outside the Marsville structure and also to talk to Mission Control on Earth. Methods of communication that work on Earth may not function in the same manner under the conditions of Mars. This communication team will need to co-ordinate, manage and monitor arriving and departing spacecraft, exploration activities on the planet and communications with Earth. Spacecraft from Earth will be bringing supplies, other crew members and equipment. These craft will need to be guided to the colony and given directions during landings and departure.
