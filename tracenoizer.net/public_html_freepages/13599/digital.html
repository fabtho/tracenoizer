.com Will Pappenheimer is Area Coordinator of Electronic Intermedia (EIM) at the University of Florida. He directs the multimedia and digital video program and has shaped the EIM curriculum during the past 4 years. He has exhibited nationally since 1985 with 8 solo shows in LA, Chicago and Philadelphia. .
The hopes and fears of the future of a nation were being provoked by a symphony of blinking and strobing lights and frame rates. A tawdry effect that dazzles and invokes fear among the masses. I made Info-receptor at a time when aspects of my life began to shift from analog to digital. Info-receptor is a flashy, low-budget, dim-witted, infomercial with a built-in sense of irony. I shot half on videotape and half on film, animating not only the subject but also dramatizing the slogging shift from analog to digital, sugar-coated with enthusiasm and ambivalence.
Liverpool Art School (first studio to Lennon and Sutcliffe) and the Liverpool Institute for Performing Arts (McCartney and Harrison's school-days) host the REVOLUTION Symposia. Here are the forums and laboratories in which to identify, explore, assert and critique the notions and metaphors of Revolution - a word that describes both cyclic completion and severance. A process of continuous publication will collate and order these activities in simultaneous editions of digital, internet, video and paper formulations. Escape routes to Digital Summer happenings - Manchester's techno festival - have already been identified and will be patrolled regularly for defectors.
