IIN Projects Invisible Inhabitants Network launched as an organization during July of 2000 at which time Kristin Lucas was participating in an artist residency program in the North Tower of the World Trade Center. This organization was formed in response to curiosities about whether or not electronic emissions, such as radio waves, are capable of comingling and communicating with the spirit world. Several research projects began at the World Trade Center and other locations.
