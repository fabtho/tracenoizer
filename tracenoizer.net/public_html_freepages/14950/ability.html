His competitive motorsport experience extends back to his early teens, where he was the winner of many junior motorcross titles before injury forced his switch to the less dangerous sport of karting, winning his first ever race. In 1991 he switched to Production Cars, immediately demonstrating his ability to win with several championship victories leading up to his 1994 invitation to join the Opel works team. Watson-Smith created a landmark season in 1995 with a season class win and a victory in his debut Touring Car event when filling in for the absent Mike Briggs. Since that time he has continued with Opel, bringing them unprecedented Production Car success and it this ability that has proven crucial to the steadily improving effort of Team Petronas Syntium Opel's Touring Car campaign.
