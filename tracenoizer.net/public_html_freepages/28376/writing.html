LIU The Southampton College Learning and Writing Center helps students realize their full academic potential through its Placement Testing In order to identify first-year entering students who are underprepared, the Learning and Writing Center administers placement tests to all incoming first-year students whose scores are below 530 on the verbal SAT and 480 on the math SAT or whose high school GPA's are below 85. On the basis of the results of these tests, students may be placed in developmental English or math classes. Developmental Program The developmental program offers courses specifically designed to strengthen basic skills in reading, writing, and math.
