. The Idea During last ten years there has been a rapidly rising interest in effects taking place in systems with very small tunnel junctions of sub micron size at temperatures of 100 mK. The quantum nature of electron charge state leads to devices which manifest single electron effects. The basic device is the single electron transistor (SET), which consists of an island isolated by two tunnel junctions. C is the sum of the capacitances of the tunnel junctions and the self capacitance of the island.
