- pearce -

Graduating from the Arts degree in 1978, he completed an Honours year in composition, which he had studied with Ross Edwards and Peter Sculthorpe, and graduated from his B. He was awarded the University Medal and the Sarah Makinson Prize for Composition on graduation. In previous years Pearce had also won the Frank Albert Memorial Prize and the Ignaz Friedman Memorial Prize. A Rotary Scholarship to Sussex University, awarded in 1980, enabled him to continue his studies in composition under Jonathan Harvey at Sussex University. In 1981 Pearce took up a position as a lecturer at the NSW Conservatorium of Music.
