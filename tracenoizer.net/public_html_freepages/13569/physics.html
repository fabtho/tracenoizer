This multidisciplinary research center provides a focus for rapidly expanding research, education and training in spin-based electronics and quantum computation, with an emphasis on the potential realization in coherent electronic, magnetic and photonic nanostructures. The Center brings together researchers from physics, materials science, and electrical engineering to address new paradigms for quantum electronics that rely on the electronic and photonic manipulation of quantum information in semiconductors. Several of the projects are aimed at investigating the physics of quantum coherence and the engineering of quantum computation in semiconductors. .
