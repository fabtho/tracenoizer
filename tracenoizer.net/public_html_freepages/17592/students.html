ICP Educational Resources Software The Digital Frog Your Source For The Finest In Educational Software. The Digital Frog is a dynamic and effective educational tool. It is an interactive CD-ROM that enables students to learn about the anatomy and ecology of the frog. The student workbook guides the student through the disc with tips, suggestions, quizzes and cross-word puzzles and acts as an excellent study guide. The teacher workbook includes the answers to the student workbook, teaching suggestions and directions on where to find the needed information on the CD.
