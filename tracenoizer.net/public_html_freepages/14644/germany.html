Dirichlet proved case 1 and presented his paper to the Paris Academy in July 1825. The complete proof was published in September 1825. On 28 November 1825 General Foy died and Dirichlet decided to return to Germany. He was encouraged in this by Alexander von Humboldt who made recommendations on his behalf. There was a problem for Dirichlet since in order to teach in a German university he needed an habilitation.
