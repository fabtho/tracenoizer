So if you have the time, this makes a wonderful gift. It is the best way to wake up your love, telling them how much you love them and waking up to recorded kisses and sweet words from you. It really makes a person feel good about themselves, the relationship and of course the other person. Get an anti-fog stick and write love notes on the bathroom mirror so when your mate gets out of the shower the message will show up in the steam. A new window will come up with this URL already printed in it for you, just click "send".
