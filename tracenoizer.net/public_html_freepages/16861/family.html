A new public room for the Division of Special Programs in Lewisohn Hall was dedicated in memory of Ward H. Dennis, dean of the School of General Studies from 1977 to his death in 1992, in a ceremony last Friday. Members of his family were on hand as Provost Jonathan R. Cole and Frank Wolf, dean of the Division of Special Programs, spoke about Dennis' long-standing commitment to bringing the best a major research university has to offer to qualified people from all sections of society and from all parts of the world. Cole said the opening of the room completed the University's effort to recognize Dennis' achievements.
