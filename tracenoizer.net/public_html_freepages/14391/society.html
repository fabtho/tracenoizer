The Crystal Lake Historical Society Mission Statement The purpose of the Crystal Lake Historical Society is to identify, preserve, present and promote through education the history of Crystal Lake, Illinois. The Society invites all who share this concern to discover and learn about our exciting heritage and plan for our future. Any specific property presented, such as historical pictures or research materials, should be considered the property of the Crystal Lake Historical Society.
