Many of you may have heard of me before, but for those who haven't I'd like to take this opportunity to introduce myself and talk about some of the goals I have for the CFA this year. I first got involved in the "Atheist Movement" two years ago when I started a high school Atheist Club in Michigan. Forming the club was no easy task and I ended up enlisting the support of Americans United for Separation of Church and State as my lawyers. First, it taught me that running a club is extremely hard, both because of the lack of funds and simply because it takes some great ideas and materials to sustain the club's membership. The second thing I learned is that there is a whole community of individuals who are willing to help.
