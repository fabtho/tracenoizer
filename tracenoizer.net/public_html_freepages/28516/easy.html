Editorial Reviews Book Description International Marketing Strategy sets out the context, techniques and strategies involved in successful international marketing. Through its treatment of global case studies from Europe, Australia, the Pacific Rim and the USA, it breaks across national boundaries presenting a truly international vision of marketing strategy.
