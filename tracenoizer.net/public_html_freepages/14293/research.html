To determine the tectonic stress orientation, different types of stress indicators are used in the World Stress Map. The global compilation was a task of the International Lithosphere Program (ILP), headed by Mary Lou Zoback (Zoback and Burke, 1993), and new data are continually by independent researchers. Today, the World Stress Map is maintained and extended at the Geophysical Institute of Karlsruhe University as research project "Weltkarte der tektonischen Spannungen" of the Heidelberger Akademie der Wissenschaften (Heidelberg Academy of Sciences and Humanities). Map was initially Zoback and Zoback 1980, now continually updated. LOCALITY--place name or well name COUNTRY--Usually a country name, except for U.

Social Scientists from any country who speak either English or French may apply to the summerschool. All applicants should have completed at least their first degree in the Social Sciences or Humanities. Applicants from Western Europe must have had experience in social anthropological field research. Applications can be made with an informal letter stating the reasons for the interest in the summerschool and including a Curriculum Vitae.
