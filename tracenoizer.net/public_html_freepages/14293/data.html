html Description Abstract The coverage contains information on contemporary tectonic stress in the crust. To determine the tectonic stress orientation, different types of stress indicators are used in the World Stress Map. The global compilation was a task of the International Lithosphere Program (ILP), headed by Mary Lou Zoback (Zoback and Burke, 1993), and new data are continually by independent researchers. Today, the World Stress Map is maintained and extended at the Geophysical Institute of Karlsruhe University as research project "Weltkarte der tektonischen Spannungen" of the Heidelberger Akademie der Wissenschaften (Heidelberg Academy of Sciences and Humanities). Map was initially Zoback and Zoback 1980, now continually updated.
