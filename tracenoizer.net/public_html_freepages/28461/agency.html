Community Services for the Blind and Partially Sighted (CSBPS) works with individuals, families, and the community to restore, maintain and enhance the independence and well-being of people with impaired vision. Founded in 1965, CSBPS has developed into the Northwest's leading vision rehabilitation agency and specializes in working with people with age-related vision loss. The "Medicare Vision Rehabilitation Act of 2001" (H. 2484) was introduced to expand Medicare coverage to include vision rehabilitation services to promote the independence and well being of Medicare beneficiaries who are blind or partially sighted.
