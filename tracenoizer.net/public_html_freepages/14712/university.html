Robert Nicholls of Middlesex University has begun his appointment as SEDAC Visiting Scientist on February 26. Nicholls' research focuses on coastal implications of climate change, large-scale coastal behavior and modelling, human response to coastal hazards, and geomorphology and coastal zone management. He has been active in the Intergovernmental Panel on Climate Change and has led a major international research project on Synthesis and Upscaling of Sea-Level Rise Vulnerability Assessment Studies (SURVAS).
