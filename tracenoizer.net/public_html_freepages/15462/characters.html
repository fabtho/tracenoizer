The wooden dolls are made by hand from wood and rope and are only 3" tall yet have nice detail. They are part of a new retro Disney toys collection celebrating the evolution of these American icons. They are based on the original renditions of these Disney characters from the 1930's. The price includes one character but see below for a special price to purchase all four. .
Most of the one-line descriptions of the characters are from Fredrik Ekman, back from when he edited the list of Disney characters with translations into lots of languages. . Disney Comics Characters Disney Comics Characters The English names of the characters are used here. From the character pages you can find the translations into other languages, from a database of translations. Hopefully this will make it easier to find the character you are looking for (even if you don't know the name), but be aware that these "universes" often mix with each other, so you might find Grandma Duck in a Mouse story or Tinker Bell in a Duck story.
