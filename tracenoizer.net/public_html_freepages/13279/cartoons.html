. Filmography Tex Avery Filmography This filmography is by no means definitive. Many of Avery's cartoons went uncredited, some were finished after he left a studio and many were re-titled or re-edited. In fact Avery himself was unsure of exact titles and even the existence of some cartoons, but it's hardly surprising when you think that he made over 200 films in under two decades.
