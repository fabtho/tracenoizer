Zeman, Inner Models and Large Cardinals Zeman, Martin Inner Models and Large Cardinals 2002. The monograph contains a detailed presentation of general fine structure theory as well as a modern approach to the construction of small core models, namely those models containing at most one strong cardinal, together with some of their applications. The final part of the book is devoted to a new approach encompassing large inner models which admit many Woodin cardinals.
