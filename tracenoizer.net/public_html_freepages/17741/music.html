. Welcome to Hicksville American singer-songwriter Dan Hicks has been creating his own musical legend since the early 1960s. Dan's unique way with rhythm and words has given us music which defies convention, yet seems oddly familiar to even first-time listeners. His lyrics range from wry and downright funny, to deeply moving and introspective. He is also known for his delightfully edgy stage patter.
