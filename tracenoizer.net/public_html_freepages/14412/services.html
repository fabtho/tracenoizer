Social Events Course information Postgrad issues Support services Useful links Antitheses Welcome to the website of the Postgraduate and Research Students' Association of the Australian National University. PARSA concerns itself with the welfare of postgraduates at the ANU, particularly with respect to their studies. All postgraduate students at the ANU are members of PARSA, so please browse the site and see what PARSA has to offer.
Graduate and undergraduate students are encouraged to seek funding assistance from their home departments and Graduate Studies. Students may also use the World Wide Web to locate funding and research opportunities. Here, students will find links to the Community of Science, advising services, and research opportunities geared specifically towards them. Students may also visit Shields Library and access the UC Davis library Web site and online catalogue. Searches for "Research Grants--directories" and "Endowments--Directories" can prove fruitful.
