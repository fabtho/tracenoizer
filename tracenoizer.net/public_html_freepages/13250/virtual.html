Christian Moeller, born in 1959, is a leading European artist who creates interactive virtual architecture and virtual cities. The works of Moeller, which make use of electronic media, are based on the idea that people entering into the virtual world without wearing special gadgets or equipment, can change the whole environment by their reactions to the creation or discovery of new space.
