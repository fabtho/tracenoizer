In a country where most politicians pride themselves on their low profile, Fortuyn stood out with his snazzy designer suits, two yapping dogs called Kenneth and Carla, and a statuesque butler. Born in 1948 in a conservative Catholic family in a small town in the Dutch northwest, Fortuyn went to the hippie mecca of Amsterdam in the 1970s to study sociology.
