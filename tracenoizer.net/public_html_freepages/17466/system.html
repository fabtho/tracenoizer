.About the Curriculum The Information Technology Core Concepts Curriculum The technology that drives corporate information systems continues to change rapidly, particularly as Internet technologies impact on every aspect of business applications. The audience for the Managing Information Systems curriculum is MIS and business managers, as well as new application developers and system administrators who require foundation training on how the corporate IS function is structured, developed, and managed. This curriculum is linked to our new Project Management curriculum for those specifically interested in the discipline of managing both IT and non-IT projects. This curriculum takes students from a fundamental knowledge of PCs and networks through a thorough grounding in Internet and intranet technologies, internetworking, and operating systems.
