Rationale For Use The ranking system provides an ecologist or resource management specialist with a tool to sort exotic plant species based on their present level of impact and their innate ability to become a pest. Based on conscientious consideration of all the factors in the system, a person with good taxonomic and ecological skills should be able to separate those species that are innocuous from those that are disruptive or have a high potential to become disruptive.
