. Charlie Edwards November, 2001 Hollywood, California - Colony Entertainment, a newly formed Los Angeles record label is pleased to announce the addition of Charlie Edwards to its' artist roster. Edwards' first release with Colony, Bougainvillia, is currently being tested in a number of domestic radio and retail markets. If you work in the industry, please contact Colony to request promotional copies of this release. Bougainvillia is published by Seven From The Sand Music (ASCAP) Following the 1999 release of Charlie Edwards, For A While, Bougainvillia is the artist's second collaboration with Los Angeles based producer, Tree Adams.

A two-time Class A British Saloon Car champion and Class A British GT champion, he then moved onto the BTCC before his career was curtailed by a serious crash at Thruxton in 1995. He has also competed in the World GT series and the famous Bathurst 1000. Charlie was born in Sydney, Australia and began his career in radio. In 1990 he moved to the UK to continue his broadcasting career and motorsports interests and became chief executive of the worldwide broadcasting division of the Daily Mail group. A huge bike fan, he owns a Ducati 888 Superbike and a Harley Davidson.
