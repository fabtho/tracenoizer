Faculty of Education Student Societies - ESS The Education Students' Society The Education Students' Society (ESS) at the University of Regina represents over 1200 education students. Our goals are to create a sense of community in a large faculty, represent all of our students' voices at the faculty and university level, provide social and recreational events, and aid in the professional development of our future teachers. Nominations are held in March of every year to nominate a new executive council.
