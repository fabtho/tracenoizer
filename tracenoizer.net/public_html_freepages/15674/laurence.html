Mick Norman biography Mick Norman 'Mick Norman' was the pseudonym used by Laurence James for his massively influential Hell's Angels books. As co-editor of New English Library with Mark Howell, he changed the reading habits of a generation with a list that ran from the wildly reactionary (Richard Allen and Mary Whitehouse) to the wild. New English Library adopted a production line approach to literature -- identify a niche, then commission a hack writer with a fake name.
