Dino Quintero was one of four players named first-team "All-WAC" Four Spartans earn first-team "All-WAC" honors For the first time since San Jose State joined the Western Athletic Conference, four Spartan baseball players earned first-team "All-WAC" honors. Senior second baseman Gabe Lopez was the only Spartan to repeat as a member of the first team. Senior catcher Adam Shorsher, junior center fielder Dino Quintero and junior designated hitter Hector Zamora also were selected to the first team. Four other Spartans were named to the second team "All-WAC" including Kevin Frandsen, Nate Corrick, Jahseam George and Matt Durkin.
