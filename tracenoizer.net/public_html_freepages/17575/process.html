Good pre-employment assessments are behaviorally based as opposed to the standard personality test or inventory which may only have face validity. Behavioral assessments provide for a more predictable outcome when used for screening applicants. Return to Top The Use of Pre-Employment Assessments Complements the Interview Process Job fit is very important and many companies use behavioral interview questions to try to match the candidate with the job. The assessment report is most effectively used as a part of the employment screening and selection process. Most of the articles that you have probably read pertain to the use of psychological testing.
