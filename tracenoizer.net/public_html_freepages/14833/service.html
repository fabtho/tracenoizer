We have around 2,000 members, whose interests cover the whole of Great Britain and beyond. These include Census Indexes, Parish Register and Monumental Inscription transcripts. courier service for certificates to the Family Records Centre and for wills to First Avenue House; searches of our indexes to north west Kent inhabitants; a surname enquiries service; members' interests; Overseas and Out-of-Area Co-ordinators etc. Some of these services are available to non-members too. The content is continually changing, so please return from time to time.

further information: http://www.ganet.state.ga.us/services/ocode/ocgsearch.htm
