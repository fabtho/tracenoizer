We seek to equip our clients and families with skills that enhance their autonomy and self-care. . Our services are provided as an alternative to state-run institutions, clinics and hospital systems. Our service delivery model emphasizes the creation of respectful, supportive and healing environments we call "normative communities". These communities support positive culture and empower clients to engage in mutual help, personal growth and accountability.
