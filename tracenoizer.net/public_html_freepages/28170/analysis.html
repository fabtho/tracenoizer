In order to profile active proteases, this project will make use of the specificity of chemically synthesized and natural protease inhibitors. Inhibitors will either be immobilized to beads or biotinylated followed by incubation with various purified proteases. Analysis of the bound proteins will be performed by peptide mapping and mass spectrometry. The initial part of this work will focus on establishing conditions under which specific proteases or protease families can be enriched and quantitatively bound. Subsequent work will be related to enriching certain proteases from biofluids following spiking with purified recombinant enzymes and finally from the original biofluids as obtained from clinical samples.
