Harriet Judnick, an administrative assistant in San Francisco, is currently suing the largest company, Doubleclick. Judnick sued Doubleclick less than a year after getting her first computer and then being deluged with ads for products related to her Internet browsing habits. (Roba 2000) After some research she determined Doubleclick had been sending cookies to her computer and sharing that data with other Doubleclick clients. Doubleclick was going to merge its data base with that of Abacus Direct linking personal data to individual buying habits. This would create very specific and personal profiles on millions of people.
