Commonwealth of Kentucky Web Server - CINS The Commonwealth Integrated Network System (CINS) The Commonwealth of Kentucky is striving to achieve vastly improved access to and sharing of information and technology resources which exist throughout the state. Communications technology will be a major enabler in achieving these objectives. This project continues the development of a digital inner-city network initially authorized by the 1990 General Assembly. This intergrated backbone network is planned to carry voice, video and data via the linkage of hub sites throughout the state. The CINS is the major component of the Commonwealth's communications infrasture.
