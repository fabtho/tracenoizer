The success of digital technology is largely due to the capabilities of efficient transmission, storage, and perfect copying. However, especially the last feature leads to severe problems because unauthorized copying is also simplified. Digital Collections Caltech Library System Caltech Library System Digital Collections All CLS Digital Collections implement the Open Archive Initiative (OAI) protocol.
