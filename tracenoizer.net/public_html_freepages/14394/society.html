The Walkington Society was founded in 1988, by Roderic Walkington, with the purpose of bringing together the relatively small number of people who possess the Walkington family name or who have a Walkington family connection. Roderic located the 150 Walkington households who had their names entered in the UK telephone directories and wrote to them all asking for expressions of interest. There was an enthusiastic response to that initial letter and the fairly informal Walkington Society was formed.
