Feedback Member of The Federation of Family History Societies. The Beresford Family Society is dedicated to uniting all descendants of the Beresford family throughout the world whatever the variant of the name. Since the society was founded in September 1980, our research team have been hard at work seeking and recording details of all types of events relating to the name of Beresford . We hold databases of family history information, of all kinds and at the same time we invite you to submit any information you may have relating to present or past Beresford history which may help to piece together the many parts of the jigsaw still unsolved. We hope you will find something of interest whenever you visit.
Conduct workshops and seminars, and to discuss topics of interest to members. Sponsor projects to further the availability of genealogical knowledge, e. Organize field trips to various research centres, such as archives and the National Library. So our resource material tries to satisfy some of all our members needs. .
