Library The HUT library provides library and information services. It specializes in technology and related natural sciences, and is both a university library and a scientific central library. As the technological central library of Finland, its purpose is to support and promote technological and scientific research, teaching and study and to satisfy the library and information service needs of society and thus of trade and industry in technology and related natural sciences. The basic function of the library is to select, acquire, organize and store information of various kinds and ensure that it is available. It is mainly concerned with maintaining the collection, providing library, interlibrary loan and information services and arranging for training.
