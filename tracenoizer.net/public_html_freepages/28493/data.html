Distributed software engineering (DSE) efforts offer difficult challenges to those who need to monitor and manage the overall process. Without the capability to know what is happening in the process, the risk of failing to produce a quality product on schedule increases greatly. With Internet-based DSE, the opportunity exists to capture data from the process at relatively low cost, since so much of the process is already being supported by technology which can be instrumented. Our position is that capturing event traces from these processes can be done fairly easily, and will enable the use of analysis and management tools that provide feedback to the enactors and managers, thus improving the overall process and enabling the efficient production of high-quality software products.
