Future of Music Coalition FMC Articles Below is a list of articles, interviews, and opinion pieces that members of the Future of Music Coalition have written over the past couple of years. Most of them were originally posted at Insound on The Machine, a project that Jenny and Kristin worked on in conjunction with Insound to explore the new issues facing musicians in the digital realm. We encourage you to check these out, as well as the other articles posted on The Machine.
I marveled over the simplicity and magic of light blinking on a screen, perhaps best represented in movies like Wargames(1983). I was similarly attracted to structuralist films and interactive computer animation. The hopes and fears of the future of a nation were being provoked by a symphony of blinking and strobing lights and frame rates. A tawdry effect that dazzles and invokes fear among the masses. I made Info-receptor at a time when aspects of my life began to shift from analog to digital.
