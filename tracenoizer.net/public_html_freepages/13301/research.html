Clean Technology Research Group Home Page The Clean Technology Research Group IntroductionOur Group covers a wide range of research activities, largely aimed at understanding fundamental chemistry or at developing environmentally acceptable processes and materials. The core themes of our work are supercritical fluids, infra-red spectroscopy and lasers. The group comprises several members of staff, postgraduate students, postdoctoral reasearch fellows and overseas visitors.
