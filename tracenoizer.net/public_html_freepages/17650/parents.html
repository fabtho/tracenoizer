In its seventh year, the Pinnacle Awards are bestowed jointly by The Record and the San Joaquin County Office of Education on students to recognize their academic excellence, community service and citizenship. A reception for the recipients was held in April at University of the Pacific's Raymond Great Hall. More than 200 students, parents, teachers and school officials attended the event, sponsored by The Record and UOP. "It is our honor to recognize these fine students for their leadership and academic achievements," Record Publisher and President Roger Coover said of this year's Pinnacle Award winners. The Pinnacle Team represents the top 1 percent of the senior class at each high school in the two counties.
