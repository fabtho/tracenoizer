There are great songs lurking under performances dulled, perhaps by too many takes, perhaps by the airless space the recording embodies. The producers of this record have attempted to appropriate the reputation (justified, if not well-enough known) of Thompson's solo concerts by pairing a disk of 'conventional' rockinroll-type stuff with a disk of 'acoustic' recordings. A couple songs on the acoustic side are fine, but overall it has a better feel as background music. If he does a score for a Disney cartoon, say, an all-broadway-tune version of "On The Road", then we go after him with pitchforks.
