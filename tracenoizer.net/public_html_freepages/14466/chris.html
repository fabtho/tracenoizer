Biophysical Instrumentation Home Page Biophysical Instrumentation Facilities at the Centre for Protein Engineering Biophysical studies play a central role in the research effort of the Centre for Protein Engineering. The research group headed by the director of the centre, Professor Alan Fersht, has a comprehensive range of instrumentation and these facilities are managed by a permanent member of staff, Dr Chris Johnson. The Centre for Protein Engineering has a range of equipment for performing calorimetric measurements on biological systems. Through our close links with Microcal Inc we have been involved in beta testing their latest 'VP' range of calorimeters.
