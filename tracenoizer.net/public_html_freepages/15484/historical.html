I found the book both educational and entertaining. Whether you are interested in the history of Dracula, or are just looking for a good book, I cannot recommend this one highly enough. This is an impressive piece of historical fiction - well researched and well told. While Vlad Dracula is certainly a famous, or infamous, historical figure, not a lot has been written about his world. Augustyn does a very good job of introducing the average history buff, such as myself, to the region and time.
