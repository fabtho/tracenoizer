Some of the material written this year for the new AS album will still undergo significant development, other songs have been recorded to final album standards.

We made an industry demo CD from those sessions and sent it to England, and Kerrang. magazine, who used to be one of the most consistent supporters of Celtic Frost, immediately suggested to forward the demo to Sanctuary head Rod Smallwood. Sanctuary were very much looking for a modern act to sign, and it just totally clicked on both sides.
