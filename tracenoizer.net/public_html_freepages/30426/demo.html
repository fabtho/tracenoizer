. Apollyon's Sun nahmen die Sepultura-Version des Celtic Frost-Lieds "Procreation (Of The Wicked)" in ihr Bandrepertoire auf. Nach 14 Monaten Aufnahmesessions wurde im September 1997 ein erstes Demo fertiggestellt und Ende desselben Jahres ein Plattenvertrag mit der Sanctuary Music Group (London) unterzeichnet. UK-London, The Drome, "Gig Week" throughout the remainder of the year are currently being discussed. Tom Gabriel Fischer will also participate in the BBC Radio 1 anniversary session as a guest of the Foo Fighters.
" Given the music community's reverence for what Celtic Frost achieved, success was and is expected. Press reaction was immediate, in fact, largely responsible for turning Apollyon Sun into the esteemed Sanctuary Management's first modern rock act. "We were working on the Apollyon Sun demo for some 14 months," recounts Fischer. "It was a very long time for a demo, much longer than most bands work on their album (laughs). But we wanted to be certain that we presented ourselves the right way.
