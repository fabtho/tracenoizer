Not only would the cost of games be slashed, but the CD-ROM drive would enable full orchestral musical scores for their games. Games would often take up to a minute to load initially, and a pause of 20-30 seconds between rounds or levels was common. The CD audio was wonderful, but the long loading times were a far cry from the instant action of the arcade. The Neo Geo CD also received a very limited distribution, and never ended up reaching most of its target audience. In 1998, almost a decade after its introduction, the Neo Geo MVS is still sucking quarters from peoples' pockets.
