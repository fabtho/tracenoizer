LBC Rides from the Past LBC Rides from the Past - May 1998 PENRYN PANCAKE PROMENADE, May 2 Perhaps some riders like pancakes; however, not getting wet was their choice. Since I was closest, I drove to E-Town to see if anyone showed up. While we were standing in the lot, a patch of blue sky appeared and we decided to ride about 5 miles into the ride.
We agreed we were all better people for the experience. .
