. Lovelace Discussion Group Lovelace Discussion Group The Lovelace Group discusses the genealogy and history of the Lovelace (Loveless, etc) family. If you have research interest in this name, or its variations you might join this list. This is a very active discussion group with 5-10 posts per day. This group has a tendency to get off the subject of genealogy more than I like.
