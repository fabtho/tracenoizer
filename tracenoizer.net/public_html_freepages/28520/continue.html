The final part of CodeIt is where students can come up with their own description of a program and completely design and code it. Once they get their program to work, they can send the program, design, and description to the library review panel and try to get it accepted into the library. Revisions may have to be made but the panel will continue to work with the student until the program can be accepted. Plenty of support will be available to them to learn the concepts so they do not get overwhelmed in a new environment and domain. Adjustable scaffolding will allow them to customize this support to their own level and will fade as their skills grow.
