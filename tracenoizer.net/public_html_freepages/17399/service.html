Written appraisals, even for one item, can take hours to prepare. Written appraisals are most often done by professionals who belong to an Appraising Society or Organization. The cost of this service varies depending on time spent and the expertise of the appraiser. A verbal appraisal is an informal appraisal given verbally, not in writing. Usually no time is spent researching auction records and price guides.
