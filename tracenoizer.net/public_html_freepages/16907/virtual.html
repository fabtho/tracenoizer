Based on "TwisterGame on Network", this thesis examines the work, "Three men three legs" , which three users can collaborate and play through the computer network. Three players use each three networked computers (Silicongraphics, Indigo2). Each players see only one puppet which has a sphere body and three legs, in this virtual space. One player manipulate only one leg and this puppet keep own balance automatic. But if only one player won't go to the forward, the puppet don't go forward.
In the project Impressing Velocity, I brought GPS with Laptop Computer for climbing up Mt. and visualizing the impression of the hardness of climbing. There users are sharing same virtual environment and when they are in a same room, they can talk each other and see their face which is mapped onto his avator. This is an example and testbed for showing the possiblity of the future digital networking. 1982-1984 Member of Computer Graphics division, SEDIC Inc.
