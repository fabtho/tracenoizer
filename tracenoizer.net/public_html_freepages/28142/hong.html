At the age of 19, he took up ballet again with Cheong Thing Kin and later Wong Fook Choon as well. He joined the Selargor Kwaigsi Association Dance Group in the same year. He joined The Hong Kong Academy for Performing Arts as a scholarship from 1994 to 1997, majoring in modern dance. He graduated as a Bachelor of Fine Arts (Dance) with Honours in 1997, and joined the CCDC since October of the same year. Boy Story (the 30-minutes version of le beau) is the most special one.
