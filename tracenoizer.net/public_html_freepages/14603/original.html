HOME NOW ON DISPLAY IN FILM FORUM'S LOBBY Matthew Barney has created for Film Forum a 250 piece, limited-edition work of art CREMASTER 5, now on display in Film Forum's lobby. ) It depicts a double image of Ursula Andress, who stars in his film of the same name. The processes used to create the piece include offset lithography and silk screening from an original photograph by Michael James O'Brien. The piece is 33" by 23" (vertical), produced on 300-gram satin Somerset. Printing is courtesy of Jean-Yves Noblet of Noblet Serigraphie.
