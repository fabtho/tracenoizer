. A Trash Day Spilt Oil Exhibition Photo Installations on the Beach In January 1997, the Russian Tanker has wrecked and almost 6000 tons of the crude oil has spilt in Japan sea. This photo installation was intended to represent the oil-spilt beach. We first took the photograph on the beach, then printed out on 40 sheets of cloth. The exhibition has toured around and exhibited at 14 locations in Japan.
