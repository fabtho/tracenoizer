This production's theme is virtual books which take the shape of installations. On the table, the book is reflected as an image of the computer; however, when the pen in one's hand touches the image, the pages will be turned and new pages appear one after the other. The functions of the books are moved to the computer and they are produced with interactive format which could not be possible with ordinary books. ..
