The idea for the semi-circular battle mat was inspired by the excellent Zulu War board game published in Battleplan magazine, designed by Erich Faust. In the course of a campaign, battles often occur on the strategic map that are either very small, very remote, or just not very important. They don't merit a full tabletop treatment, but at the same time you might not want to arbitrarily choose a winner and loser.
