- spears -

As long as a diver eats all of what they kill, hunts legally, and does not try to clean out a reef for trophies, then there is little that can be logically said to ban spearfishing. However, if someone wants to hunt legally and ethically, knowledge of how to make a quick kill can reduce the suffering of the prey. Spears used for fishing are generally most effective when propelled by some kind of force underwater. Hawaiian slings, and propelled spears can be used effectively with a lot of practice but most novice fish hunters should consider using a gun. Treat all spears with respect and remember that the best safety device is the brain that commands the hand that uses the weapon.
