Nancy Pollard's Home Page Nancy Pollard Nancy Pollard is an Assistant Professor in the Computer Science Department at Brown University. She received her PhD in Electrical Engineering and Computer Science from the MIT Artificial Intelligence Laboratory in 1994, where she performed research on grasp planning for articulated robot hands. Before joining Brown, Nancy was a postdoctoral fellow in the Animation Lab at Georgia Tech, where her research focused on physically realistic animation of human motion.
