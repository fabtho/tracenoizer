Southampton Archaeology - Research - Avebury University of Southampton Department of Archaeology Back Home Index Help Press Release Megalithic Mystery Solved Archaeologists believe that they have this week located the remains of a 'lost' megalithic stone avenue leading from the Avebury stone circle. The excavation, only a mile from the famous monument, has revealed the remains of six stone settings. These form an avenue that the excavators believe to be part of the 'Beckhampton Avenue' which was last recorded in the 1720s by the antiquarian William Stukeley, and whose existence has been disputed by archaeologists ever since.
