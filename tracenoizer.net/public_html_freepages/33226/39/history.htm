California Women's Retreat 2003









 

 

 

California Women's Retreat was founded and organized in 1980 by Oralyn Denison. The first Retreat was held January 30, 31, and February 1, 1981, at the Sheraton Inn, Concord, California. The weekend retreat became an annual event.

Oralyn Denison and her husband, Joe, died in a tragic private airplane accident January 30, 1984, just three weeks prior to the 1984 Retreat. The committee dedicated that Retreat to Oralyn Denison, Founder and Chairman of California Women's Retreat. The Oralyn Denison Memorial Scholarship Fund was established in 1986.

On October 31, 1985, California Women's Retreat was incorporated as a California Non-Profit Corporation, with California and Federal tax exempt status before the California Franchise Tax Board and the Internal Revenue Service.

It is now possible for California Women's Retreat to receive donations from individuals or businesses.  For further information, please contact C.W.R., P.O. Box 30144, Walnut Creek, CA 94598-9144.
