Mornington Peninsula Family History Society Welcome to MPFHS on the Web. An associated member society of the Genealogical Society of Victoria. About the Society The group was established in 1979 with the aim of helping people to research their family history without having to travel into Melbourne too often for research. In the years since, the group has grown and has now over 300 members. .
