UCSD Modern Chinese History Site Welcome to the Chinese History Research Site at UCSD The reviews and essays section provides book reviews and critical essays on fifty classics in the historiography of modern China. The sources section is a guide to English and Chinese materials relating to historical research on modern China. The archives section provides information on Chinese local archives and also serves as a site for researchers to exchange information. The bibliography section contains a list of English-language works on Qing, Republican, and PRC history. The links section contains a list of China-related web sites and provides quick access to the on-line catalogues of East Asian libraries throughout the world.
