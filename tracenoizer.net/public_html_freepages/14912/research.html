He is on several editorial review boards of scholarly journals, including The Journal for Applied Behavioral Science, and The Journal of Small Business Management. Astrachan is also a member of the review board for The Family Business Professional (a Hartcourt Brace publication). He has been involved with such lobbying groups as The Committee to Preserve American Family Businesses, Family Businesses of America, and the Center for the Study of Taxation; is a member of the Academy of Management and Southern Management Association; a founding member of the Yale Program for the Study of Family Firms a member of Arthur Andersen's family business research project; and director of Kennesaw State University's Estate Tax Research Program. Astrachan's research presentations, consultations and publications concentrate on family businesses, succession, and the management of professional partnerships. Astrachan is also editor of the Family Business Bibliography.
