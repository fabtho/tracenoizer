THE SOCIETY The Cornwall Family History Society (CFHS) was formed in 1976 to assist members in tracing their family history. The Society, a registered UK charity, is administered by an Executive Committee elected at the Annual General Meeting, usually held in November each year. Members are invited to attend and take part in the AGM.
