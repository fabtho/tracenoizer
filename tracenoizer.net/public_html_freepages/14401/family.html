History of the Barney Family The Barney Family Historical Association is a non-profit family history society dedicated to preserving the heritage and history of the Barney Family throughout the world. Most researchers agree that the name BARNEY came from the small town of Berney (pronounced "Barney," and usually spelled Barney today) in Norfolk Co. Many have said the name is Norman, possibly derived from the norse BJARNE, meaning "Bear".
