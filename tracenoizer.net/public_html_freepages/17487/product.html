Occasionally, the term "microbe" or "microbial" is used instead of the term "microorganism. " Microbial hazard means occurrence of a microorganism that has the potential to cause illness or injury. Municipal biosolids (biosolids) are the by-product of human waste treatment by local government that may be used as fertilizer or as a soil amendment. Pathogen means a microorganism capable of causing disease or injury. Sanitize means to treat clean produce by a process that is effective in destroying or substantially reducing the numbers of microorganisms of public health concern, as well as other undesirable microorganisms, without adversely affecting the quality of the product or its safety for the consumer.
