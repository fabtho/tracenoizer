Judge Johnson is an active member of his county and community. . Johnson is serving his tenth term as Washington County Judge for the State of Arkansas. He is responsible for a system of county roads, county buildings and property, staffing of various county departments and chairs Quorum Court meetings. He grew up in Washington County, attended Fayetteville Public Schools and graduated from the University of Arkansas in 1965 with a degree in Economics.

He is a monthly book reviewer for the Los Angeles Times, fiction editor of the Seattle Review, and former director of the Creative Writing Program at Washington. Johnson is co-director of Twin Tigers, a martial arts studio. .

After writing several screenplays, including Booker (1984), which appeared on PBS, Johnson became a teacher of creative writing at the University of Washington.
