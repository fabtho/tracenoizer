Spear Tips There are basically three kinds of tips, barbed straight tips, paralyzer tips, trident spear tips. These can be modified to be a breakaway tip so that once the tip penetrates the fish, the tip separates from the shaft thereby reducing the risk that the fish will snap the spear while thrashing around. Barbed tips can only insure a quick kill if it strikes the fishes brain or spinal cord. Should the spear penetrate the body with this kind of tip, the fish will be caught by the barb but it will thrash around until it dies.
