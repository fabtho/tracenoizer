The SME Club Specifically set up to support small and medium-sized enterprises (SMEs), the SME Club is open to all companies irrespective of market sector. It provides a unique opportunity for smaller, technology-based companies to benefit from the technical and professional support provided by the Institute of Physics and to network with each other on an informal basis. Events A listing of the Institute's events for the business and industry community, plus an archive of past events. Many of the events are arranged especially for our Business Partners and SME Club members. Foresight The Institute of Physics Associate Foresight Programme aims to put physics at the forefront of this important national activity, and to ensure that Institute members are both informed and involved.
