In Be my Baby she mixes images from, for example, The Sound of Music and the horror classic The Texas Chainshaw Massacre with brief scenes from porno films. Cantor adapts these fragments from feature films, creating her own narrative style with which to examine such universal themes as love, beauty and hope. 'My perversion is the belief in true love' reads the title of her recently published book in which she describes her work; it will be on sale in the Museum during the film festival. -- These two American artists are each related -- in a particular way -- to the world of Hollywood. Barney imitates the smooth perfectionist picture production while Cantor uses images from existing films.
