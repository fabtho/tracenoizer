It is a must have for any serious Barks fan and for all of you who want to become one. This comic book is about Uncle Scrooge's past and his one and only girl friend. Naturally this splendid story is also about gold and Scrooge's love to money. It features all American titles (with an image for each comic story), dates of production and release, complete story index in both English and German. Although this book was made for the German market it still has a lot of useful information for the Barks Comic collector.
