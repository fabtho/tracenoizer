He is a member of the EPSRC Structural Materials College. .uk John Nicholls' research group is the leading research activity in high temperature surface engineering in the UK and has an international reputation for excellence in research into novel high temperature corrosion protection coatings, thermal barrier coatings and coating systems to resist erosive wear. He is joint owner of three patents, has published over 150 papers and has jointly edited two books, one on the prediction of high temperature materials performance, the second on high temperature surface engineering. He is a chartered engineer, a member of the Institute of Corrosion, chairman of the Surface Engineering Committee, the Institute of Materials and a member of the High Temperature Materials Performance Committee the Institute of Materials.
