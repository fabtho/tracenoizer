The journey from New York to Pomeroy took a day longer than our entire ocean voyage of 21 days. A few of us got out in Cleveland and traveled on in various directions. Some of these men have already died, while others are still alive. A few of them went to the country and became farmers. The cobbler Adam Reitz, the mason Johann Lentz, Jacob Schwarz and I remained in Pomeroy.
