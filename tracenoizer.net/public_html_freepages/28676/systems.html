Chargeback Systems Resource accounting, capacity planning, cost allocation, and chargeback systems are critical to the efficient management of any data center. Chargeback systems are designed to accurately and equitably allocate the cost of computing facilities to the various users of the service. Properly implemented, these systems provide the basis to chargeback the cost of existing IBM mainframe, UNIX open systems, and LAN based computer resources.
