This discipline deals mainly with properties of the real numbers. Mathematically, this is the establishment of the neighborhood of a point. In other words, point-set topology involves the study of geometric figures on a very small level, usually in two dimensions. Brouwer constructed point-sets which his predecessors had thought could not be formed. The proofs written by Brouwer between 1910 and 1912 are also considered by historians to be the first rigorous proofs in algebraic or combinatorial topology.
