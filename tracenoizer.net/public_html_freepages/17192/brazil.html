Williams (known to some as "Vinny") was born January 8, 1974 to Neal Williams and Ruth Jane Neibaur Williams. Anthony graduated from Snake River High School in 1992. After high school he attended Ricks College for a semester and then went on a mission for two years to the Brazil, Belo Horizonte mission, which later split into the Brazil, Rio De Janeiro North mission. After returning honorably from his mission, Anthony went to another year of college at Ricks where he played baseball. He went on to play baseball the next year at the College of Eastern Utah in Price, Utah where he earned his Associates Degree in June of 1997.
