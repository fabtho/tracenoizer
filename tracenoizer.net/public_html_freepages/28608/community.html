Once you have returned the registration form, we will send you a pack containing information on how to organise your activity, recruit volunteers and get local press coverage. Headlines about UVA is a daily compilation of articles appearing in the media about the University, higher education issues and trends. Inside UVA serves as an official record of University activities, features articles on news and policies affecting the U. community and highlights the work of faculty and staff. Inside UVA will run signed "Letters to the Editor" on subjects of general University interest as space allows. These sections are usually published once a month, when enough entries accumulate and as space allows.

The Equity Office at the University of British Columbia works to prevent discrimination and harassment on campus, to provide procedures for handling complaints and to coordinate UBC's employment and educational equity program. We help educate members of the UBC community about their rights and responsibilities. Workshops and training sessions for students, staff and faculty on issues such as discrimination and harassment, equity and diversity are offered regularly and are available on request. If you have experienced discrimination and harassment on campus, have been accused of committing it or have an equity-related query, you can turn to our Equity Advisors for assistance.
