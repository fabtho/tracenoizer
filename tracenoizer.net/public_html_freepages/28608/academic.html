send a question What happens to my paper's copyright when it appears in Education-line. Education-line asks for your permission to display the work on the internet. If another party (such as an academic publisher, College or Learned Society) owns the copyright, you need to secure their permission for its appearance in Education-line. In either case, the copyright remains with the original owner. Further exploitation of the work by the copyright holder can therefore take place without reference back to Education-line.
