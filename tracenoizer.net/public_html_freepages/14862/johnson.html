DEPARTMENTRESEARCHACADEMICSPEOPLENEWSJohnson, David B. Computer Science and Mathematical Sciences (1982), M. Johnson's research interests are primarily in the area of wireless and mobile networking protocols. Prior to joining Rice's Department of Computer Science in Fall 2000, Johnson was an Associate Professor of Computer Science at Carnegie Mellon University, where he was a faculty member from 1992-2000. Johnson is leading the joint Monarch Project between Rice and Carnegie Mellon, developing adaptive networking protocols and protocol interfaces to allow truly seamless wireless and mobile host networking.
