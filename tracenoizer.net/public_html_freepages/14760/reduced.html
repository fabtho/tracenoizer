Setting 23 hospital-based diabetes clinics in the United Kingdom. Patients allocated to intensive treatment had lower median hemoglobin A1c levels (7. 001), reduced diabetes-related clinical end points (40. 001) than those allocated to conventional treatment. No difference existed for macrovascular events, diabetes-related deaths, or all-cause mortality.
