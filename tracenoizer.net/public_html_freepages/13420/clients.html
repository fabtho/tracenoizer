Product Features In today's fast-paced global marketplace, the ability to provide a work force with access to the right applications and data-whenever and wherever they are needed-is indispensable for maintaining a competitive edge. Currently there are over 140 million PCs, workstations and servers installed on networks worldwide. Unfortunately, the administration of networked PCs and thin clients leaves a lot to be desired. Solving problems for end users is often a full-time job for IT staff members, and they are constantly working just to maintain consistent desktop configurations throughout the organization. ScriptLogic solves this problem by delivering the configuration created for each user wherever they log in.
