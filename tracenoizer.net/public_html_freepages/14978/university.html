The body plugs for Monsoon have now been belatedly machined and the project is back on track racing to make it to American Solar Challenge. .This is the homepage of the Arizona Solar Racing Team, a student run organization at the University of Arizona. The goal of our project is to promote alternative energy and engineering excellence through the design and construction of solar powered vehicles. We raced our most recent car, Monsoon, in the American Solar Challenge, finishing first in our class.

Valparaiso University Storm Intercept Team Home Operations Constitution Guidelines Web Log Executive Officers Current Officers Previous Officers Announcements News Releases General Meetings Fund Raising Activities Chase Documentary Chase Equipment Chase Gallery Chase Logs Cities of the Year Weather Resources Chaser Links Wx Resources Archived Data Minutes Weather Data The Valparaiso University Storm Intercept Team is devoted to observing severe convective weather as it happens and in the safest manner possible in order to enhance our undergraduate education in meteorology. At the same time, we will provide a valuable service to the residents of Northwest Indiana and other communities by using our knowledge of severe weather and our observing skills to report any severe weather occurrences to Skywarn and local authorities in hopes of saving the lives of those persons in the path of severe weather. For more information about us, please take a look at our policies page, which describes in detail how VUSIT operates.
