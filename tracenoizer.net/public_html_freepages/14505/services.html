He then asked how many had used one of the currently available subscription-based substitutes. Do you, perhaps, use Kazaa or BearShare or Morpheus to download music files for free. Those big file-sharing services put little pieces of spyware on your hard drive that keep track of your activities on the Internet and report them to advertisers, who then send you pop-up or pop-under ads tailored to you. The people who put free software on the Web, including many free games, have to make money somehow. So some of them allow spyware to piggyback on the free program.
