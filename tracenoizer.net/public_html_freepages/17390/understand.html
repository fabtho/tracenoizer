Customary law How law is created through private arrangements, rather than by the limitless coercive power of the state. Federalist Papers Articles by some of the founding fathers. Had this declaration been observed, France would have had a constitution like Americas early constitution, based on popular sovereignty limited by natural law and natural rights. Instead they wound up with a constitution based on the limitless and absolute majoritarian popular sovereignty of Rousseau, leading inevitably to the great terror.
