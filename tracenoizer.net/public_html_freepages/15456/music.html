This piece revolves around the various relationships between individual players and groups (or tribes) of players. There are layers of drama and theatre, with individual relationships developing simultaneously. These express themselves in the rhythmic, energetic music which is a reaction to the oppresive and harmonically static music, dominated by the groups. This piece is dedicated to some good friends, who supported me in all sorts of ways as I was writing it. He is currently a DPhil student with Martin Butler at Sussex University.

He is a Yamaha Corporation of America Artist-Clinician, also endorsed by the Avedis Zildjian Cymbal Company. He also serves as an Instructor and Marketing Manager for Percussion One, a Houston-based organization directed by Lamar Burkhalter. Pollard has instructed organizations which have presented concerts and clinics for the Percussive Arts Society, Music Educators National Conference, Texas Music Educators Association, Texas Bandmasters Association and Washington Music Educators Association. He instructs for Yamaha Corporation of America's Sounds Of Summer camps and is a member of the SFA-Yamaha Percussion Symposium faculty. Pollard's students have earned positions in Regional and All-State bands and orchestras in Texas and Washington.
