. CASSP is an acronym for the Child and Adolescent Service System Program. CASSP helps children and adolescents with emotional disturbances to gain access to needed services. These services are planned collaboratively with the child's or adolescent's family, the mental health system, the school and other agencies. CASSP also gives technical assistance to provider agencies on state-of-the-art mental health services for children and adolescents, supports best practice and assists in communication among all those who serve children in Pennsylvania.
Colorado's Southwest Regional Library Service System The Southwest Regional Library Service System (SWRLSS - pronounced "swirls") is one of seven legislatively-created regional library systems in Colorado. SWRLSS works for, with and through its member libraries. Its mission is to strengthen, support and equalize member library services by providing and enhancing leadership, providing services and access to resources, and encouraging cooperation among libraries in order to improve public access to information. Other libraries in those same counties may be associate members. Each full member agency is entitled to a representative on the Membership Council.
Law Library Management Incorporated The Professional Library by Law Library Management Incorporated Law Library Management, Incorporated was established in 1982 by Raymond M. Jassin, to fill the need for professional law library service to the small and medium size law firm or corporate legal department. Our goal is to provide these services at the highest quality level on a part-time basis. Our staff consists of librarians, assistant librarians, and library assistants to effectively handle every aspect of your law library needs. We can provide total library management, or you may select any of our individual library services.
