What we call "preservation" in the film archives is, in comparison to many other areas of museum science, a misnomer. The motion picture is one of the most ephemeral objects yet created in the cultural sphere, with a life span shorter than almost any previous medium. There is in fact no adequate system for the long-term retention of the motion picture.
