Writing projects will be a mix of directed, spontaneous, and student-inspired activities. . DISCUSSIONS In August 2001, Hugo House hosted a series of informal, facilitated discussions, in anticipation of the Maps event. August 9th Suzanne Sowinksa led a discussion about the principals of memoir writing addressed by Judith Barrington in her various teachings on writing memoir, and examined them in relation to Judith's own memoir. The discussion included Judith Barrington's recent memoir Lifesaving and her non-fiction book From Truth to Art.
