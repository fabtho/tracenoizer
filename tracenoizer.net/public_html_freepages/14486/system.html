The gene - the first to be definitively linked to language - switches others on and off, and so could lead the way through a genetic network of language learning and use. It's an unbelievably complex system, and we've got one tiny glimpseMichael Tomasello, Max Planck Institute for Evolutionary Anthropology, LeipzigFinding one gene is like finding one part of a car.
