Valgardson was born and raised in Gimli, Manitoba, a town settled by Icelandic immigrants in 1875. Because Bill believes that writers should write what they care about and what they know about, he often writes about Icelandic immigrants and their descendents. Bill has been a teacher for many years; first of high school English then of creative writing, most recently at the University of Victoria. Throughout his adult life Bill has also been writing.
