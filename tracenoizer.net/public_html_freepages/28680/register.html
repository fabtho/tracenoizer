6 Other Day Services 28 1 No Day Service 80 3 Annual Review 4 0. 1 Total 2,688 100 Autism The Brothers of Charity Services have become increasingly aware of the need for autism specific services. At present Health Boards maintain a manually held register but are in the process of developing a more formal register of autism to help identify the needs in this area and facilitate future service planning. In recent years there has been a significant increase in children presenting with autism. We feel that there is a need for increased collaboration between the Department of Education and Science, the Department of Health and Children, service providers and parents so as to devise and implement models of service provision and delivery in this area.
