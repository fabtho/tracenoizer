When Nate was eight years old, he knew that music was the path he was going to take in his life. Nate began listening to all genre's of music, picking up the "soul" of Blues and the hard "edge" of Rock-n-Roll music from his uncle and other relatives early on in his life, giving Nate a very diverse feel of music that would in no time would be flowing through his blood.
