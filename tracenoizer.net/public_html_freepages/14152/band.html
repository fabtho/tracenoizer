Pearce Soccer Band (located in Richardson, Texas) is an organization dedicated to the highest musical standards while striving to support the superb J. The Soccer Band attends all boys varsity soccer games, some girls varsity soccer games, and all varsity playoff games regardless of sex. The Soccer Band encourages both the players and the fans (while distracting the opposing team and, sometimes, the officials).
