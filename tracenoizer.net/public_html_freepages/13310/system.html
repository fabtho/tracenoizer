Technical Tools - Find tools to assist in modeling environmental conditions, assessing water quality and more. EPA Models - Models are used to increase the level of understanding about natural systems and the way in which they react to varying conditions. Biology Registry System (BioRS) - The Biology Registry System (BioRS) provides information on biological organisms and how they are represented in the Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) regulations and information systems. Chemical Registry System (CRS) - The Chemical Registry System (CRS) is the EPA's central system for chemical identification information and provides a common basis for identifying chemicals listed in EPA regulations and data systems, and chemicals of interest from other sources, such as publications. Envirofacts - A national information system that provides a single point of access to data extracted from seven major EPA databases.

I run PGP at home on my own PC, and unless someone breaks into my house or monitors my electromagnetic emissions, they probably can't see my plaintext files or secret keys. On a Unix system, any other user can read your environment information remotely by simply using the Unix ps command. Similar problems exist for MSDOS machines connected on a local area network.
