Endo The study of geomagnetism is one of the oldest of the geophysical sciences. Since before the publication of Gilbert's De Magnete in 1600, people have sought to solve the mysteries of the Earth's magnetic field. The National Geophysical Data Center maintains extensive archives of geomagnetic data to further the understanding of Earth magnetism. Answers to some commonly asked questions can be found at our FAQ site. NGDC participates in national and international projects involving the preparation of regional and global geomagnetic models and local and global indices of geomagnetic activity.
