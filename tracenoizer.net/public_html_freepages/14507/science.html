Mike Nicholls' Home Page Science Department Canterbury Christ Church University College Mike Nicholls' Home Page My name is Mike Nicholls. I teach on many of the courses in the Science Department which have a biology component, but especially those with an environmental theme. I have been here since 1986 and I have been teaching in the University sector since 1979.
Teaching My teaching in biochemistry has included courses in 'Bioenergetics' at the 4th. year undergraduate level and in 'Proteins and Enzymes'for biochemists at the 3rd. But I am now developing my broader interests in the social and historical aspects of science. Some time ago I examined William Walwyn's contributions to politics and medicine in the 17th. In the January-April and September-December terms at Brock University I shall be teaching a course in "Human Sociobiology".
