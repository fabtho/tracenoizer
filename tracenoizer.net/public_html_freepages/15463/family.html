McMurry University Family Business Center The Center for Family Business was formed in 1994 as a part of McMurry University's School of Business. The Center's mission is to provide a forum to help family business members better work and live together, perpetuate the family business, and have a smooth management succession from one generation to the next. The Center encourages family business owners to plan for business transfer to the next generation by offering quarterly seminars and monthly luncheons on topics such as estate planning, succession management, second-generation successes, and other applicable topics.
