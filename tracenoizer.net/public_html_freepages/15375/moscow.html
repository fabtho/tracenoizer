This guide is written with bigger cities of modern Russia in mind, the period of Communist rule or Tsar's rule before that were greatly different and require their own guides - and sometime I might write them. Climate Climate in Russia varies widely, and it has just about any climatic zone the planet has to offer. It is NOT always winter in Moscow like Hollywood makes it look. The other cities vary greatly - some of the southern areas of Russia sometimes don't even have snow at all. Consult an atlas for a climate map for your particular setting.
