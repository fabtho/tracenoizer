USA Hey anyone out there that went to CKIS during 84 or 85. During that time there was only 4 white people in the High School. Frank Allen was there also his sister who was a model at the that time named Gina Na Na ( I think). Also looking for anyone named Randy Brown or Brian Coe. How about a Japanese guy named Higa that hung out at the seawall with a great dane dog and a Woody that he drove.
Other than that, there was a small general store and the rest was houses. It was the type of town that was perpetually peaceful, nestled in a forest with only one road going through it, under a large mountain that protected them. This nameless town probably should have been named peace for that was how it was designed. The architecture was old, indicating to Sirus that it was probably around during the Kouma war. A small child ran by them rolling a hoop on the ground with a small group of others chasing him.
