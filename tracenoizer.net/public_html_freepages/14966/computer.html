The primary requirement for the GIS source data is that the locations for the variables are known. Any variable that can be located spatially can be fed into a GIS. Several computer data bases that can be directly entered into a GIS are being produced by Federal agencies and private firms. Different kinds of data in map form can be entered into a GIS. A GIS can also convert existing digital information, which may not yet be in map form, into forms it can recognize and use.
