Follow virtual routes over Interstates, highways and byways from Bennington to St. Johnsbury, Wilmington to Stowe, Killington to Montpelier (plus lots more) and see all the points of interest in between. Visitors and Vermonters alike find this unique mile-by-mile presentation of Vermont's fabled picture postcard scenery, covered bridges, fall foliage, history and folklore enjoyable and easy to use. Print the pages to explore with maps or as detailed driving instructions. Click a sign for introduction to that highway Williamstown Gulf, the Royalton Raid The "World's Fair and Jiggerville Central Electric Railway Many other Vermont highways are included on the site - above are only some major routes.
