Production of event data sets Using the KEDS and TABARI programs, news reports from the Reuters wire service, and the World Events Interaction Survey (WEIS) event coding scheme, we have produced several event data sets -- primarily for the Middle East -- for the period 1979 to the present. Shorter data sets (1989-1999) are available for the Balkans and Central Asia.
