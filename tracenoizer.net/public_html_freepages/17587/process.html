As a student in Waterloo's Chemical Engineering department, you'll start by building a foundation of core courses in fluid mechanics, thermodynamics, and equilibrium stage separations. In your upper years, you'll choose a specialization and carry out a research and design project. You'll use specialized process design and analysis software in your courses and work in well-equipped laboratories, including our new ChemCAT process simulation lab. Help with operations and research projects in technical department. Support process engineering with calculations, mass and heat balances; correlate plant operational data, perform statistical analysis.
