Between the usual name-calling between self-described liberals and conservatives were a few ideas that were intelligently argued, so I decided to do some research on the Summit. The basic purpose of the summit was to promote the idea of volunteering as an American tradition and to limit the role of government in solving the social ills of America. To that end, the organizers believed that if Americans themselves get together and help solve these problems, then all would be grand. As each of us has the right to Life, Liberty and the Pursuit of Happiness, each of us has an obligation to give something back to country and community -- a duty to take responsibility not just for ourselves and our families, but for one another.
