Caswell-Chen Research Research in the Caswell-Chen Laboratory The research conducted in my laboratory is focused on the population biology and ecology of the sugarbeet cyst nematode (Heterodera schachtii). We also do some research on the root-knot (Meloidogyne) nematodes. We are investigating nematode ecology within the applied context of environmentally-sound nematode management.

Professor Chen's research addresses thermal problems in micron and nanoscale materials and devices using analytical, experimental and numerical methods. The goals of this research are (1) to understand fundamental heat transfer processes in small scales and (2) to solve important heat transfer problems in electrical and optical engineering and materials science.
