She came to the United States in 1948 and worked 25 years at Ellis and St. In 1993, Helena was awarded the Military Decoration of the Battle Cross of the Polish Armed Forces in the West. She is the only female recipient of this award as a registered nurse, serving in France and England during World War II. Helena also received the Polish Defense of War Medal in September, 1939, the Silver Cross of Merit, Silver Treasury Medal and Haller's Swords. Helena's personal accomplishments also include her talented artwork and gardening, but her greatest attribute was her kindness and compassion.
