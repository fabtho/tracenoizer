North West Kent Family History Society Homepage North West Kent Family History Society Covering north-west Kent and the ancient Kentparishes now in south-east London Welcome To Our Web Site The North West Kent Family History Society was formed in 1978 with the object of encouraging and aiding the study of family history, genealogy and heraldry in South East London and North West Kent. Our geographical area is bounded to the north by the River Thames from Deptford to Northfleet, thence southwards to Tonbridge, westwards to the Surrey border and then northwards back to the Thames. We have around 2,000 members, whose interests cover the whole of Great Britain and beyond.
