Johann Nickolaus BUeTTNER and Johanna Barbara BUeTTNER Genealogy Return to Home Page Johann Nikolaus BUeTTNER and Johanna Barbara BUeTTNER Genealogy FIRST GENERATION 3. Johann Nikolaus BUeTTNER was born on 2 Jul 1828 in Lempertshausen, Germany. He was married to Johanna Barbara BUeTTNER on 4 Jan 1852 in Rodach, Germany.
The Heusser home was on the high Swiss Plateau, overlooking Lake Zuerich. Beyond it lay Alpine pastures and towering mountain peaks. In this idyllic setting Johanna spent the first 25 years of her life. In 1852 she married Bernhard Spyri, a young lawyer, and moved to Zuerich. When Spyri was 43 years old, she began to write stories about the people and scenes of her youth.
