Barrett's research interests are focused on the quality, impacts, and mitigation of urban stormwater runoff. He has also developed a groundwater model for the Barton Springs portion of the Edwards aquifer near Austin, Texas. The goal of this research was the development of a model that will allow the prediction of the impacts of urban runoff and various regulatory scenarios on the quality and quantity of water in the aquifer. ..
