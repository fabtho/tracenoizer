Automatic alarm systems notify our staff of any system failure on a 24 hour basis. A continuous streaming tape backup system ensures against catastrophic failure. This means that if your host server fails, the second 'mirror' server will automatically take over within seconds of the event and notify our staff accordingly. It also means that during routine operation, if your server suddenly experiences peak traffic loads, the second server will automatically take over delivery of your site. Should a power failure continue for an extended period of time, a generator automatically provides power for continuous operation.
