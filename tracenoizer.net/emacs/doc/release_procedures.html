RELEASE PROCEDURES is an internal reminder for developers. These are last steps
to check, before releasing EMacro.

1 Update the emacro-version variable in ~/emacs/emacro/e-macro.el and in
   ~/emacs/contrib/unixdist/emacro.list and ~/emacs/contrib/w32dist/emacro.iss
2 Clean e-*custom.el, e-*cache.el, e-postload.el, e-prefs.el, e-preload.el
3 Check manifest.html
4 add-change-log-entry()
5 Update release_notes.html
6 Check that all files can bytecompile
7 Check copyright dates annually
8 Test these conditions:
  A) XEmacs without its sumo tarball
  B) Test with and without EMacro libs and other libs, such as tiny-tools
  C) Also test with and without support programs including gnuserv, sql, etc.
  D) Test with no e-prefs.el nor e-*custom.el
