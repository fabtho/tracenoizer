. UofT Cinema Studies Students Union Homepage CINSSU The University of Toronto Cinema Studies Students Union (CINSSU) was founded in the fall of 1989. CINSSU was the Innis College Students Society's (ICSS) first academic union. Since that time, the union has been active in participating in the academic affairs of the U. Cinema Studies Programme by selecting film and journal acquisitions; taking part in programme evaluations; serving on the various Cinema Studies programme sub-committees; and selecting films for the ever popular Free Friday Films screening series, now in its fifth season and sponsored by the Students Administrtive Council (SAC).
