Along the way in life you may meet people that you think are sincere, trust and quickly give them your confidence without really knowing who they are, only to realize that maybe they aren't quite what you thought them to be. Could be that you just didn't take the time out to really understand each other from the beginning. However, if any damage was done and the smoke clears, forgive them, bless them and wish them the best without ever holding a grudge or having hard feelings. Grudges and hard feelings are for the insecure and petty. If the friendship was meant to be then it will exist.
