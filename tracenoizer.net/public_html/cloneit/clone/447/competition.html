. 24 (607) Poster Art Graphic Artists Strut Their Stuff The International Poster Biennial in Warsaw is the world's first and foremost serial event of its kind. The Warsaw competition, organized under the patronage of the Council of the Graphic Design Association (Icograda), has won recognition from artists and critics since its inception.
