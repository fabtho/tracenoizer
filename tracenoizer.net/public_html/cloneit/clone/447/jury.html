The number of award winners in a category is not limited and their works are published in the ADC Yearbook, the globally distributed, ultimate creative awards reference book. This year, at the 80th Annual Awards, more than 16,000 works from all over the world had been submitted. The two series of illustrations that received Merits from the ADC Jury were published in the course of 2000 in the Vienna daily newspaper Der Standard in co-operation with art director Brigitta Bernart-Skarek.
