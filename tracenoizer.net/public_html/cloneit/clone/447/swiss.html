The 90 centimes stamp both looks like and smells like chocolate thanks to the design and a special lacquer coating of millions of minute globules of artificial fragrance which burst under gentle pressure to give out the aroma of Swiss chocolate. Originally Swiss Post intended to make the adhesive gum taste like chocolate but that idea was abandoned for hygenic reasons because Swiss Post felt people may try and lick the stamp too often and remove the adhesive.
