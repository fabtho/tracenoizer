Think about what the target audience should learn from your program. Develop learner objectives based on wants and needs. Learning objectives are simply statements that identify what the learners will be able to do, think or feel after completing the educational experience. Write out these learning objectives to keep focused on what you need to do. Remember, these are not objectives for you as the teacher but rather for the learners.
