In this paper we discuss the tendencies in functionality and technology of software for text analysis and reflect on those areas where more development is needed. The basis for this discussion forms a comprehensive review of fifteen currently available software for text analysis. Here we only delineate our methodology and criteria for selecting which programs to review and concentrate on discussing the types of support the selected programs offer, the commonalities and differences of their functionality, point to some of their shortcomings and put forward suggestions for future development.
