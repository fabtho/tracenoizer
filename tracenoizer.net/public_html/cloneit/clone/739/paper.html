. Example of an Out-of-class Peer Response Example of an Out-of-class Peer Response First, a few comments . This paper was originally written for a very different assignment, the definitional argument in E306. Nonetheless, it should give you some idea of the sort of analysis expected of you. Please don't panic if some of the terms seem strange; remember that, for the content of your response, you should rely on the worksheet I have provided.
If you're having difficulty finding a thesis, write out the main idea of what you want to explore, then go ahead and write out your points and your support for those points. Then, discern how all of your points relate to each other. After using this worksheet, you should be able to sit down and write your paper. The only things you have not written on this form already are an intruduction, a conclusion, and a clear explanation of how your example(s) support(s) each of your key points. Make sure all the things you have written on this form are clear within your paper as you write it.
