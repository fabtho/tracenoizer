Acute hospital care and older people with dementia and acute confusion Older people and acute hospital care Although most older people are healthy and active for most of the time and not receiving health care, sometimes they do become ill and need to be admitted to hospital. Just like any other group of patients, older people may have acute health care needs for a variety of reasons. For example an infection, dehydration, suffering from the effects of being prescribed multiple medications or with a fractured hip.
