The funeral, although one of the saddest days of our lives, is one we will always remember as a day which was well organised and filled with music and smiles along with the tears. We all loved John so much and there are so many people whose lives have been enriched by knowing him, he had qualities which very few people ever possess. Our time in Berlin helped to begin to come to terms with what happened and we now know he was so well loved by you all, that he will always be around in our hearts and in the memories we share of him. He was laid to rest with more love and care than anyone we have ever known and for that we wish to thank you all. With love, Sheila Berlin, 27th January 1997 Following John's death, the parish received many tributes and letters of condolence.
