The publicizing of innovative ideas and concepts through these expositions played an increasingly important role that fundamentally influenced the development of city and landscape planning and design. Garden expositions also left behind lasting traces and monuments, as seen in parts of Zurichs lakeside promenade, or the Bruglinger Area in Basel.
