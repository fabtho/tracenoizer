Aberdeen University Cinema Club The Aberdeen University Cinema Club was started in 1992 by a group of students who were interested in films. We attempt to show a wide range of films, not always available at other cinemas, including popular, as well as foreign and art-house films. The Cinema Club is entirely self-funding and is run by student volunteers.
