Tibet Online is operated by the international Tibet Support Group community, providing information on the plight of Tibet and serving as a virtual community space for the movement. This movement is dedicated to bringing about substantive negotiations without preconditions between the Chinese and Tibetan Governments, so that they can find a solution which will bring an end to the suffering of the Tibetan people, in accordance with the people's right to self-determination. Tibet's ancient and fantastic civilization and ecosystem are faced with extinction due to 50 years of mismanagement and abuse under its colonial ruler, the People's Republic of China.
