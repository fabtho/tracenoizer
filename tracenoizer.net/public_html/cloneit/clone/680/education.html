These six programs fall into three types -- certificate, graduate, or pre-certification. Nurse-midwifery graduate education programs are post-secondary programs that provide all the essential components of the nurse-midwifery curriculum as defined by the ACNMCore Competencies for Basic Midwifery Practice, have the essential elements for program implementation as defined in either the Criteria for Preaccreditation or Accreditation documents, and are incorporated into a program of professional studies that grants an academic degree at a master's or doctoral level.

As the Clinton administration works to fulfill its promise to provide Internet access to every classroom in America, many teachers have never been trained in how to use the Internet in their teaching. There's a new academic mania ignited by the E-rate, the federal education rate discount provided for in a new telephone tax to wire every school and every library to the Internet. School boards cite the percentage of computers in their schools and cut industrial arts, art and music programs to make room in their budgets for more and newer technology.
