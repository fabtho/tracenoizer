Stopped by the tourist office for a city map and off to the old city two blocks away. The old city is like an island in the middle of St. Gallen dominated by the Kathedral and its buildings. It's a typical old city, with its pedestrian shopping area but it seems to lack a special character of its own. We wandered along the twisting and narrow streets from the Bahnhofstrasse down to The Limmat River drinking in the old and restored buildings housing designer shops, gourmet restaurants and food emporiums, fine hotels, upscale home furnishings shops and galleries.
