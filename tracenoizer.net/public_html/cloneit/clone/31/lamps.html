Among the special technical features of Stella spotlights is the option of locking all the movable parts into place - from the positioning of a sculpture lens to the fixing of angles of rotation and tilt. The specific settings of each spotlight can thus be preserved during maintenance work. The rotatable spotlight housing is open to the front, so changing lamps is a simple operation. A fixing device ensures that the lamp retains its focus. The spotlights are available in three sizes depending on performance.
