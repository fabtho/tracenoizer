. -- The azaleas that grow in such abundance here were robbed of some of their brilliance by the dark gray skies, and those 67 trees that line Magnolia Lane provided precious little shelter from the intermittent rain. Armed with multicolored umbrellas and ponchos, they waited through a two-hour suspension of play on Tuesday for their chance to walk the fairways at the revered Augusta National. There is a mystique about The Masters -- the only one of the four major championships to be played on the same course each year. Those emerald fairways and vexing greens provide a tidy frame of reference for a game that thrives on putting things in context.
