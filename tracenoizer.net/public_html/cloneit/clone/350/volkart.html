Paper magazines are interluding the age of the web, combining experimental theories with new forms in performance art and music. While reading the article it became another introduction in media-gender, full of information but lacking a personal stance and content. With 'Kunst als Hysterie' by Yvonne Volkart analysing Freud's studies I became interested again, Volkart combined historical reflection with interesting remarks on male hysterics. Surprised I was with the article on Kenneth Anger, because summer 1996 Joern Bakke, a Norwegian performer re-introduced Anger movies to me and I looked at them with complete boredom but was nevertheless triggered by the way of presenting, camp as a way of live. Springer is a good magazine, somewhat between Die Beute and Kunstforum.
