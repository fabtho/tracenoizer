Photography Europe Germany Landscape by Thilo Koenig From sublimation to denial Aspects of contemporary German landscape photography. Talking about artistic landscape photography today no longer means referring to representations that reach from the turn of the century picturialists' escape to the photodesigners' glossy colour photographs (). Above all because of the influences of the New American Topographics in the Seventies, the topic of landscape can no longer be separated from nature being invaded by civilization.
