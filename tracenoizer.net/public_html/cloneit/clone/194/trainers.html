The Team of direction chooses among her members one president, named for two (or tree) years, which represents the EFAPO in its corporate measures and coordinates the team of direction. Yves Brault is an administrator of the School, he also coordinates the teaching reflexion. Anne Fraisse coordinates the inter-institutional relations and the reflexion of the College of the trainers. For the patient there is no confusion about to which medical vision to adhere. Many allopathic trained doctors in developing countries hesitate to openly support herbal medicine, for fear of not being taken seriously. By offering courses in homeopathy and establishing a Trainer of Trainers programme, the cooperation between allopathy, homeopathy and herbal medicine becomes possible. We also have been asked to advice for the establishment of new trainings for traditional healers. The increase in pluriformity of treatment modalities offered in a clinic contributes to the effectiveness of the care and the emancipation of herbal medicine.
