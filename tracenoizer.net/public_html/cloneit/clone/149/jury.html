Der ADC hat ebenfalls ein Forschungsarchiv und seine Ehrenhalle, das Art Directors Hall of Fame. Preisausschreiben des Art Directors Clubs wurden mehr als 16. Die beiden Illustrationsserien, die die ADC Jury mit Anerkennungen auszeichnete, wurden im Laufe des vergangenen Jahres in der wiener Tageszeitung Der Standard unter der Leitung von Art Director Brigitta Bernart-Skarek veroeffentlicht. ..
The number of award winners in a category is not limited and their works are published in the ADC Yearbook, the globally distributed, ultimate creative awards reference book. This year, at the 80th Annual Awards, more than 16,000 works from all over the world had been submitted. The two series of illustrations that received Merits from the ADC Jury were published in the course of 2000 in the Vienna daily newspaper Der Standard in co-operation with art director Brigitta Bernart-Skarek. According to the Club, the jury had received 16,000 entries. Markovic's works will also be published in the 80th yearbook of the Art Directors Club.
Most exhibitions remain open until July 26, with the exception of Ralph Schraivogel's one man show and the exhibition of turn of the century posters, which closed on June 28. The prize winners The theme of this years poster competition was Photography, and about 5000 invitations to submit recent works were sent to poster designers in forty countries. 1350 posters were received and prescreened down to 84 by a french jury, and these are exhibited and shown in the catalogue. ..
An extraordinary point of this year was a special ICOGRADA prize award, presented by the vice-president of ICOGRADA, the graphic designer Sanja Rocco from Croatia. A collateral event to the competition was the international workshop for graphic design students under the name Poster Today - the Environment of Poster, organized by the POSTER association in cooperation with the Academy of Fine Arts in Bratislava. Competition Results In compliance with the competition rules, the steering board of the Triennial suggested 5 laureates of the Master's Eye Award to become members of the international jury. ..
