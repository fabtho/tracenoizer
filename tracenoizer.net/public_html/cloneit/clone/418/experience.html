the_living as a digital character embodies the dream of total connectivity; she is a veritable connectivity hero. Her live performances are rituals in blurring the borders between the physical and the on-line worlds. Living, communicating, creating and being part of a live-experience herself the_living attempts to 'be in the present' while simultaneously relaying her experience to her remote audience through streaming video and chat. the_living is hyper living, living more than once, at once.
