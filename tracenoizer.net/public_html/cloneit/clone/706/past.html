. Foundation Pupils Association The Foundation Pupils Association Inc was founded in 1994. About 400 past students and staff who were at Cashmere High School for the first two years, 1956 and 1957, are on the mailing list. They take great interest in the school and support its continuing success. Committee members initiated the very enjoyable 40 Year Celebrations held in October 1996.
