. eRENA Homepage An Artist Directed Mixed Reality Environment We report on task 7b. It is a three site distributed augmented reality art installation that articulates and explores fundamental issues related to the linking between real, surrogate and virtual spaces. We describe the concept of the installation within the frame of human-to-human-communication in mixed environments, the artistic intentions, and the future development from the interactive installation to the extended performance environment. This heterogeneous undertaking combines a range of mixed reality techniques based on communication between physical humans, an avatar, an autonomous agent, and a robot-puppet.
As a permanent installation, it is also consistent in the sense of leaving traces, aging, developing a patina. . The data helmet transports the visitor into an endlessly vast, virtual world of letters and texts that have been left behind by earlier visitors to this installation or have been entered by virtual exhibition visitors via Internet. The position and orientation of the visitors head is measured by a 3D sensor attached to the data helmet, so that the graphics workstation is able to calculate and display the correct image for each particular perspective. In this way, visitors construct a continuously growing architecture of curtain-like, semi-transparent surfaces, which become readable at a closer look.
