Dalai Lama meets Tibetans in Taiwan Dalai Lama meets Tibetans in Taiwan TAIPEI, April 1 (AFP) - Tibet's spiritual leader, the Dalai Lama, on Sunday met nearly 100 Tibetans including some illegal immigrants on the second day of his nine-day trip to the island. The Nobel Peace Prize laureate, clad in yellow and red robes, gave his blessings to the gathering and listened to the problems they faced while living in Taiwan. There are some 1,000 Tibetans in Taiwan, including 100 illegal immigrants.
