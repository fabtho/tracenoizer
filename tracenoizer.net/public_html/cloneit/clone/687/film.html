Berlin International Film Festival 2000 - 50th Berlinale Berlin International Film Festival . Many of the films in this year's Forum programme reflect the state of mankind at the end of a century of upheaval. They explore strange and different worlds in the face of threats to our own existence, involving the audience as confidents and reminding us that more often than not, the answers to the most burning questions are more likely to be found on society's fringes than at its centre.
