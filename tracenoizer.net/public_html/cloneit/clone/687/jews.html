Reserve kapitein (Captain in the Reserves) Jacques Schol of the demobilized Dutch Army Reserves and former leader of another refugee camp called Hellevoetsluis was appointed commander of refugee camp Westerbork on 16 July 1940. Captain Schol introduced new regulations for the German Jews in February 1942. Since the internees for the most part spoke the German language new camp rules were written and introduced in the German language.
