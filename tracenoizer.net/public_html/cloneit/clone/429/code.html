Area Code Change Detailsprovided by C E Service North America has seen an explosion of new area codes over the last six years, fueled by increased demand for numbers (modems, fax, wireless) and local competition. To meet this demand, the North American Numbering Plan was amended in 1995 to permit area codes with middle digits other than 0 or 1. As a result, millions of phone numbers have moved to new area codes in recent years.
