Each movie title is linked to the Internet Movie Database which contains more information on that movie. If the film contains an icon next to it, you may click on it to purchase that movie through REEL. You may also use the search box at the bottom of the page to search for other movies. ..
Videos may be VHS, SVHS (PAL or NTSC), Video 8 (not digital), Mini DV (SP PAL only) and must have a 10 second black lead in and only one movie per cassette. Motion picture film may be 16mm, or 8mm, including widescreen (anamorphic system) and should contain a two-meter leader. Whilst every precaution will be taken with all entries, no responsibility for any loss or damage to movies can be accepted.
