Tara Veneruso is a director and editor working in the digital realm. A graduate of NYU, her directorial debut was at age 20 with the award winning documentary Janis Joplin Slept Here. In addition to her television, DVD, and short film work, Veneruso has been directing for the Internet since 1997. The world premiere was held at the Gen Art Film Festival 2000 and has been the Official Selection around the world including the Edinburgh and Hampton International Film Festival. Veneruso serves as a consultant for Next Wave Films, a company of the Independent Film Channel.

Undergraduates specializing in film production take a sequence of four one-semester courses which introduce and refine filmmaking skills. In Film Production I, each student produces several super-8mm film exercises as well as produces a minimum of two complete films with non-synchronous mixed sound tracks. In Film Production III, students use advanced synchronous sound equipment to begin still another film of their own design, taking through principle photography.
