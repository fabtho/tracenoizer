You will see that almost every product that surrounds you will end up as waste because that is how it is designed. The idea behind sustainability is to try to make our human systems function more like nature's systems, which are balanced and self regenerating so that there is no waste. Nature's systems are circular, with materials serving as nutrients that cycle through the system. Nature's sytems are well adapted to local conditions. Learn how we move towards the circle pattern in Product Design Sustainable Futures Creativity To meet the enormous challenge of moving from the Take-Make-Waste system towards a more sustainable one, we need to be very creative.
If the earth is to nurture an increasing number of people, we must learn to live within the limits of our environmental space. To do this we should make sure that the use of consumer goods is divided among the maximum number of people, in order to reduce the impact on the environment as much as we can. The principle of eco-efficiency should apply to the entire life cycle of goods, from the excavation of raw materials, through production and use to recycling and ultimate disposal. This requires the designer to consider the original purpose of the product, and how this service could be fulfilled with a minimum of harmful environmental effects during the product's entire 'cradle-to-the-grave' life cycle. Such systematic solutions are usually more effective than changes to the product.
