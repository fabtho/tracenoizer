The new dates of November 12 -18 will allow the Festival a little breathing room from the planning stages of the mammoth Seattle International Film Festival (May 18 - June 11, 2000) and move WIC away from the heavy activity surrounding the Sundance and Rotterdam Film Festivals. The Women in Cinema Film Festival was established to provide a forum for and celebration of the accomplishments by women filmmakers in contemporary cinema. Remarkable feature films, documentaries and short works will be featured from women directors around the world. For the second year, Women in Cinema will offer audience based awards to filmmakers in the categories of Best Feature Film, Best Documentary and Best Short Film.

.The special prize, instituted by the International Federation of Film Societies, went to Karunam at the Berlinale which ended Sunday night. Jayaraaj said it was a recognition of contributions made by regional cinema. The four-member international jury cited the 77-minute film shot with English sub-titles for showing a "simple tale with a great emotional power and deep imagery that it could be understood universally". The film, last among the 300 films shown at the theatres at the 12-day long Berlinale, features Vavachan and Eliyamma portraying the role of an old couple waiting for the arrival of their son from the US in their hamlet.
