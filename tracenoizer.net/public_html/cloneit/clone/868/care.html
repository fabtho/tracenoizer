Healthy Child Care America Healthy Child Care America In many communities around the country, innovative but scattered projects are forging new ground to ensure that children are in healthy and safe child care environments. These communities recognize that the public health community can combine efforts with the early childhood community in order to create the best care for the millions of children in child care.
