(RCA), Director of HyperStudioMischa Schaub has been involved in the design of interactive media since the mid 70s, when he was a student of the Royal College of Art in London. His background consists of around fifteen years of experience as an Industrial Designer (mainly electronic investment and consumer goods), two books about the design of new media, ten years of coaching postgraduate research teams. In the nineties he acted as a director of the institute of visual media of the ZKM in Karlsruhe, did teach as a guest professor at the Hochschule der Kuenste in Berlin and is teaching presently at the Fachhochschule beider Basel in Switzerland. He has supervised several CD-ROM productions and the design of highly interactive websites. His main pride is the media institute HyperStudio, whose founder and director he is.
