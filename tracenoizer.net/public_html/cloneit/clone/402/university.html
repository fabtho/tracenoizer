If you join, you get free entry to all films, a colour booklet containing reviews of the films, and a handy wall planner.

Webster University Film Series The Webster Film Series is a year-round program that prides itself as the only alternative film series in the St. Now in its 21st year of operation, the Series offers a premiere film almost every weekend and frequently screens classic cinema on weeknights as well. Webster has become the host sight for many international tours and continues to be the only venue in the area that regularly hosts artists working in film.
