Biennale of Graphic Design Brno 98 The Biennale The city of Brno, the capital of Moravia in the south of the Czech Republic, has held Graphic Design Biennales since 1964. This year's turn was posters, including corporate identity and information and advertising graphics. More than 6000 entries were received, from which the local preselection jury picked 1200 works. About half of the designers took this first hurdle.
