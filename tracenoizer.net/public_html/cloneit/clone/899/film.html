Tara Veneruso is a director and editor working in the digital realm. A graduate of NYU, her directorial debut was at age 20 with the award winning documentary Janis Joplin Slept Here. In addition to her television, DVD, and short film work, Veneruso has been directing for the Internet since 1997.
