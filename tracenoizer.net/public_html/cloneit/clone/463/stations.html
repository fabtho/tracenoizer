Friends Amateur Radio Station W3AA Tucked away near the telescope on the fourth floor, is one of the world's finest amateur radio stations. Originally licensed in 1952 as W3TKQ, this continuously active exhibit has demonstrated to countless thousands of visitors, young and old, the exciting modes of radio communications available to licensed amateur radio operators. Daily radio contacts are maintained around the world, and emergency messages are often handled during natural and man-made disasters.
