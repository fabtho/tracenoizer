UIC Division of Specialized Care for Children - About What is the Children's Habilitation Clinic (CHC). The Children's Habilitation Clinic, founded as the Center for Handicapped Children in 1956, is a multidisciplinary clinic which provides care for children with developmental disabilities and education for health professionals in the care of children with disabilities. Children from birth through twenty-one years of age with known or suspected impairments involving movement, self-care, communication, and learning are appropriate for care.
