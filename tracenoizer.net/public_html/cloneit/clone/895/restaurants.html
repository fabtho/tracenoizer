Helsinki celebrated its 450th Anniversary and was a European City of Culture in the year 2000. HelsinkiMenu forms part of the offering of the Year of Culture. HelsinkiMenu was initiated by the Restaurant Trade, and by various gastronomic and business organisations (one of them being the IWFS Helsinki Branch), together with the food industry, and it can be experienced in a large number of restaurants within Greater Helsinki from the beginning of the year 2000. The project targets the general public, especially restaurant customers.
