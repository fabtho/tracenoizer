The Goal Equivalence Model, GEM TEQ is hindered by N-factors such as absenteeism and a high turn-over rate and is improved by P-factors such as motivation, knowledge and responsibility. The working life quality, WLQ, is the basis for the P-factors and a cause of the N-factors. There is also a positive relation between TEQ and WLQ due to job security, pride in working in a successful organization and the rational measures taken by a company understanding the importance of WLQ for TEQ. The model also includes the very important D-tools - work organization, man-machine systems, learning and dialogue, which operate on both TEQ and WLQ. The strength of the relations are dependent on culture.
