MUFS - Manchester University Film Society Welcome to MUFS. The Manchester University Film Society shows films to professional cinema standard in the Union's main hall. If you join, you get free entry to all films, a colour booklet containing reviews of the films, and a handy wall planner.
Publication of the scholarly journal FILM-HISTORIA three times a year. . Cinema Studies at the University of Barcelona Cinema Studies at the University of Barcelona The Centre for Cinematic Research FILM-HISTORIA was founded in 1983 and is based at the University of Barcelona, Spain. It was patterned after similar foreign organizations such as the Historians' Film Committee (U. ), Le Institut Jean Vigo (Perpignan, France), and the International Association for Media and History (IAMHIST).
