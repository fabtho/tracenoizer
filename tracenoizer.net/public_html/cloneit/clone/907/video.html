The Harrogate Cine and Video Society is now the HARROGATE CAMCORDER GROUP 50 Years of Film MakingJUBILEE YEAR 1950 - 2000 Formed fifty years ago the society has undergone considerable change in recent years to encompass modern technology in present day film making. Now predominately camcorder orientated the club is able to project members films on its highband video projector. We have members filming on all the present day video formats but still have a small number of enthusiasts using super 8 cine.
We invite you to join our happy band of movie makers and come along to any of our advertised club evenings completely without obligation and EXPLORE THE WORLD OF AMATEUR MOVIE MAKING. . Though at first being fairly inexperienced in this field we have attracted new members and can now boast a video projector allowing us to show films and videos on the same size screen. The society is keen to expand its membership and encourage the making of both cine and video movies. We would hope to create an environment in which the inexperienced as well as the more practised can show movies that even a complete stranger would find entertaining.
