MUFS - Manchester University Film Society Welcome to MUFS. The Manchester University Film Society shows films to professional cinema standard in the Union's main hall. If you join, you get free entry to all films, a colour booklet containing reviews of the films, and a handy wall planner.
A member of BUSU Arts Union and the British Federation of Film Societies BUFS is supported by BUSU at the University of Bath. . The Harrogate Cine and Video Society is now the HARROGATE CAMCORDER GROUP 50 Years of Film MakingJUBILEE YEAR 1950 - 2000 Formed fifty years ago the society has undergone considerable change in recent years to encompass modern technology in present day film making. Now predominately camcorder orientated the club is able to project members films on its highband video projector. We have members filming on all the present day video formats but still have a small number of enthusiasts using super 8 cine.
