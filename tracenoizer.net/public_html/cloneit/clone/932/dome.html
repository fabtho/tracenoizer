1st SPOT Synergetics, Geodesic Domes, Buckmister Fuller 1st SPOT Synergetics R. Fuller was one of the greatest geniuses of this century - or for that matter, any century. Fuller's genius is often measured by his inventions - flying cars, air deliverable apartment buildings, a distortion free map of the world, tensegrity structures and of course the geodesic dome. But what made all of this sing 'the music of the new life' was that it was developed in service of a vision of humanity - of a species whose fundamental destiny is to be a success in the universe.
