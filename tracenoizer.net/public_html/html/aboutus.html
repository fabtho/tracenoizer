About Us
The people behind TraceNoizer are LAN,a group consisting of students, media workers, artists and designers working under this name in varying compositions, sharing skills and resources in our locally networked environment and beyond. LAN creates network-oriented interactive projects.

The artistic mode of production is important to us. It enables us to develop applications and tools by using free software modules and scripts and then publicize our products as such for the use of others. We regard this open source/open content position as a small but effective stance against the cultural dominance of large IT-corporations.

Questions
If you have a specific question please contact the member of the TraceNoizer team (people who worked on TraceNoizer) whose field of expertise best matches your question. For general feedback or questions that concern all of us (or nobody in particular) send all of us a mail!!! .
